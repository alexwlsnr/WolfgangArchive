Part 7

A dragonman quarrels with the evil guys Dark Guss and Lynx. He manages to flee from them.
Soon after this event you have to fight your way to the next town north.

After you entered the town (it looks somehow familiar for those who played Shining Force II on the Genesis/Megadrive) an ambush awaits your troop. The dragonman flies towards you and wants to warn you but the town gate closes just before he can reach you. The townspeople take off their disguises and the attack starts. Don´t forget to open the chest in the northeast corner of the town. You will find a Robin Arrow. Search the two wells to find a Quick Ring and a Steel Sword. If you miss those items you can buy them in the next shop in the bargain department, but the Quick Ring costs you 10,000 bucks. Too expensive, if you can have it for free. Dragonman Eric becomes a new Shining Force member.
