Part 10

Before you attempt to storm the castle, retreat to the nearby camp and stock up your supplies. Hopefully you will have enough money to buy some new and stronger weapons. Buy Healing Seed for everyone, you may need it in the next battle against not only some well known enemies but two new ones, Hydras and a nasty Master Mage. Inside the chest on the left a Valkyrie is waitig either for Meddo or Sylvia.

You enter the ´Inner Saktum´ of Ike, one of the major devils. Await some Gargoyles which will creep from behind the statues in the middle hall.
Ike summons them when your party comes nearer.

There are four to six Gargoyles in total. It all depends on how quick you finish off Ike. Try to get the contents of the chest to the right. It´s a Heat Axe for Peiji.
