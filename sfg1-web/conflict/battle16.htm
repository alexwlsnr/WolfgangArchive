BATTLE 16 ´Meet Mishaela !´
Short Description
Mishaela awaits you at the end of this battle. Up to five Sandworms will show up in the middle of the narrow path. Two chests with a Healing Rain and a Running Pimento should be picked up by one of your ´Airforce´ troopers. After Mishaela is beaten (nonetheless, you will meet her again!) she manages to kill the old mentor of the Shining Force.

Enemies:
3 - 5 Sandworms
2 Executors 2 Gargoyles
2 Master Mages 2 Harpy Queens
2 Stone Golems 2 Brass Gunners
2 High Priests Mishaela
