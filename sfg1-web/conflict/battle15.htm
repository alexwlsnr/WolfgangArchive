BATTLE 15 ´Let us strike Ike !´
Short Description
After you entered the ´Inner Saktum´ of Ike, one of the major devils, be aware of Gargoyles which will creep from behind the statues in the middle of the hall. Ike summons them when your party comes nearer and nearer. There are four to six Gargoyles in total. It all depends on how quick you finish off Ike.
Try to get the contents of the chest to the right. It´s a Heat Axe for Peiji.

Enemies:
4 - 6 Gargoyles
2 Mist Demons 2 Bulls
2 Skeletons 2 Bowriders
2 Hydras 2 Master Mages
2 Executors Ike
