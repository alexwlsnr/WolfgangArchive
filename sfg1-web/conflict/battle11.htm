BATTLE 11
´Meeting Dark Guss´
Short Description
SF II fans now know it for sure. This battle takes place where in former times the Nazca Ship was buried under the sand. Some bushes have grown in the meantime, but who cares about this. Sweet remembrance feelings, indeed. Finally you caught up with Dark Guss, who is one of the major evil guys. Be careful when approaching him, first of all more evil foes will jump from behind the stones where they have hidden themselves and Dark Guss will cast a Freeze spell. In the chests you will find a Power Spear and a Battle Axe. Don't miss them. After the fight the Goddess Mituala will appear with a little donation for our hero Ian, a necklace. Shortly after this event the Shining Force welcomes a new member, the female wizard Minto. She shows the crew that the Nazca Ship is still functioning properly and after a short flight the Shining Force will enter the next scene.
Enemies:
3 Hobgoblins 2 Vampire Bats 2 Priests
3 Golems 2 Dark Snipers 2 Harpies
3 Hell Soldiers 2 Witches Dark Guss
