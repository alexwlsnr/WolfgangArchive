Part 2

After Ruburan is caught, the party returns to the town only to see it in ruins. The enemy has invaded the peaceful town. Soon the friends are spotted and the battle starts.

Ian, the swordsman and future hero joins the Force. The old mentor of the Shining Force is kidnapped by the evil Misheala right after the fight. (Remember her from Shining Force I ?)

Guided by Adam the friends follow the kidnappers and stumble into the next battle. Can the foes stop them? Surely not.


After the enemy is history, Cynthia, a capable healer magician join. Perfect timing, indeed.
