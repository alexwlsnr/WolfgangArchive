Part 9

Here Sir Astral met his former student Sarah. Find a Protection Ring inside the chest, will you? Some more enemies are hidden within the fog clouds.

After all is settled you meet an old guy. Don't underestimate him, his abilities are quiet opposite to his weak appearance. His name is Motton and his profession is that of a Master Monk.

Wandering around the countryside again gets you in trouble. Just before you reach the main enemy, a Bowrider, the earth shakes and a huge area of former dry land will be flooded right under your feet. To make things a bit bit more worse, a sea creature named Tentacle now can and will attack your group.
