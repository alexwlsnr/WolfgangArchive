Part 12

After you enter the castle and speak to the King, the building is taken over by the enemy troops. The castle is set on fire. You have to fight them back. Inside the chest to the left you will find some Kaiser Knuckles (CURSED !!) either for Knuckles or Motton.

When the fight is over you can finally enter the shop for some real good weapons.


The next battles is really tough. Not only 6 High Witches stand against you, but there are 5 Lesser Demons which can cast spells, too. So move onward carefully and try not to loose too many comrades in the battle. At the end there is a Mishaela Doll waiting to sparc you to pieces. So keep yourself healthy until the confrontation starts.

The shining balls will have no effect, neither they move nor do they attack. Wonder why they were placed here?
