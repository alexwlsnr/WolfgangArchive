Short Description
This the Max, the hero of the first Shining Force. After you and your friends beat him he is free from the evil influence of the Dark Sword. He then joins to help you in the final confrontation with Misheala, Dark Sol, and Dark Dragon. Knows EGRESS (RETURN) and even SPARC (BOLT). He must have learned this spell somehow because he could not cast it in his first adventure.
