INTRODUCTION

Welcome to the little storyboard. Don´t expect to much of it.
My knowledge of the Japanese language is not as good as I want it to be.
It took me some time to translate the names of all characters, enemies and items, which can be bought or found. And I am not even sure that I translated them correctly.

So I had to use my intuition and the stories of the previous played Shining Force games to write what you are reading now. I mainly want to describe the battles here, mixed with some pictures which could not be used elsewhere on this site. The text can be found in the Battle section, too.

The story takes place in times after Bowie and his friends of the second Shining Force fought their battles against the evil hordes of Zion. After he was defeated another evil foe and his comrades try to fill the gap. You will remember them very well.

Ok, enough smalltalk, let´s start !!
