Part 11

Mishaela awaits you at the end of this battle. Up to five Sandworms will show up in the middle of the narrow path. Two chests with a Healing Rain and a Running Pimento should be picked up by one of your ´Airforce´ troopers. After Mishaela is beaten (nonetheless, you will meet her again!) she manages to kill the old mentor of the Shining Force.

The confrontation with Lynx is near. This battle seems easy but during the fight more enemies will appear. They are: 2 High Witches, 2 Archknights, 2 Stone Golems, 1 High Priest, 1 Harpy Queen, and 1 Executor in total.

Lynx takes his last breath when a Warrior named Rideion shows up. It seems that he knows Lynx. Maybe that Lynx was missguided by Mishaela. Rideon joins to avange Lynx´s death..
