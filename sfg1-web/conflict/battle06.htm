BATTLE 6 ´Moutains so high´
Short Description
A conversation between Misheala and some Birdmen ends with the two birdmen falling down the cliffs. Soon after this event you are spotted and the fight starts.
Your first enemy with magic abilities, a Dark Mage awaits you. A Cheerful Bread as a present awaits the one who knocks him down.
Birdman Kiddo joins your party when all is over.

Enemies:
2 Brass Roaders
3 Green Oozes
2 Worms
3 Dark Dwarfs
4 Giant Bats
1 Dark Mage
