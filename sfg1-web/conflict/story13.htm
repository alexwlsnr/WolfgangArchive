Part 13

If you thought the previous battle was hard, try this one. Get the chest to the left. Keep the party healthy until you reach Max. He, hidden under a red mask, is the main enemy. Remember Max?

Yes, it´s Max of Shining Force I, who sunk with the Ancient castle and managed to survive. No one knows, what happend to him then. Now you meet him again but he is under an evil spell, caused by the Dark Sword he carries. This weapon once had the same effect on Kain. The only cure known to break the spell is: Beat the owner! So, what are you waiting for?

Another surprise is waiting for you after the fight:
A blind man appears in front of the tower, guess, who he is! Again you are right. His name is Odd Eye, the right hand of Zeon in Shining Force II. Once one of the evils guys, walking around with Bowie and the Shining Force was not in vain. This time he joins on the right side to send the evil back where it belongs.
