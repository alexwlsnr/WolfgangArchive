BATTLE 14
´Sneak into Algam Fort´
Short Description
Rhode, a new member of the Force, shows the group the weak spot of Algam Fort. Sneak into it from the top. There is an Evil Pixie flying near the door. It will be replaced by a new one every time you killed one.

Enemies:
3 Lizard Men
2 Arch Knights
2 Dark Rangers
1 Dark Bishop
1 Sorcerer
Unlimited Evil Pixies
