BATTLE 11
´Trapped on Board´
Short Description
In Portobello only a few crew members managed to get on board of the ship before it sets sails and leaves the harbour. It was a trap to separate the Shining Force. While one part is left behind in Portobello, the rest has to face the enemy to finally bring the boat under their control.

The birdwarrior Claude manages to reach you after you finished off the last of the evil guys.

Enemies:

4 Pegasus Knights
2 Ratwings
1 Dark Bishop
1 Evil Pixie
1 Master Mage
