BATTLE 9
´On to the Portobello´
Short Description
Things start to get worse. The Cypress army has lost and King Nick is captured by the evil Warderer. His hobby, as you might know, is to sacrifice kings to Iom. So you must hurry to rescue Nick.

The kingdom of Warderer lies beyond the ocean. So you reach Portobello, a harbour town.

Enemies:
2 Ratwings 2 Dark Snipers
2 Zombies 2 Dark Monks
2 Pegasus Knights 2 Skeletons
2 Brass Rorders 1 Master Mage
