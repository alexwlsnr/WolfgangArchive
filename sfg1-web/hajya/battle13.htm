BATTLE 13
´The Cliffs near Algam´
Short Description
Meanwhile all Shining Force members who were left behind in Portobello try to reach their goal on a different tour. So they have to pass Algam. This fort lies on top of a cliff.

Use Healing Seeds or Medical Herbs before approaching the Master Mage or he will freeze your party member to ice.

Enemies:
2 Arch Knights
2 Pegasus Knights
2 Skeletons
1 Dark Bishop
1 Lizard Man
1 Dark Sniper
1 Master Mage
