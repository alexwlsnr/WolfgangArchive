´Walldol has returned´
Short Description
To all those who have not played Shining Force CD, Walldol is an unknown enemy.

True SF fans know him as the opponent of Nick, former prince and now king of Cypress. He transformed into an evil scorpion and is responsible that the right hand of Nick has turned to stone.

And now he is back again. Truely persistant, won´t you ever die?
Yes, today´s the day!!
