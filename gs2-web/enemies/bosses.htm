Bosses


Once again I have to apologize to all those of you who seek complete information how to beat the bosses. There are so many ways to get rid of them in the Golden Sun series. You can use Psynergy spells, use standard attacks or, if you prefer, make extensive use of Djinns and summonings.

You would spoil the whole game and fun for yourself if you refer to walkthroughs which offer every detail. Don't be a slave of suggestions other people make, but explore by yourself and have more fun!

The bosses you see are not listed alphabetically but shown in order of their appearance. Of course, depending on which way you go, this list might not look like yours during your quest!
