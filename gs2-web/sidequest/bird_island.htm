Angara Islet

 Southeast of Champa you will find Angara Islet

There is nothing much to do or to find on this little island.

Come here with the Pretty Stone in your inventory, solve the little Mov'n Freeze puzzle to get near the bird's nest. The bird comes near, sits onto the nest. Now use MINDREAD and you will gain a scarf named Red Cloth in exchange for the stone.

Items to gain
Red Cloth
Psynergy needed
MOVE
FROST
MIND READ
