Indra Islet

 Indra Islet is located between Indra (of course) and Gondowan.

Once you found Indra Islet you should try to get near the little dog sitting in the water.

Don't fear about drowning, you won't sink into the water too deep. Offer the doggie the Milk via MIND READ and you can trade an other interesting item for it.
Finally, you found a small turtle called Li'l Turtle. It is missing its mother and so (just a guess) you should reunite them.

Somewhere on Indra Islet an Oil Drop is hidden, not that you need it, just mentioned it for some more text for you to read!!

Once you got Li'l Turtle, leave Indra Islet and sail far, far, far east to Sea of Time Islet, which can be found south of Apo jii.

Items to gain
Li'l Turtle
Oil Drop
Psynergy needed
MIND READ
