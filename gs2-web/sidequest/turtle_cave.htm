Sea of Time Islet Cave

So you found you way inside Sea of Time Islet Cave. If you don't know the TELEPORT psynergy, you will be forced to return here later during the quest. So let's talk about the first two rooms of this cave here, the rest will be described below.

Two items can be found in the chests of the first room, in the second room a Venus Djinn called Convoy joins after some tackling.

Rusty Ankh
Turtle Boots
Psynergy needed
TELEPORT

Once you used TELEPORT you can enter a very long hallway. I shortened it "just a bit" due to the fact that it normally would have taken four to five screen heights (depending on your screen resolution) for a complete display. So I decided to concentrate on the important facts.

First of all, the enemies are unusually strong! But you will find one enemy in here worth fighting again and again and again (...), the Wonderbird! It gives you 8622 EXP each, ideal for some leveling up your characters.

Next, another Mercury Djinn is waiting for you. After a long journey through boring hallways you will enter one with a shaking statue. Approaching it causes Zero to leave its hideout.

Last but not least, another Combination Tablet can bee found. It won't be easy to get is because it is guarded by Sentinel, a living armor.
As a reward for beating him you will get Catastrophe, which can be summoned by three Mars Djinns ( ) and five Jupiter Djinns ( ).
