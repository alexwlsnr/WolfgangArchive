Madra Cave


This cave underneath Madra holds to precious items (amongst others). Both items are hidden in the house to the top left. The first time you will search the house you will get the Tremor Bit. If being equipped it gains the TREMOR psynergy.

The second item is a Combo Tablet. This can only be gained with Piers in your party due to a puddle which must be frozen for a nice "crossover". There is an entrance top right, which will be blocked later. Here you can cross over to reach the third and last entrance to the underground house.
