Garoh Village (the village itself)

 "Bonefires" and werewolves in Garoh

If you havn't been to Air's Rock and get the REVEAL psynergy, you cannot do much here in the first place. In the top right corner of the village a wereworf like animal appears out of a rock, which entrance can only be seen via REVEAL.

If Sheba knows about the spell, follow the wolf and you will meet a cute werecolf child in the cave inside the rock. Follow the tiny wolf and you will meet the werewolf mentioned above (named Maha) at the end of the cave. A conversation takes place (don't ask me, what they are talkung about).

But one thing is important, spend one night at the inn once you talked to Maha and return here on the next day.
