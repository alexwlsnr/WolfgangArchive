Alhafra Cave (including prison)

 POUND the "button"


You will enter this cave two times. First you can only get the items listed under Part I.

Once the pirates escaped from the prison, they will remove some barriers which prevented you from exploring the second part of the cave. But luckily the pirates were in such as haste, that they forgot to get all the nice goodies listed under Part II.

So take them before someone else finds them!

Items to gain (Part I)
Action Mail 123 coins
Lucky Medal

Items to gain (Part II)
Psy Crystal 777 coins
Potion
