Alhafra Town

 Alhafra is the biggest town on the Osenian Continent


You have to pass Yampi Desert if you want to enter the harbour town of Alhafra. But not only you will visit the town but the folks which were stuck in the desert (detail about that in the Yampi section) will arrive here soon after you. I am not quite sure, but those people are obviously from Madra, the major town of the Indra Continent. They want to talk to Alhafra's mayor and I believe that the trouble here in Alhafra deals with a pirat's ship which has stranded here with a broken mast (details about that in the Pirate Ship section).

Again search everywhere to find the hidden items, then visit the inn. Here a big surprise is waiting for you. On the upper floor you will meet Alex again, so he hasn't been drowned by the tsunami at the start of your adventure. He will not join, of course, but on the other hand, he does not take Jenna and Kraden as hostages again.

You cannot enter the castle first but once you have beaten Briggs and his pirates on the ship, you will be invited by the towns mayor, while the pirates are kept in prison.

So, what is of importance I did not tell until now? Oh yes, You will find a Large Bread in the pirates ship. If you offer this bread to a little boy with blond hair at the harbour, the guy in the rightmost corner will let you enter a secret cave where some goodies can be found.

When you visit Alhafra for the second time, you have to free the pirates ship and then you are blamed for doing so, because the pirates will break out of the prison and make a hasty escape with the ship to their hometown of  Champa.
