Shaman Village

 Shaman Village, the only town on the Hesperian Continent

 The Shaman Staff is the Key!

Finally you found Shaman Village and now it becomes clear why Ivan's Shaman Staff is so important for the Jupiter Lighthouse.

Only if you show the staff to the towns chief you will be judged trustworthy to take part in a trial which will gain you the Hover Jade if you win. Without it you will not be able to perform HOVER, which is neccessary to reach the top of the Jupiter Lighthouse at last.

You can find the Jupiter Djinn Aroma, too, once you have finished the trial successfully. And you may visit an old shaman in the tent to the right, although I am not sure, what his purpose is. Again we have to wait for the translated version!
