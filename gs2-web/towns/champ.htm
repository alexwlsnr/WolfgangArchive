Champa Village

 Champas, hometown of the Pirate Briggs

Briggs and his family escaped from Alhafra to this town on the Angaran Continent.

Once you found the town, a well known face will appear near the town entrance, the girl named Feihzi will tell something about Isaac and leaves (as I think) a Golden Ring behind (only if you helped her friend Hsu in the previous adventure).

Now search the town for items, use REVEAL near the graveyard for the Viking Helmet and enter the cave in the northern part of the town.

Depending on what you did before the story line may differ a bit.

  1. If Briggs is not set free until now, you will not meet his grandmother Obaba.

  2. If you don't have found all three Trident Parts, Obaba will not
    create the Trident for you.

It will be in Champa, too, that you meet not only Karst for the second time, but her friend Agatio will show up! You don't have to fight them yet, maybe because Alex appears in Champa just in time. He talks to them and they leave, but I cannot tell you what the conversation was all about!
