Izumo Cave (Part II)

Only two obstacles are left before you will gain what is hidden inside this cave, the Combo Tablet which will give you the ability to summon Lily Chews once you have two Mars Djinns ( ) and two Mercury Djinns ( ) ready in standby mode.

Use POUND on the pillar then Piers must perform PARCH near the water (see left) to let it dry out. Now a door will be seen which leads you to the last part of this dungeon.

If you want to get the Robber Armor you must leave this floor and return back here once more. The pillar has "popped out" once again and can be used to jump over the gap to reach the chest.

Item to gain
Robber Armor

Psynergy needed
POUND
PARCH

Combination Tablet
Lily Chews
