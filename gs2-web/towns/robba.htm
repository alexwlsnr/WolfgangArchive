Loho Village

 Loho Village can be found near the west coast of the Angaran Continent


Loho seems to be a miners village far to the west of Angara Continent. You can't do much here unless you found the Magma Ball inside the Magma Rock. This item, used in the cannon to the right of the village will blast a wall to dust.

If you ask yourself why this act of destruction is neccessary, you should keep three things in mind, which won't happen if this wall will not be destroyed.

First, you now can reach the Jupiter Djinn Peace, which is waiting on top of one of the houses.

Second, you won't get 2 Golem Cores, rare items which can be transformed to mighty tools by the blacksmith of Yallam.

Third, the most important fact is, that the village people will donate the cannon to you and install it on your ship!
This will come in handy, if you want to destroy a nasty barrier somewhere in the Northern Ocean!
