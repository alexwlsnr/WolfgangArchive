Prox Town

 The northernmost town of Prox cannot be found on the worldmap


Hidden from the view of normal people, far behind the Northern Ocean you will finally find the town of Prox. It is the Hometown of Agatio, Karst, Mernardi, and Saturos, your four enemies who tried to beat you many times on your long and dangerous journey. And this time, they will have no chance, too.

What else can be told about this village, yes, you will meet many people at the northern town exit, discussing something very important (sorry, I did not understand a single word!). I think it has to deal with the Mars Lighthouse, lying north of Prox and of course about Agatio and Karst which seem to have been here before.
