Shaman Trial Cave (Part IV)

Short description of Trial Cave (Part IV)

Left Trial: Move the stone pillars up, climb the vine and jump over to the right. Now push the last pillar to reach the vine, climb up to win the race!

Psynergy needed

None at all!!

Item to be gained

Vial


Right Trial:Run into the sand fall (push B-Button), immediately use SAND to reach the top cliff.

Remove the unneeded bush with WHIRLWIND and climb up on the rest to reach the door.

Psynergy needed

SAND
WHIRLWIND

Item to be gained

Potion
