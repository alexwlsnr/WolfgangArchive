Yallam Village

 Yallam is known as the hometown of a famous blacksmith!

Welcome to Yallam. In this village in the northeast of Osenia a famous craftsman is living. He will help you by working on rare materials and rusty items. It is always a surprise, what he creates from the stuff you give to him. Even his price will surprise!

To gain your hands on the Muramasa Sword you will need the FORCE psynergy, coming with Isaac and friends. Now you only need CYCLONE to get rid of the leaves hiding the ladder to the underground!

Oh, if you need some advise how to master the Sea of Time around Lemuria you should talk and play with the children on Yallam's playground to the right!
