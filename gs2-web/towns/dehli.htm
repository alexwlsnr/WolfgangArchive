Daila Village

 Welcome to Daila, the first village you will visit


The first village you will enter during your quest will be Daila, lying southwest of the place where your party has landed after the typhoon.

Some items can be found here, the tiny weapon shop does not offer very much of interest.

Come back here once Piers become a party member to get the Sea God's Tear from inside the temple.
