Mikasalla Village

 Mikasalla lies in the southwest of Osenia

Mikasalla is the third of the four towns you can find on Osenia. Not much will happen here but you can find a Mars Djinn named Gutts in the northern part of the town.
You cannot reach him directly but have to find a hidden path first.

 Use SCOOP to find the trapdoor
