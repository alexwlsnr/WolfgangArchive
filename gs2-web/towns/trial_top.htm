Shaman Hill (the very top)

 You have reached the top of Shaman Hill

Your final test awaits you here: the fight against Moapa and the Senjis. Depending on how fast you reached the top the fight will be more or less harder.

But you will manage it and will be awarded with the Hover Jade, the key item to beat the Jupiter Lighthouse.

The person equipped with this jewel can perform HOVER on special places. This psynergy will lift the whole party into the air for a short period of time.

Try it on the violet/pink symbol to the left and transport yourself over the gap. Inside the cave you won't get far until you meet Isaac's party. Details a can be found in the next part.
