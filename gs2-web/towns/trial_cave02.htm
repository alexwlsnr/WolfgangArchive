Shaman Trial Cave (Part III)

Short description of Trial Cave (Part III)

Left Trial: Move the stone pillar up two places, climb the vine and wait for the fountains to jump over to the left. Meanwhile you will have much time left to grab the Potion from the chest and reach the door in time.

Psynergy needed

None at all!!

Item to be gained

Potion


Right Trial:Only some pushing is needed to solve this puzzle. First push the horizontal trunk up and the downmost to the left.

Climb the vine, push the horizontal trunk back to its previous position, push the stone pillar out of the way and give the topmost trunk a kick to the right. It will fall into the bassin.

Use the trunk to cross the water, climb up the vines to the right and you can reach the door without any problem.

Psynergy needed

None at all!!

Item to be gained

Vial
