Atteka Harbour

 Atteka Harbour is located on the west coast of Atteka Continent


Besides the town of Contigo, this harbour is one of the two places, where people settled on the continent of Atteka.

The chest on top of the cliff containing a Vial can be reached only from the left side. So you have to visit Atteka Gate to the left and return here on top of the cliff to get it.

When coming here first, you are forced to venture here via the river which crosses the continent from east to west. The barrier to the ocean (somehow bubble like) can be crossed later, once your Lemurian Ship is upgraded with wings. One of them is lying on the beach already. Don't bother about it now. When you return with Isaac in your party, the village poeope will have upgraded your ship (free of charge)!

Remember Master Hama who donated the REVEAL psynergy to Ivan? Yes, even Master Hama waves goodbye after your successful return from Jupiter Lighthouse. You will get another blacksmith item called Orihalcon, too, before you leave.
