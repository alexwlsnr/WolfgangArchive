Gaia Rock (Part V)


Inside Magma Rock you have to find a way not only to fill some dungeon parts with magma but to get rid of it once it blocks your way. The first task is easy, use BURST on the small stoneheads which causes the bigger ones to release the magma.

To let the magma flow out of the bassins, simply use the switch near the floodgate doors like the one you can see on the map.

Once you have reached the stairs to the next sublevel, press the NEXT button.
