Gaia Rock (Part I)

 Gaia Rock lies south of Izumo

 Create a stepstone with the wood statue

Don't blame me if I tell you something completely wrong here, but due to the lack of my knowledge of the Japanese language, I might as well heavily missunderstood the story of this Rock lying south of Izumo. A girl named Kushinada shall be sacrificed to a dragon called Orochi. Her friend, Susa, seems to have come here to kill the dragon to rescue his girlfriend. You will meet him here, when you enter the place for the first time.

Your main task in order to help him and get a new psynergy for Felix at Gaia Rock is to climb to the very top of the moutain.

Later you can enter the big hole into the inside of the rock. You may try entering earlier, but you won't get far without a special item, which obviously, has to be found on top of Gaia Rock!
