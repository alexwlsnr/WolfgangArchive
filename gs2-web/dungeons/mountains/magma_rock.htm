Magma Rock (Part I)

 Magma Rock is the last mountain to visit

Welcome to Magma Rock, a red mountain lying slightly northwest of Kibombo.

Unless Isaac is not a member of the gang you will not be able to LIFT the giant rock blocking the way to the mountain itself.

So be patient and visit Jupiter Lighthouse first, meet Issac, let him join and come back here for an important item and a new Psynergy, this time for Jenna.

You may enter the cave but a stone pillar will block the stair and inside the chest a Mimic is hidden. Very unpleasant, indeed, so climbing up the hill would be the better choice.

Inside the chest near the top you will find an Oil Drop, so it may be left where it is. The enemies here won't be too impressed of the effect the Oil Drop will have on them, anyway.
