Gaia Rock (Part VII)

If you listened to what was mentioned above, you will enter this dungeon part from the southeast door first. Venture forth and use CYCLONE while standing in midst of the plants seen to the left. Some of them will lift in the air, remaining as "stepstones" to reach the center doorway.

Now return to the entrance, take the left door, return to this room and use the plants to reach the door leading to the northern part of the cave.
