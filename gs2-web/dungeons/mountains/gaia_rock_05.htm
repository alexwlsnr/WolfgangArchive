Gaia Rock (Part VI)

Once you enter the Gaia Rock you will find a pedestal, similar to the one of the top. If you wandered what to do with the Dancing Idol, here is your chance. Simply use it in front of the pedestal and it will dance for you.

A nice side effect of this dance is that the stone masks reveal hidden doors. You now should use the right door.

Don't take the path in the center, although following it will bring you to the main emeny inside the mountain. But you will not have a chance against him unless you ventured through the rest of the dungeon. And, at last, the path to the left is completely useless until later.
