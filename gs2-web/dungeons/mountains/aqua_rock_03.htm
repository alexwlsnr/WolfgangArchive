Aqua Rock (Part IV)

You will enter this room via Steps 1. Leave it via Steps 2 and you will enter a room with a huge Psy Crystal like in Air's Rock. You can't reach it, so step up and re-enter the previous room via Steps 3.

Now use the small path underneath the rope, jump on the yellow stepstones and climb up the ladder to the left. Walk through the door to enter another room with a stream. Create a Wooden Stepstone with the statue, so that you can reach the chest with a Tear Stone inside.

Leave via Steps 5.

The second time you will enter this room will be via Steps 4, your goal is to reach Steps 6 to the right.
