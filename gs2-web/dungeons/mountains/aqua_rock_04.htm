Aqua Rock (Part V)

 Look what happens after you place the
Aquarius Stone onto the socket!



Due to the fact that this cave part is extremly huge, I devided it into a center right and a left part. Shown here is the center right. It is connected to the left via the doorways marked as Doorway 1 and Doorway 2.

Main goal is getting to the chest in the center which contains the Aquarius Stone. This must be placed onto the socket to devide the water and reveal a passage to the inner sanctum.

After Piers learned about the PARCH psynergy, you can reach the Mercury Djinn Mellow, which is waiting for you at the end of a stream in the room to the right. Stand above him, PARCH the stone head, the water stops flowing and you will get him.
