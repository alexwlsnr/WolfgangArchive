The Pirates's Ship in Alhafra Harbour


 Pirate's Ship for the second time


After you gained the BURST psynergy in the Southern Tower, you can return here to free the ship from its bindings.

After you have done the hard part, Chaucha, Briggs's wife and the little Eoleo will inform the pirates, who still are imprisoned in the Alhafra dungeons, about the good news.

While your party visits Alhafra's chief, the bandits enter the ship, fix the mast and sail away north to Champa, the hometown of Briggs's gang.

The villagepeople of Alhafra will blame you and you will not be allowed to enter the chief's house any more!


But it can't be helped, you have to free the ship to continue your quest.

Otherwise you will never meet the old Obaba, who will help you to get your hands on a very important item!
