The Lemurian Ship


 The Lemurian Ship near Dehkan Plateau


After you leave Dehkan Plateau you soon will spot a Lemurian ship. Time for a short break, but you cannot do anything else now.
Later during the quest you will return here and enter the ship. Don't forget to grab the Cookie from the chest on the cliff!

Psynergy needed         Items to gain

Frost                  Cookie


 First deck


After returning from Kibombo, having a Black Orb in your pocket and the permission of the Madran elder in mind, your finally can enter the Lemurian boat.

But beware, the boat is "flooded" by Aqua Jellies, which you must beat to explore your own ship. Luckily an Aqua Jelly leaves a puddle after its defeat and you can frost them to reach the unreachable parts on the decks! Oh, sometimes some chests have to be pushed, too.

 Third deck


The basin to the left in the room you will enter now should give you a clue what to do next. After the door to the next gangway opened the Black Orb will do its job on the crystal in the center of the next room.

You did it, an own ship at your command, feel free to roam the whole world of Golden Sun on the oceans!
