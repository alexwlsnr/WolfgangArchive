The Pirates's Ship in Alhafra Harbour


 Pirate's Ship at the first sight


When seeing the Pirate's Ship for the first time it will look like shown to the left. The mast is broken and the sails are blocked by many obstacles.

Don't care about that for the moment but search the inner decks for clues. Be careful to be well equipped and healed up before entering the ship. Your party will have to fight the pirate Briggs and his underlings.

Once you beat them, search the ship more closely to find the Large Bread.This will be helpful to find a way into the hidden Alhafra dungeons. Simply USE this bread on everyone in Alhafra (especially small boys) and you soon will find one in the harbour area, who might need it.

After you finally solved this puzzle, talk to the guy in the westernmost part of the city. Obviously being the father of the little boy he will step aside the gate and let you enter the cave behind the fence!
