Mars Lighthouse (Part I)

 Finall, the last Lighthouse, dedicated to Mars

Right north of Prox you will find the last challenge in your adventure (OK, that's not right true, if you want to get all djinns, secrets and combo tablets), the Mars Lighthouse.

You may notice a chest top left outside the building. Go there to get an Apple. Now enter the lighthouse for even more puzzles, items and two djinns, one of the Mars and one of the Mercury class!
