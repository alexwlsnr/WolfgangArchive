This is the center part of the Ankohl Ruins. As you can see the clue to reach the different parts of this dungeon is to MOVE the wooden statues to free some sandy spots, which now can be passed with the SAND psynergy of Felix.


Once the stone head on this floor looks like shown to the left, the sand will flow and you can use SAND to reach the stairs (5), which will lead you to the top of the tower. But it will take you much time to complete the statue head.

On the right you see one of two "sand trap rooms". You cannot avoid to step on the switch next to the right door. Once the switch is activated, sand is filling the room. You must reach the second switch right to the exit before it is covered by sand. If you fail so, simply leave the room and try again.
