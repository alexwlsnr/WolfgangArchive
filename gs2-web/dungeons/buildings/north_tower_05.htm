Ankohl Ruins Right (Part V)

This seems to be the most tricky part of Ankohl Ruins. Once the Stone Head in the center is completed it will start to move towards your party when you approach it. So be quick and use SAND before the head can push you behind the sandy part of the hallway!

Otherwise you will not be able to reach the stairs No.8 via "underground".
