Joke aside, CARRY is needed to lift the two stones on the ground floor into the two gaps in the wall. Then push the third stone over and block the dragon head.

After you used the rope to get down there is one (not so obvious ) trick to keep in mind. Use SAND while standing on the tiles colored different, because they are small sand pitches. Only so you can avoid being blasted down and restart again. At last you will reach the door to the bottom and leave the lighthouse once again.
