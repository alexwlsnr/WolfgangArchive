Mars Lighthouse Dragon Symbol (Part I)



 The wind is blowing!


Not much to tell about the first two maps behind the Dragon Symbol besides that you can find the Isis Robe in the chest outside the lighthouse.


But some action is needed in the room to the left. First Burst the cracks in the walls to the left and to the right. To blast the middle wall, you will need the help of the dragonhead.

To convince the dragon, simply hop over to the top left and activate the switch for the small dragon head.

Now go to the top right and use BLAZE on the now lit torch. The big dragonhead statue will blast the center wall to oblivion now.

Return to the entrance and find you way to the stairs on top of the map.
