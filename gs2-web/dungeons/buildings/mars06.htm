Mars Lighthouse (Part VII)

Careful here, one of the major battles will take place on this floor. After Jenna uses BLAZE to light the switch, both dragonheads will blast a crack in the huge iceblock standing in the center of the room.

Once examined and cracked open due to BURST the two firedragons inside are not very thankful for being freed again and attack your party. Once they are defeated, it will be revealed that both dragons were Agatio and Karst who used a transformation trick well known by all who fought Saturos and Menardi in the first adventure of Golden Sun.

 Finally, they are beaten!

Now take the Mars Stone dropped by Karst and with the help of the Mythril Bag, which Felix had in his inventory from the very beginning, place the item into the mouth of the third dragonhead of the room.

 Place the Mars Stone where it belongs!

It looks like you started the central heating of Mars Lighthouse, even the colors of the walls are changing to a warmer tone. Your work is done here, so use RETREAT and head back to the lighthouse's entrance.
