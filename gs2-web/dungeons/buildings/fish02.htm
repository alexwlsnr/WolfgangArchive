Mars Lighthouse Fish Symbol (Part II)


You will enter through the doorway on the bottom of the left room but your first goal will be to reach the topmost door in the room to the right. The reason is simple, the small dragonhead is not functioning properly (steam instead of flames).


Back on the floor to the left you will see and fix the problem, a broken pipe! Returning to the room above right, you will notice that the huge dragon statue will be lit by the smaller one, melting away the greater ice pillars, that were blocking your way.
