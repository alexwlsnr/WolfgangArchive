Ankohl Ruins (Part II)

It looks like Ankohl Ruins has not been entered for years. Grown vines hide most of the doors and stairs and must be removed by WHIRLWIND. In the rooms next to the entrance there are six empty treasure chests. But you will find many goodies in this tower soon, while trying to reach the top of the building. Due to the architecture you can easily get lost inside, but you will find your way. The main goal is to fix the statue heads to let the sand flow. You will soon see what I mean.
