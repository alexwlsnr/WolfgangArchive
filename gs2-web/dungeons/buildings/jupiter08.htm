Jupiter Lighthouse (Part IX)

 Outside of Jupiter Lighthouse

After you used doorway (C) you soon will notice a massive stone pillar is blocking your way over the bridge. Simply push it onto the bridge, then press the purple switch on the floor, the drawbridge will drop and the stone is history. Now move over the bridge (trust me it is safe now) and re-enter the lighthouse via door (F).

Find your way back to the room shown to the left. If you took my advice when you were here once before, the white pillars now all should levitate for a safe crossover to the right. If not, you can move the missplaced pillar now. Take doorway (10) and after a short visit in the room with the Erin's Coat you will be in the main entrance room again (11).
