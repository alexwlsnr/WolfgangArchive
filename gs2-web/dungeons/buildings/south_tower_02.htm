Tundaria Tower (Part II)

Once Piers has dried up the wet spots of the entrance room with the PARCH psynergy you should move on to the right parts of the dungeon. The left part of the tower cannot be reached until you found the Burst Brooch.

Psynergy needed
PARCH
BURST


After you found the Burst Brooch you can get the most important item hidden in the Tundaria Tower, the Center Prong.

Don't forget to visit the left part of Tundaria Tower before you leave, many nice items can be found!

Items to be gained
Sylph Feather Center Prong Vial
Lucky Medal Lightning Blade
