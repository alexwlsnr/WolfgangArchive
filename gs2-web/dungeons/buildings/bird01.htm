Mars Lighthouse Bird Symbol (Part I)



 The wind ist blowing!


Behind the Bird Symbol walk from the right to the left then you will leave the lighthouse. Cross over the bridge and re-enter.

This dungeon seems to be dedicated to Sheba or Ivan, because you will need REVEAL psynergy to solve the puzzles.

Study the maps and you will know where to use CYCLONE to switch from the floor displayed to the left and the floor above, which will be shown once you move to the next part.
