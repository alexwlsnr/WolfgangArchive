Jupiter Lighthouse (Part XVII)

The room to the left is (as I believe) the most tricky in the whole adventure. You will enter through the door next to the isolated levitation point surrounded by cracked tiles. Use HOVER and hover to the left for a nice sword, the Phaeton's Blade, turn right for the exit and the way to the tower top.

The trick is not to be blown over the cracked tiles areas on both sides. You will fall through to the floor below and have to start once again. Try to move near the cracked tiles, if you come near the same level as the blowing statues use the (B-Button) to get past the statues. If the wind catches you, try to steer into the aera with the levitation point.

If you manage to reach them, it does not matter if you fall off the gravity circle. Simply use HOVER once more to reach what you had in mind (either the sword or the doorway).
