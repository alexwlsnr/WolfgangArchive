Jupiter Lighthouse (Part XIII)

Finally, you are near the top. Two things must be done here and you have to decide, which one you will do first.

My suggestion is to go to the top first. Sorry, you cannot enter the room to the right when coming back here from the roof, because the door with the "pillar switch" will be locked then. But it is much faster than to fall down to the floor with the Blue Key and fight your way back to this room once again. If you use the tile next to the doorway you only fall down one floor and then return here to get the key!

 On top of the left tower


So here is a short explanation how to reach the door and then find the right tile to break through for getting the key. To reach the door, use the left levitation point and hoover to the left, then move the pillar to activate the door switch and walk your way back to the right levitation point, use it and get as far down as possible. Walk through the door and go up the stairs for the exit. If you decide to get the key first, simply use the right levitation point, hoover down and step on the isolated cracked tile. That's all.

Now only one question must be answered, what are you supposed to do on the of the left tower? The answer is simple, fix the energy circulation! This will cause the bowman statue to shoot an arrow in direction of the central tower of Jupiter Lighthouse, hit the winged statues there and lift it slightly up. The lid of the central tube of the lighthouse is partially open! If you have not got the Blue Key yet, don't forget it on your way back to the central tower. You will need it there!
