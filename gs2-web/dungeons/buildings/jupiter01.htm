Jupiter Lighthouse (Part II)

Once you have entered the lighthouse you soon will notice a firmly closed door blocking your way. A glimpse of a trail can be seen behind it which is obviously one part of a switch to open the door. So how can you get there?

The answer to that question lies to the left. Although it seems to be a dead end, you should use CYCLONE next to the circular object on the ground outside the lighthouse. You will be transported into a cave underneath Jupiter Lighthouse.

Now take the path to the right, enter a cave with a Psynergy Stone, continue to the right ignoring the leafs lying at the end of a branch of the path (unless you want to encounter a Mad Plant) and you find your way to a room with a wooden ladder and a mysterious glowing symbol on the wall.

Use HOVER while standing on the object and you will be lifted up to the right. Jump, walk down and use CYCLONE again to find yourself back in the lighthouse itself, this time behind the door which blocked the entrance.
