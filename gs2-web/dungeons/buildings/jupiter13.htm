Jupiter Lighthouse (Part XIV)




After you enter the right tower of the Jupiter Lighthouse, you should use the door in front of you because the door to the right will be a dead end first. Inside the next room you will soon notice, that some more repair work is waiting for you. This time you will face two tasks, first to arrange the missing blocks to reach the Jupiter Djinn Sue (picture above top right) and then to get to the exit. (picture bottom right). To get the blocks out of their holes you must use POUND on the iron pillar in the topmost right corner. The blocks will "pop" out and can be re-arranged.
