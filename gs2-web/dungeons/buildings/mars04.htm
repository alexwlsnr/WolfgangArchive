Mars Lighthouse (Part V)

When entering this floor via stairs (5), climb down the ladder and take your way to the left. Inside the chest on top of the leap you will find a Valkyrie Mail. Now go back and jump down the chute with two possiblities ahead.

Take the challenge to run against the moving and flame spitting dragonhead and reach the door before the huge dragonhead to the left will be lit and starts spitting fire and blast you down to the room you see on the right. In this case it would be wise just to wait a bit where you are standing until the fireball passes you.
Then you have more time than you will need and no obstacles any more on your way to the door to the left.




Your next task is to reach the exit displayed right above the stairways (5). Once you are behind the door next to the dragonhead, take your way up, but do not use the teleport circle you will eventually find. Pass it and venture forth until you enter the room with the second dragonhead. Block the fire, return to the teleport circle and use TRANSPORT now for a much safer warp back into the room with the dragonhead. Climb the stairs walk to the right and enter the next part of the dungeon.
