Anemos Temple (Part V)

Just a few "easy" puzzles and the last Combo Table is in reach. If you beat Durahan / Dulahan you can summon the ultimate combo spell, Iris.

You will need a total of four Mercury Djinns () and nine Venus Djinns () for a summoning of Iris.

One last tip: Move the stone block near the inner sanctum to form a spiderlike shape. It then will be lit. Step on it and face the guard of the temple (he is a hard one!!).
