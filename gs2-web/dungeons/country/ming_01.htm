Ming Moutains (Part II)

 Kibombo Moutains Northern Part

Ok, this is the northern part of Kibombo Moutains. Still, be careful not to be seen by the guards. The first guard must be "boxed" and to avoid that the second guard may notice you, you have to distract the cute little but also loud doggie. Clues how to manage that can be seen below.

The Jupiter Djinn (its name is Bouquet, the Japanese spelling is Bu-ke) can only be reached with Piers in your party. So you may leave him on top of the moutains and get him on your way back.

Use the passageway to reach the "plant ladders" to the right. Climb up and cross over the head of the guard to the left side of the map. Now venture down the hill and exit Ming Moutains by using the stepstone in the stream.

Inside the small cave you will find a Smoke Bomb in one of the pots. You may leave it where it is, because when leaving the cave you might get caught by the dog!
