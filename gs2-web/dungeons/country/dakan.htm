Dehkan Plateau (Part I)

 Entrance to Dehkan Plateau

South of Kandorea you will find the entrance to Dehkan Plateau.

The ground here doesn't seem to be very solid. You may fall through different cracks in the ground into one of many underground caves.

Sometimes its annoying but sometimes you have to choose the right "fall" to reach chests or to venture forth through the plains.
