Dehkan Plateau (Part VI)

 Finally, you are reaching the exit!

 So it should look like if you want to reach the wooden pillar in the center!


Unfortunatelly the Mars Djinn steps on the only pillar which could shorten the path. If you step on it, it will crumble. So you have to use the long way round!

If you finally reach him the djinn will jump into a hole in the ground. Choose the right hole to follow him. You must land on Cannons head. He then will drop a precious item, the Pound Cube.
