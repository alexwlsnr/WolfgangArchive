Yampi Desert (Part IX)

 The last and vast Part of Yampi Desert

You will enter from the west and have to find your way to the NORTHEAST exit. Don't use the sandfall to the southeast before you visited Alhafra Town. You will return here, just be patient. Otherwise you have to wander all around Osenia to re-enter Yampi Desert from the west only to return here!

Oh, I nearly forgot to tell you about the chest in the middle: it contains the Trainer's Whip , an item like the Mysterious Card which you found in the Kandorea Cave. The character which equips it changes his / hers class to that of a Beast Tamer.

Later during your quest, when Sheba has gained the REVEAL psynergy in Air's Rock return here for some goodies.

The arrow, for example shows the way to a cave hidden in Yampi Desert.

But you will need the SAND psynergy to get near it!
