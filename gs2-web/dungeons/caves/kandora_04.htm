Kandorea Temple Cave (Part IV)



Item to gain:LASH PEBBLE

Now only two obstacles are between you and the LASH psynergy. First MOVE the boiling kettle aside, pushing would only end in burned fingers.

the second obstacle is more serious: you have to fight three Sleepy Gorillas. Once they are beaten, a ladder will fall into the cave. So climb up, pass the next passage and you will come to a room just above the temple entrance.

Talking with Master Poi will take some time but in the end Kraden can convince Master Poi to give you the permission for using the Lash Pebble.
