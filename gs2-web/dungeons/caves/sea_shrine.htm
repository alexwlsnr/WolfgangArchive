Shrine of the Sea God (Part I)

 Use LASH to rescue the little boy!


During your quest you will enter this shrine two times. When visiting the cave for the first time two tasks are waiting for you. First you have to rescue one of the twins named Pecharo and Kupul. I cannot remember who of them is trapped in the left part of the first cave, they look too similar to me. You have to use LASH, a psynergy you will gain in Kandorea Temple. After the boys are "released" (and relieved) you can venture forth into the inner parts of the Sea God Shrine.

The right part of the cave is a dead end, so you should take the way to the left!
