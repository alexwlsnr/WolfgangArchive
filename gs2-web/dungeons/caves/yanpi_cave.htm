Yampi Hidden Cave (Part I)


To find the hidden cave in Yampi Desert you have to use REVEAL next to one of two stone formations. It then will show you the location of the cave. More information about this can be found in the Yampi Desert section on this site. Inside the first part of the cave (shown to the right) you can grab a Water of Life, then use TRANSPORT while standing on the stonecircle. You will be "warped" to the circle displayed on the bottom of the page.


Items to be gained
Water of Life
