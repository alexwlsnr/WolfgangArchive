Shrine of the Sea God (Part IV)


Depending which stairs you have chosen to climb up here, the Jupiter Djinn will cross your way over or (far better) under the bridge.
Just leave this part of the cave and re-enter it to follow him. Rescue will wait for you on the stone wall in the right part of the cave. Simply following him is not very helpful due to the fact that this nervous djinn only tries to flee from you round and round the stone wall.
Before you become dizzy you should push the candelabra to the position shown below. Now Rescue's way is blocked and after a short fight, he will join your party. That's all what can be done during your first visit, so leave the cave and try to cross the Dehkan Plateau.

With Piers in your party you now can freeze the puddles to the left to cross over the gap which was impassable before.
