Taopo Swamp (Entrance)



This is one of the "more" tricky puzzles in Golden Sun 2. Taopo Swamp can only be mastered if you use the bubbles of the swamp as "stepstones".

If you don't reach them in time Felix and friends will sink into the swamp. Don't fear to get drowned, you will be transported to the last dry spot where you entered the swamp, but you cannot reach the goodies like the one in the chest near the entrance.
