Kandorea Temple Cave (Part III)


Once you enter this cave you have to use the stairs to the left down to a room divided by an underground river. Cross it and you will enter another cave the a wooden statue shown below.

Push the statue over one of the marks on the ground and the free mark will be pushed up by a stream up water. Now stand on the mark for a natural elevator experience.

Use the right mark to get an item with magic abilities and the left mark to venture forth into the cave!

You will find the first of three items in Kandorea Cave which change a characters class when being equipped.

When equipping the Mysterious Card your character can use different magic but forgets about the psynergy known before. You can easily get back to the former class by simply unequipping the Card!

Item to be gained
Mysterious Card

Psynergy needed
MOVE
