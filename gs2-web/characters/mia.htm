Mia


Like Piers and Alex, this nice lady is a water adept. Her name is Mia and she lived in the small village of Imil next to the Mercury Lighthouse.

She was supposed to watch over the lighthouse together with Alex, but Alex quitted his job and changed sides. He then roamed the land together with Saturos and Menardi.

This was the main reason why Mia decided to join Isaac and friends. She felt responsible for what Alex was doing and wanted to help. This, more than any other fact, shows her kindness.

So, guys in the party, be nice, maybe you will have a chance!
