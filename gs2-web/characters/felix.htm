Felix


Felix is the main hero in the second part of the Golden Sun adventure.

He will be acompanied by his sister Jenna, a little girl named Sheba, which he tried to rescue as the Venus Lighthouse broke apart (first adventure!!), and the old scholar Kraden who follows the party as an advisor.

Although it looked like he was a bad guy in the first part (I mean, he supported Saturos and Menardi even when they took his own sister as a hostage!), it now becomes obvious that he has his own plans very different from those of the two mentioned above.

Felix tries to do everything that is needed to light all the four lighthouses, but for which purpose is not known until the very end of the story.
