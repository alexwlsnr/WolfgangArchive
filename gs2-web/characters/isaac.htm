Isaac


Yes, the main hero on the first episode of the story, Isaac will take part in this adventure, too.

You will meet him inside (or better outside) the Jupiter Lighthouse in a very dangerous situation alongside all his loyal followers. He will not join you here but once you finished your duty on top of the lighthouse, don't miss him in the town of Contigo southeast of the Jupiter Lighthouse.

In one of the houses Isaac will wait for you and after a conversation, which I could not follow (sorry about that) he and his party will join Felix and friends.

This will finally give you the control over eight heroes in total, you can mix the parties and use all psynergies. With the help of LIFT and FORCE, for example, you can enter places which you could never have dreamed of reaching before!
