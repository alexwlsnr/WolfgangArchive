Ivan


This litte wind adepth is called Ivan. He became friend of Isaac and Garet, as they helped him to find a very important item. So he decided to join both friends.

He still is a party member after so many adventures which finally brought him to the town of Contigo on the Attekan continent. He here will meet Master Hama again, who gained him the REVEAL psynergy back in the Lama Temple.

Master Hama seemed to know much more about Ivan himself but did not want to tell a thing. What the story about Ivan is, will be told here in Contigo, but damn, I did not understand a single word!

Once again, we all have to wait for the more common understandable version of this wonderful and richly detailed adventure!
