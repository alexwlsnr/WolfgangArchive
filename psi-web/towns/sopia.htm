Sopia (Motavia)

A town in ruins

This is a poor village surrounded by poisoned gas clouds. They are really deadly, the clouds, not the people.

Donate 400 mesetas to the village elder and he will tell you about the MIRROR SHIELD hidden on the small island in the lake of Motavia.

Unless you have this information the shield which is needed to beat Medusa cannot be found.
