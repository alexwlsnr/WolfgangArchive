Welcome to the Home of Phantasy Star Website

All those who know this site from the time back in 1997, when is was published may wonder about the new design. But I thought that the time finally had come to give the pages a more modern outfit. Right in time, because the game was relaunced for the Gameboy Advanced as a part of a Phantasy Star Collection, containing three games (Phantasy Star, Phantasy Star II and last but not least Phantasy Star III).

The contents have not changed much, I only added the story of Phantasy Star as written in the original game manual. If I have some time left I will try to transfer this outfit on the pages for Phantasy Star II and Phantasy Star IV as well, but this will take more then just four days which I spent on rebuilding this site with the new design. Maybe a new section for the still missing Phantasy Star III will follow.
