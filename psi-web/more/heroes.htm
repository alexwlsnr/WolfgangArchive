The four Heroes





 Alis
The main character of the adventure. Her brother was killed by Lassic. She has vowed of overthrow him to avange her brother's dead.



Myau
An unusual animal who looks like a cat. He speaks the luman language and is closely tied to Odin.



Odin
The second comrade of Alis. He also has taken a vow to overthrow the evil King Lassic.


Noah
He is an esper wizard and lives in Motavia. Best known for his magical powers,Noah is a close friend of the Governor.
