Lowe

Being a member of the first Shining Force, Lowe now serves as a chancellor for Queen Anri. After Queen Anri fell to sleep due to Cypress treachery, Lowe decides to accompany the new Shining Force as an adviser.
He awaits you in the headquarters when
you are forced to retreat or even if your troops need a short break. Lowe will resurrect characters, does the promotion job and many, many other things as you
will find out by yourself.
