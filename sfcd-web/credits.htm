Credits Section


As usual, here is the small part of the side where all those are listed, whom is to thank for in one or another way.

With their help, may it much or even less, they all took part in the creation of this site. Maybe they don´t even know, but without them, this site may have never been opened!

All members of Sonic Team, who did a marvelous job in programming this masterpiece of a strategic based RPG.
Only because I had much fun playing the game I decided to create a page dedicated to the Shining Force CD.
Jeff Quinn, programmer of WinAges, the first working Sega MegaCD emulator. Without the emulator, nothing would have be seen here.
Christian Schiller, maintainer of Eidolon´s Inn, the first source in web when it comes to background information about Sega consoles. He told me some tricks to make WinAges running.
Maintainers of VG Music, one of the best midi archives for video games worldwide.
Main source for many midi files played in this and other sections. The various composers of the midis are listed there, too.
Maintainers of RPG Lair, midi archive for RPG games. All midis not found in the archive above seem to be listed here. More information about the midis and their composers can be gained here.
