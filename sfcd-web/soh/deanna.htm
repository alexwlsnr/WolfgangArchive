Deanna

He is our hero in the second Shining Force CD adventure.
Like Nick in the first episode, Deanna is a mysterious guy, who was found wounded at the cliffs near Cypress castle. Nevertheless Deanna decides to fight alongside the Cypress troops to gain back the stolen Sword of Hayja.

Deanna starts as a Swordsman but can be promoted to Hero when he reaches Level 10.
