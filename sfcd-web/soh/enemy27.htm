Gordon

This ugly creature is the first underling of King Warderer you will meet. Don´t underestimate him due to his appearance. Because the location (the cave underneath the castle) is so narrow and he uses magic very often, you may get in trouble when trying a massive attack with many members at a time.

After Gordon is beaten, you will finally get the Sword of Hayja back. But no one in the party can be equipped with the sword.
