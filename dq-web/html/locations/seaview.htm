Seaview Chapel
Rest for free (overnight only)

Mini Medal
  North of Baccarat you will find Seaview Chapel.

  You will gain some information about Dhoulmagus
  here and (best of all) can rest until the next
  morning for free. That's what I call bargain!

  Find a Mini Medal inside the Chapel and behind
  the building there is a chest with a Tough Guy
  Tattoo inside. Don't miss those items.
