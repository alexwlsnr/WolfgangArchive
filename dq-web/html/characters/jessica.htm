Jessica   (Text taken from User's Manual)
The only daughter of an aristocratic familiy from a small village. Something of a tomboy, she is constantly arguing with her mother but is respected by her fellow villagers.

She is a budding sorceress who is adept not only at casting combat spells but at wielding short blades, whips, and staves as well.
