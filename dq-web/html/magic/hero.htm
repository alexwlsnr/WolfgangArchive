Hero's Skills in Detail
Here you will find a quick overview about the skills the hero will learn during your adventure. Move your mouse over the appropriate button to display the skill of your choice.

Oh, I nearly forgot, a (T) next to the skill name indicates that the effectiveness of this skill depends on the tension level the hero has the moment he will use the skill!
