Magic Spells
Here you will find a quick overview about the magic spells your party might learn during your adventure. Move your mouse over the appropriate button to display the spells of your choice.

Oh, I nearly forgot, a (T) next to the spell name indicates that the effectiveness of this spell depends on the tension level the character has the moment he or she will cast the spell!
