Kueri (Mota)

Kueri, the hometown of an Inventor

This town is located in the west. Visit the home of the Professor. He will ask you to do him a favour.



Do it, even if it is hard, you won´t regret it.

In fact, he will give you the Maruera Gum he made. Chew it and you can breath even under water.
