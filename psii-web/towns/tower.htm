Central Tower

 Entrance

After you enter the Central Tower of Paseo, you will find yourself here, in the main hall From here you can choose which room you will visit next.

 The Major

This is the Major of Paseo. He will give you orders an some advice. But he will not help you much during your quest.

Luggage Room

You can trust him. He looks after all your stuff which will not be needed at the moment. Store special items and other thing and get them back later when they are needed.

 Library

She will give you information about Mota, reads the recorder from Biosystem Lab and offers you a special item.

 Panic

This panic stricken looking guy will welcome you once the Climatrol Center has malfunctioned and the center part of Mota is flooded.
