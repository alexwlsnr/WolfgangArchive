Ryuon (Dezo)

Ryuon, the gateway to Guaron Dungeon

This town is located in the southeastern region of Dezo. The best thing about Ryuon is that it is the only place all over the Algo Star System where you can buy the Trimate medicine.

So return here very often to stock up your supplies.
