Boss Enemies


Last but not least, there are three boss enemies which you must beat to your way to glory and to set your mark in the history of the Algol Star System. One of them is well known, one is somehow looking familiar and the last is a cruel 'beauty'.

Neifirst, Nei's older 'sister'

Neifirst She is the older sister of Nei and the cause of the biohasard, which took place. Meet her near the main computer of the Climatrol Centre. Nei has to battle her alone. After the defeat of Nei (sad moment) your party will kill her for revenge. More details can be found in the walkthrough, if it will ever be finished.
