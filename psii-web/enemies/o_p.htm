Enemies O-P


Welcome to the enemy galery of Phantasy Star. This section is devided into separate parts, displaying the enemies alphabetically. Sorry, but you will only find a picture, the name and the typical location where the enemy can be found. I did not make any statistics about HP, EXP or Mesetas.

Enemies (O)
Picture

 Location
Ohx Looks like an Orangah with green slime spots.
Orangah This ape like creature awaits you at Skure spaceport first. It can heal himself.
Orangoo Lives all over Dezo. Like his relative, the Orangah, he has the ability to heal himself. On the other hand, this will help you to strike harder.
Owltalon This one is the strongest bird like creature on Dezo, and it always attacks in a group. Hard to beat.
