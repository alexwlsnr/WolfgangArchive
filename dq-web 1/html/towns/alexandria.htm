    Town of Alexandria
    The second town you will visit is the Town of Alexandria. The people here are shocked by the death of a young man named Alistair, who was killed under mysterious circumstances.

    Alexandria is the hometown of a young lady who will join your party a bit later. Her name is Jessica and she is the sister of Alistair. She wants to avenge the death of her brother.
