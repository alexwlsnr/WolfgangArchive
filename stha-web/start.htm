The "Home of Shining the Holy Arc" site welcomes you!

After finishing my last project ( Shining Force III ) after nearly a half year of constant work I had to decide what would be the next game to cover inside the Home of Classic RPGs website.

I hope that my choice will be OK for you, too: Shining the Holy Arc, which seems to be only natural, because this game is bound to the Shining Force saga and is the direct precestor of SFIII. Even characters from within SFIII are to be found in Shining the Holy Arc.

So, what is awaiting you when surfing the site? Due to the lack of computer speed and time I skipped the plan to show a mass of animations but decided to build up a standard screenshot website. Some rare animations are to be found.

Nevertheless you will find at least one picture of every enemy in the game, maps of all dungeons, screenshots of all main events and, what in my oppinion seems to be the best feature, a screenshot of every hidden item in the quest I could find! So enjoy, surf around, even those who played the game over and over might find some clues or secrets.

JAVA-Script code included, enable Java-Script on your browser.
Designed for a resolution of 800 x 600 pixels and 65.000 colors.
