Welcome to the Home of Phantasy Star Website (Section Phantasy Star III)

This is one of the most disappointing moments in my RPG carrier.

Wonder why? To tell the truth, I didn´t like the game much when playing it in 1991. Something was wrong with it.

Now, trying to build up the section, I discovered the cause. It´s the lack of effort programmers showed. I didn´t find more than TWO animation phases at any of the few foes shown here.

Seeing what the monster designers did in Phantasy Star I, II and IV, Phantasy Star III must be the work of novice programmers. Only the music composer, Ippo, did a good job.

I´m sorry, I will NOT finish this section. Maybe someone else wants to, but surely not me, allthough I am not quite sure about that now!
