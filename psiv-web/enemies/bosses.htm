Boss Enemies


This is the list of all main enemies of Phantasy Star IV, commonly known as boss enemies. They are displayed in alphabetical order, with a description where they can be found and some more or less information of interest.

The list below also includes the boss enemies of the sidequests you can 'book' in the Hunter's Guild.

 Description
Anger Tower Inside the Anger Tower you will meet Alys again. Being an illusion, you can and must defeat her. Easy to beat, but how do you feel, when fighting her?

Carnivorous Trees blocking the Garuberk Tower
Picture
 Location
 Description
Garuberk Tower This mysterious sort of plants block the entrance to the Garuberk Tower. When you visit them for the first time (when rescuing Kyra) simply retreat, because the fight will be endless due to the fact that the plant will grow faster than you can hit it. After you got back with the Eclipse Torch, this magical item will burn the trees to ashes. So your party finally reaches the Garuberk Tower.
