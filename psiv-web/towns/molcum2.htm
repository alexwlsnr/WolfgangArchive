Molcum (Motavia)

  Molcum, before Zio's invasion

After being treated not as equal society members on their own planet once the Palmanians settled on this planets more than 2000 years ago, the Motavian natives, blue-furred teddy like creatures, decided to separate from the Palmanian people.

Being aware of Zio's madness the Molcum inhabitants won't allow suspicious looking people to enter their village.
