You can find a Laser Slasher for Alys when walking around the town walls and enter the house in the northeastern corner.


Kadary can be reached by crossing a passageway with an entrance near the group of trees north of Aiedo. Kadary has an enormous church which was founded by Zio himself.

Some of the citizens seem to be very unhappy with this situation while others, commonly from other towns and villages, are weak-willed followers of Zio, having settled here to worship Zio.

Even members of Zio's guards roam the city and pubs. Who wonders that Zio does not allow Weapon- or Armory Shops in Kadary so the tool shop is the only one to be found.
