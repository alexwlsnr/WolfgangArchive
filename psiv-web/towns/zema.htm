Zema (Motavia)

  Zema, mysterious Town of the Ancients

If you enter Zema for the first time you will see stone figures all over the town. Zio, an evil magician, did fulfill his promise to turn everybody to stone if the Piata academy continues their research work in Birth valley.

Enter Birth Valley through the northern entrance to find out that Dr. Holt, leader of the research team, has been turned to stone like all the others. The shops and the inn will open if your party get their hands on the Alshlyn (Alsulin) Medicine to cure the citizens and academy members.

Refer to the table below for prices in Zema.

Later on you can grab another job of the Hunters Guild to return here for a third time. This little side adventure will lead you to a hidden control centre named Valhal. Not even Demi and Wren know of its existence.
