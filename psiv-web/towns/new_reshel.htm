You could have bought a total of 1095 burgers in Phantasy Star I for the price of only one Laconian Mail. And you need two, one for Chaz and one for Rika!


Shortly after you have beaten Dark Force for the second time (he is very persistant in this adventure) the survivors of the illness which spread over Reshel return here to rebuild it. Although not every house is renovated yet, you find an armory shop with the best protective items which money can buy.

Ok, Laconian items were hardly to be found in Phantasy Star I, so we are lucky indeed, that this time they can be bought in shops. But for these prices (we mentioned the millionaires, didn´t we)?
