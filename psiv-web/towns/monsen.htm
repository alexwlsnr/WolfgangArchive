Return here after you have taken the assignment for the Hunter's Guild job: 'Fissure of Fear'.


With Demi in your party and the landrover you now can cross over the quicksand regions to reach Monsen. Here a series of earthquakes caused demolition. Even deep holes appeared in the city like the one in the eastern part. Later, one of the jobs of the hunters guild will bring you back here, to the fissure of fear.

Not only the people who live here shake in fear of the earthquakes. Gryz seems to dislike shaking earth as well. So if you decide to help drive north to the Plate Control Centre instead of continuing south to Termi Town. Nonetheless, whatever you will do next, visit the Tool Shop of Monsen if needed.
