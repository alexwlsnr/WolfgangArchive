Meese (Dezoris)

  Meese, the town where Raja gets seriously ill

Just one advice, be careful when entering the houses of Meese, if you plan to keep Raya in your party.

He will get ill shortly after you speak to one of the espers who try to cure the ill citizens of Meese. Raja gets infected inside the greater building in the east part of the city. Sad for Raya, but do not worry, unlike Alys Raya will recover under the care of the espers.

Your next task will be to hurry helping Kyra, an esper girl. Rescue her from the carnivorous trees northeast of Meese. She will be your admission ticket to the Esper Mansion.

But before you attempt rescuing Kyra you should go shopping first. One of the shops in Meese is closed because the owner got sick, too. So return here later!!
