Reshel (Dezoris)

 Reshel, town of the Zombies

Can you imagine Dezoris without any Zombies? Like Guaron Fort in Phantasy Star I, your group will find only zombies in this deserted town.
A mysterious desease which is spreading over Dezoris, transformed all people to these undead creatures.

Rune supposes that a black energy wave causes the illness. Being well equipped the enemy zombies will be no problem. but they will appear nearly after every move your party makes.

Due to the fact that you won´t gain anything here but bruises, leave immediately. After Dark Force is beaten the black energy wave will vanish and the surviving town citizens will rebuild their houses.

If you are interested how the town looks like after the defeat of Dark Fork in Garuberk, simply press the button 'New Reshel'.
