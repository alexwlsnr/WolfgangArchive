Esper Mansion (Dezoris)

  Esper Mansion, home of Rune and Kyra


With Kyra in your party, the guards will no longer block the doorway and you can enter the mansion.

Some rare item can be found here and as long nobody does anything against it, take from the chests whatever you need. You will find:

  • 1 Laconian Rod
  • 1 Reflection Robe
  • 1 Sol Dew
  • 10000 Mesetas

So much will be revealed in the room to the right, that you should refer to the walkthrough for details.
