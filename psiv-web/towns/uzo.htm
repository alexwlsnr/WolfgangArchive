Uzo is to be found in the southwest of Motavia and can be reached only if you are in possession of the Hydrofoil, which is an improved version of the old Hovercraft of Phantasy Star I. The two reasons to travel over the ocean to visit the village on an island:

  1. A job of the hunters guild, as usual
  2. Buying Trimate in the Tool Shop

Uzo had to be visited even in Phantasy Star I and Phantasy Star II. Former located in the middle of the desert planet of Motavia in Phantasy Star I, it was an abandoned island in Phantasy Star II.

The planet forming done by Mother Brain in this adventure transformed Motavia into a green planet with plenty of water. So it happened that Uzo was surrounded by water 1.000 years after Alis' quest revenging the early death of her brother Nero.

It seems that after the Palma desaster some people stranded or landed (rhyme as rhyme can) here and rebuilt Uzo. So this little village is the oldest known settlement of people in all Algol. No other town than Uzo took part in Phantasy Star I, II and even IV.
