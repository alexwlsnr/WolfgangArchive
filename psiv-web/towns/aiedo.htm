Aiedo (Motavia)

  Aiedo, hometown of the Hunter's Guild


Although your quest starts in Aiedo you are not able to control your party until Alys and Chaz reach Piata, the town of learning. But do not worry, you will return here later. Aiedo is the biggest town in the whole solar system of Algol. So many interesting things can be found, bought or searched for. First of all it is the town where the hunters guild is located. The building in the northern part of the city is their resting place with many information and even jobs to fullfill.

You can use the inn for 250 bucks to stock up some health but it will be whiser to enter Alys´ and Chaz´ house located in the northwest part. Here you can sleep for free, naturally.

The biggest warehouse is located here, too. But all you can buy there, is normally of weaker nature. The better equipment can be bought in the Hunter´s Shop below the hunters guild. Check yourself for details:
