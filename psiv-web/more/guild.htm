Hunter's Guild

 The lady behind the counter

Profession:Secretary
Race:Human
Age:not of interest (?)
Hometown:Aiedo


If you are looking for a job, visit this young lady in the Hunters Guild of Aiedo. If there are any vacancies, she will inform you about this. In the side quests you can gain money and experience points.
Below you will find details about the sidequests offered.

1. The Ranch Farmer

Help this poor man in your first job from the Hunter's Guild. He raised worms to earn money (no one knows what the heck sandworms are good for!).

Kill the big one for a mass of money and experience.


2. Tinkerbell's Dog

Find the little doggie of the girl who ran away from her home in Aiedo. Surely not a job for a real hunter, but can you stay calm, while a little girl is crying her eyes off for help?


3. Missing Student

This girl is missing from the students hotel in Piata. The owner is afraid of the consequences for his inn and therefore asks for help.

4. Fissure of Fear

This little curious boy has fallen in a fissure near his parents house. Try to get him back before it is too late.

5. Stain in Life

You have to rescue these two teenagers from themselves. Sometimes it would be better to listen to the parents (oh, the worried dad in me is speaking).

6. Dying Boy

This one's a bit more serious. Buy a special item in the gift shop of Termi to rescue the boy (oh, it is neither the woodcarvin nor the pennant!).

7. Man without Twist

People, who risk lives of others only to save birds should be examined by a psychiatrist. This guy surely needs help you can't offer.

8. The Silver Soldier

Last but not least an old man of Zema asks for help. This town is under attack again. Help him out to gain money, better equipment and not to forget a mass of experience points. Enter this house in Zema for details.
