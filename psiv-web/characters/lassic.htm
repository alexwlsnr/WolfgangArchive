The Foes


 Emperor Lassic




Profession:King of Algol
Race:Human
Age:over 2,000 years
Hometown:Air Castle


An old 'friend' of ancient times has come back to live. Although becoming older than any other characters of Phantasy Star (even Wren with his 998 years is no more than a 'grand-grand-child') he has not become wiser.

Meet him in the Air Castle after he ordered three of his followers to steal the Eclipse Torch, which, in former times played a major role in the quest of Alis and her friends in Phantasy Star I.
