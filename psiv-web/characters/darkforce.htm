The Foes


 Dark Force


Profession:Personified Evil
Race:unknown
Age:unknown
Home:Algol


Without him, the series of Phantasy Star would never have been what it is. This personified evil lurks in his hideout only to roam the Algol solar system every thousand years.

He made his first appearance 2,000 years ago, when Alis tried to take revenge for the death of her brother.

Then he awaited Rolf and friends on board of the Noah spaceship 1,000 years ago.

And now Chaz has to confront him again not only once but three times!

Is this guy too stupid to realize that he has lost?
