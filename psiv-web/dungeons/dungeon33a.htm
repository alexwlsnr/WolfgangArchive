Ok, let's try it together. As mentioned before, this dungeon is the most complex structure of your quest. Luckily it is straight through, you can not get lost in longer dead ends. So follow the description and everything will be alright:

After crossing the entrance you enter the first main hall at 2. The middle door will be blocked by the ghosts until you have kicked the butts of their real bodies. Do not go to the left, it is a dead end with no treasure chests. Go through door 4 and you will find yourself within Map 4.

After leaving the building through door 5, cross the courtyard to reenter the building at 6. Climb the stairs, pass door 7 and walk outside to the entrance No. 8 only to leave the building again at door 9. Reenter through entrance 10 and walk inside the main hall (refer to Map 2) to the left. If you decide to go through door No.11 you will gain nothing but a breeze of cool air outside the Air Castle. So let's leave the building through exit 12.

Enter through entrance 13 and continue your way by passing exit 16. If you want to get your hands on the Swift Helmet, descend the stairs first. You will find yourself back in Map 3 on the other side of the wall. Door 15 leads to the courtyard, but due to the fact that parts of the floor have broken apart here, there is no way through. Go back to 16, cross over to the back part of the Castle outside the building and, after gaining a Genocyclaw inside the building with door No. 17, reenter again through entrance 18.

The end of a long walk is in sight, exit the building at 19. Instead of continuing through door No. 22, your party, even if only one is alive, should reenter the main building through door No.20. Here you find a refresh point which fills up all HP, MP and skills and the best of all, it revives party members. Now finally cross the courtyard and continue ...
