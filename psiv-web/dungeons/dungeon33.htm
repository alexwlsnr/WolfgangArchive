Air Castle (Outerspace)

This is what we call a dungeon. Huge and complex but it can and must be conquered. We have to get back the Eclipse Torch. Prepare yourself well and keep in mind always to carry an Escapipe with you for a hasty retreat. If you stay alive until the very end, a surprise is waiting for you.
If you dare to enter please note that the maps of the Air Castle are devided into three main parts.
