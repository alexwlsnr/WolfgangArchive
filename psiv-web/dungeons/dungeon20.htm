Dezoris Spaceport (next to Tyler)

Use the Landale Spaceship to continue your flight to Kuran. Wasn't Alis Landale the full name of our heroine Alis in Phantasy StarI ?



Like the spot in the Vehicle Control Centre you can use the spots left and right of the entrance to refill the HP and MP of your group.

This is highly recommended if you plan to fly directly to Kuran.
