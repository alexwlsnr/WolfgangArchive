Dezoris

The frozen planet called Dezoris, too, had to be visited by Alis and friends. She once looked for a precious item inside the Gumbius Tower.

As the party of Rolf was here 1,000 years ago, the towns of Zosa and Ryuon could be found on this planet. Although much has changed during the times long forgotten, some things are still of existance like the Esper Mansion, where Rolf visited an Esper called Lutz.
