Raculi is the hometown of Ronfar, Mauri and Leo. In former days everything was nice and peaceful, Ronfar was in love with Mauri, but then one day, Mauri fell ill to an unknown disease like most of the towns people. Being a priest of Althena Ronfar tried to save them and at last he managed it somehow to keep Mauri alive, but her personality had changed completely. Nothing was as it had been.

Ronfar then left Raculi, decided to quit his priest job for living as a gambler and wanted never to return here. But now he is back and again, his task is to save Mauri from her destiny.

You will meet Leo who came to save his sister but with different methods as you will find out soon. He leaves Raculi heading to Serak Palace, you should follow him as fast as possible for Mauri's sake.
