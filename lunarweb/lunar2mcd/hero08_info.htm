Heroes Four Dragons

White Dragon:
He seems to be, again, the wisest dragon of all. Find him and you will get the White Dragon Crest. You now can use the best defense spell ever:
White Dragon Protect.

Blue Dragon:
To free the Blue Dragon you have to defeat the Blue Fiend. As a gift for your heroic deed you will get the Blue Dragon Crest.
Its unmatched magic is: Blue Dragon Vigor.
Black Dragon:
Again you have to free the dragon from a magic slumber. His donation is the Black Dragon Crest. Limbo Magic: Black Dragon Shield.
Red Dragon:
This is the last of the four dragons whose magic was sealed away by Zophar and his underlings. Get the Red Dragon Crest to cast: Red Dragon Anger.
