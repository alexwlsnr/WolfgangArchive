Towns Zabak


After the Neo Vane beam hit the Destiny your cruiser is "frozen". You have to walk by foot through the Zabak mines just to end here.

Not much to tell about Zabak. Its a run down town of so called loosers (Borgan's oppinion). Nonetheless he forces these "loosers" to work for him in the Zabak mines until exhaustion.

All sorts of pople are living here who have only one in common: no or minor abilities of Magic. Besides, one curious thing: one of the towns people is Lemina's grandfather.

Nice weapons and equipment can be found in the bazaar. A visit would be a good idea before entering the transporter to Neo Vane.
