The unofficial "Home of Lunar Eternal Blue"

What can I say? A dream came true. And the result of this dream lies before your very eyes. A complete new site dedicated to the best role playing game ever published for the MegaCD: LUNAR Eternal Blue.

Since the days back in 2000 when I opened the Lunar2 pages for Playstation, I wanted to do the same for the original. Believe me, this site and, of course the game itself, have no need to hide themselves from the Playstation version.

Ok, enough smalltalk, surf and enjoy. If you like to get more information about the site take a look in the News section.
