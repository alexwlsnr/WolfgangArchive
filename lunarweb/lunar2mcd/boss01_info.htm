Tips

Waking up
Do you remember the creepy statue at the entrance to the Blue Spire? You can't sneak around the Guardian once he is awake. The main goal is staying alive, which is no easy task. Hiro has to do the main work while Gwyn should concentrate on healing the party. Lucia, weak as she is due to Zophar's curse spell, will be no help at all.

Offenser
Guardian uses his club like a morning star. Luckily, the club will split to tiny pieces after being used on Hiro's head. Nontheless, be prepared.

Normal Attack
Even without a weapon, Guardian won't give up. Smashing hit (without any boxing license!) might kill party members with low HP. Let Gwyn cast healing spells until his mouth gets dry!
