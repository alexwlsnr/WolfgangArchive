Nightmare
This attack will hit a single party member. Nothing really to fear about.

Be Vine Tonight
Being caught by the vine means no magic spells anymore. So try to wiggle off the vines by manual attacks as soon as possible. Especially when Hiro is effected.

Vine Attacks
Corpse Crusher can use his vines to smack a single person twice or two persons once. Simple monster mathematics!

Pulsinator
A glowing chest indicates the coming of Pulsinator. Devastating explosions all around the scene cause damage to everyone, with the exception of Corpse Crusher, of course.
