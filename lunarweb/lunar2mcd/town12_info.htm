Towns Pentagulia

Pentagulia, at last. At the harbour Ronfar, Jean and Lemina will split from the team for some private meetings. But, more important, Lucia has finally reached the town and Leo in person will help to arrange an audiance with the Goddess Althena. Everything seems to be ok. Still, something mysterious goes on in the Holy Town. How can it be, that Ghaleon is here? The answer to this question will be given very soon.

There is so much to happen here (I won't tell you) but less to explore. If you like, search your friends in the towers to gain some information before visiting the goddess.

No shops in Pentagulia! But you can find two valuable items in the goddess palace later.
Items to gain:
Angel Ring, Lynx Tail

 Advice: (although not very helpful)
Keep your guards up!
