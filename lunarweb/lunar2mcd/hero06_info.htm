Heroes Leo

This part-human, part-beast is a staunch supporter of the Goddess Althena. In fact, he is the leader of Althena´s Guard, a group of elite soldiers sworn to protect and uphold the orders of the Goddess.

Althena has granted Lord Leo the use of the Dragon Ship Destiny. The Destiny allows him to roam freely around the world of Lunar to keep the inhabitants save. His skill as a swordsman is unmatched by any human.

He is searching for a Destroyer that has come to Lunar. Can Hiro help him find it?
