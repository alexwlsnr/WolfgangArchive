Towns Nota ( II )

Leo got your party, or to be more precise, he thinks he got you. But Hiro and friends again find a way to escape. Leave Nota and head for the Carnival.

It will take some time but you will return to Nota once again. So let's take advantage of your attention to point out two minor tips:

  • Visit the house on the left at foot of the cliffs to find a Star Light ( ).
  • When you are able to cross the bridge, meet a guy in the bar for a special job. Deliver the Special Vine ( ) to the house mentioned above for 10 Silver until getting bored or (most unlikely) the bridge will break apart.
