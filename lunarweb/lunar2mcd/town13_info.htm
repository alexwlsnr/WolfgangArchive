Towns Horam

After Jean discovered the truth about Lunn in Pentagulia, she knows that hiding from the past will not help any longer. Realizing that, she decides to confront her former "master" in a one to one fight. So she drops her outfit (sad to say, the movie starts too late!!) and changes to a karate fighter once again.

Sell all here dancing equipment in the shops, buy the stuff you need most, board the Destiny and venture forth to the Battle Arena.
