Sidecasts Ghaleon

This ghost from the past will emerge from the shadows to forge a new alliance. In times past, he fought for the protection of the Goddess, alongside the legendary heroes of that time.

Since he last appeared on Lunar, he has mastered a host of new magics, making him extremely powerful. He seems to be missunderstood by the people of Lunar. Some believe that he is serving the Goddess, while others are convinced that he´s turned to the service of evil.

You´ll have to decide where his allegiance lies yourself.
