Fighting Underlings
Remember the scene when Hiro and friend stumble over the kidnappers down in the Meribian Sewers?

Instead of fighting a one to one fight when being challanged by Jean, the masked coward sends his assassins to fight your party. To minimize the number of attacks, Ronfar should use Herbal Breeze and Jean can perform some Needle Step to put the enemies to silence one by one. The fight will be much easier then.

After the fight is over you will gain a rare trophy:
the Mysterious Mask.
