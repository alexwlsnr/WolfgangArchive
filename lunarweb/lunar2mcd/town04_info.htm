Towns Gipsies

Ok, not much to say about this location with one exception: You will meet Jean!

Some vines are blocking the pathway and the carnival folks cannot follow their trail to this years festival. To make things worse, an ugly creature from the underworld sucks some of these normally happy people down to its private Hades.

Something must be done and quick,of course. Guess, who will be sent on a rescue trip!

You cannot do much unless Jean becomes a member of your recsue team, so speak to her and convince her to join.
