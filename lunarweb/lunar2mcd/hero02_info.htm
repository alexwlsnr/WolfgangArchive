Heroes Ronfar

Ronfar is rumored to have been a priest for the Goddess Althena. He possesses impressive healing powers. When he was unable to save his true love from an evil spell, he swore he would never use magic again. He now spends most of his time in bars winning people´s money.

He also tends to make decisions on a throw of a dice. His dice, like his charm, seem to be magic, because they never let him down. He´s a womanizer who´s always concerned about the fairer sex, and they´re usually all too happy to return his affections.
