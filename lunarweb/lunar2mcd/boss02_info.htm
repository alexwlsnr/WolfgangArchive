Tips

Bad Seed
Ok, so the easter eggs hidden in the Illusion Woods look like. The changing of the color indicates the cast of Bad Seeds, which should be avoided by the party.

Stripped Off
After being hit several times Pantella reveals her real "beauty". Continue with Poe Sword (Hiro) and Needle Step (Jean), while Ronfar should heal the party.

Thorn Shower
This nasty attack will injure all the heroes who try to save the carnival people from being eaten by Plantella. Careful with this one.

If Plantella turns to her shiny pink outfit, the Insane Shocker spell comes in the next round.

Vine Attack
As if the attacks shown above are not enough, Plantella sometimes will use her underground vines to snag single party members.
