Towns Hiro's Home

Home, sweet home.
If Hiro would have known what will await him in the near future, maybe he would never have left this cosy place. Me, like Ronfar, never will bet on that.

Nonetheless, let's start the adventure with some advice. After Leo finally left Hiro's home and the Blue Spire showed some spectacular illumination, get the Ancient Knife from the chest in the basement, take the left dragon eye from Gwyn and leave home.

Hit some minor enemies nearby and return to Althena's statue for some free refreshment. Later on in the adventure you may have to pay for this goddess service but not here.
Items to gain:
Ancient Knife, Left Dragon Eye

 Note:
Instant heal at Althena's Statue
