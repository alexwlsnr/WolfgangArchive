NALL
This mysterious stranger is the leader of the Dragon Kids (also known as the Lostest Boys) of Taben´s Peak. Because of his impressive fighting skill and strong leadership, he´s earned the respect of his loyal followers.
Granted, they´re just orphan children who don´t know any better, but they need someone to look up to, even if it is this guy. Due to his recent raids are travelers, he´s become a wanted outlaw. Fortunately, he´s got a master plan.

Seek him out, as he has many secrets that will no doubt aid you in your quest.

(Text taken from the Official Strategy Guide (Sega CD)
