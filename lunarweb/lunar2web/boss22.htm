 Healing Guard

This guy either heals the entire party normally or sucks your HP to do this trick. He should be your first aim.
Tip:

Concentrate on the Healing Guard first. His abilities to heal his party and even suck your HP can and will make the fight longer and longer.

After the fight the legendary ocarina of Alex is yours. You now can listen to all music themes of the game whenever you like.

 Magic Guard

Weak against physical attacks but very strong against magic, this Guardian will use different magical spells against your party.
Knight Guard

He is strong against physical attacks but very weak against magic. His abilities are limited to normal attacks against a single person or (by summoning an obelisk) against all fighters within range.
