 Black Dragon Fiend normally starts with this attack.

It will suck a mass of MP from all party members. Bad luck, you can do nothing against it.
Try to prevent it with Jean using the White Dragon Protect because she is the fastest. But there will be no guarantee.
Tip:
Enter the Black Dragon Lair after the Black Dragon Fiend is history.

Free the Dragon with the crest given by Borgan.

With your new medallion you can suck all enemies into a limbo (but you will get neither EXP nor silver for this!).
Above and on the left you see the Closed Wings Attacks. Both forms above indicate a 1 person attack while the one with the purple flames (on the left, of course) causes damage to the entire party.
