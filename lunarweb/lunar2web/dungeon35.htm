NEVER ENDING CORRIDORS
(ILLUSION WOODS)

Dont´t ask me how to get to the end of the this dungeon.

The only tip I can give is never to use the Warp Stars in
a straight line horizontally or vertically.
They only lead to a point you visited before.

If you finally found your way to the end,
you will meet the three musketeers (sorry, Guardians)!

Oh, I nearly forgot. You will find Alex´s Ocarina inside the chest, the second best item in the adventure besides the Memory Crystal. Play all your favourite tunes any time you like!
