 If White Dragon Fiend looks like the ugly guy on the left, he will cast an Ice Wall spell which causes damage to all party members.

Below you can see two more attack forms. The one on the left indicates an other Ice Attack against your group, while the one on the right (with the Pentagram) shows the coming up of his Power Pressure. White Dragon Fiend increases his ATTACK and DEFENSE with this magic.
Tip:

A big surprise is waiting for Ruby. For the first time in her life she will see the transformation of Nall into his original form.

The White Dragon Medallion is yours now.
White Dragon Fiend will use his unique Ice Scream attack on one person in this round. Keep an eye on your party´s HP.
