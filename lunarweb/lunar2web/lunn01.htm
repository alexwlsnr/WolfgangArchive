LUNN
Lunn is the current governor of Meribia. He looks after many of the towns in the aera, keeping them out of harm´s way.
He has also established the town of Horam to further instruct people in the martial arts. The people that know Lunn think he is the greatest of the Four Heroes, but he views himself as a humble servant to the Goddess, at least in public.

Some have come to question Lunn´s true motives. If only they knew his secret...

(Text taken from the Official Strategy Guide (Sega CD)
