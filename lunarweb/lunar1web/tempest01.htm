HEROES SECTION
TEMPEST
Prairie Prince

After Tempest and Fresca saved you from an ambush in the Tamur Pass you will meet him once again in Tamur.

Tempest is the leader of the Prairie Tribe and although he and Alex fight against each other they become good friends at last.

Without a gift given by Tempest you will not be able to pass the Forest of Illusion, or enter the Black Dragon Fort.

Later in your quest Tempest will help you entering the Grindery by fighting the hordes of evil in your place.
