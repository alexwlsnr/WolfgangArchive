Teiris, Queen of the Palemoon Kingdom (Part I)
Teiris, a true beauty

This charming offspring of the elves possesses an unusually strong magic.

Even though she was recently chosen to be Palemoon's Queen, she is still unsure of herself. Though her face is that of an angel, it masks the steely resolve to keep the kingdom of Palemoon safe from attack by the surrounding jackals.

Do not underestimate the power of her friendship.
