Gongos, Guardian of the Forest

Despite his diminutive appearance, Gongos rules the jungle of Bozack with the strength of thousand beasts.

He is a very generous and chivalrous person who hates crafty strategies and unnecessary plunder. He is held dear by his subjects, who have named him the "Guardian of the Forest".
