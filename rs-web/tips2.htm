Tips for Playing The Royal Stone (2)
After you examined everything, talked to the townspeople and eventually bought one or two (or even more) items you should look for and find Eva. She ´hides´ in different places. Talk to her and she asks you if you want to continue the journey.


Normally you cannot depart the village or towns unless all important things have been done. If Eva won´t respond in the way shown below, you should tour the location again.
Depart
 Depart (Translation)
If you are sure that you got all you need you should answer Yes to Eva´s question. Otherwise choose No and explore a bit more.
