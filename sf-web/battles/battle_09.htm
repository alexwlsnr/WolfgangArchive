Battle 9


  Free the men of Bustoke

All men of Bustoke are forced to do miners work for the Runefaust army. Stop this madness here, once and for all.

Before the fight make sure to visit all houses of Bustoke.

You will meet Diane here, and archer, who joins and get some clues how to cure wolfman Zylo, a mighty beast and a marvelous addition to your force. Oh, and speak to Kokichi after the battle, otherwise he will not join later.

Enemies you meet:
Skeletons
Dark Elfs
Dark Priests
Lizard Man
Master Mage

Master Mage is the main enemy.
