Battle 12


  On your way to Urbanatol you meet General Elliot

Visit the traveling town Pao, then move on to Urbanatol. Only General Elliot and his army stand between you and the harbour town. One of the Lizard Men carries a Heat Axe. Make sure to get it.
And if you want a second one, use Egress and restart the fight!
