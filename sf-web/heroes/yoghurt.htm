This little hamster is the cutie of your troop. Leave him as he is,
he can not be promoted anyway. If he beats an enemy he gains
the Yoghurt Ring. If you equip this ring to any other group member
it will transform to Yoghurt, too.

Refer to the special event section of chapter 4 to see how he
will become a Shining Force member.
