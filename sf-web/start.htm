The "Home of Shining Force" site welcomes you!

From time to time "good" pages in the www need to be improved, too.

After being online now for 5 years in its original outfit, the Home of Shining Force pages were a bit outdated in design and navigation. So I decided to rebuild them in a new outfit. Fans of the former pages might miss some details (like the inside screenshots of the buildings) but they were not really neccessary. On the other hand, I tried to improve the navigation a bit, because you easily could get lost on the old pages. But decide for yourself.

About the game Shining Force, what can I tell much more? All fans of this series loved it for its gameplay and the wonderful detailed graphics. This game started a whole new series of strategic based RPGs and offered brilliant pictures never seen before on the Genesis / Megadrive up to the year1993 when the game was published.

Yes, the game has its 10 years anniversary this year, so the new design can be taken as a birthday present for the game as well!
