In the second part of this section some more or less important events or tips are listed.


You start your adventure in Guardiana, the hometown of the main hero Max and his friend Lowe. The King in person sends Max to investigate rumors that the enemy has been seen near the Gate of the Acients. While Max and friends are on their way to the gate, an earthquake takes place.

Max has to fight the evil Runefaust army to return back to his hometown. But in the meantime the enemy has overrun Guardiana. The king and his best knight Varios are killed by Kain, a general of the Runefaust amy. Max and frie3nds can't do anything but watch helpless.

Now the Shining Force wanders north to the town of Alterone. This town, too, is in the hands odf Runefaust. But Max and his pary manage to free this town. And so the first chapter ends.
