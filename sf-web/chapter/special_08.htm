Special Events Chapter 8

Near the town entrance in Prompt, you will notice a bush with flowers. Search here and you will find Hanzou. This ninja is the last hidden character of the game.



Use the Chaos Breaker at this point to rise the sunken Castle of the Ancients. Now you can use the entrance in the building above this point.



The Castle of the Ancients rises again.


Shortly after you finally defeated Dark Sol, the evil guy uses his last remaining power to revive Dark Dragon. Be prepared for this last confrontation. If you use EGRESS or an Angel Wing now you must beat Dark Sol a second time.
