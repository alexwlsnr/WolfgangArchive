In the second part of this section some more or less important events or tips are listed.


There is one town to be visited in chapter 3, Bustoke, which was built right into the side of a huge moutain. All men of Bustoke are forced into slave labor for the Runefaust army, so do your best to set them free again.

The next challenge in this chapter will be the fight against the Laser Eye on a narrow path north of Bustoke.
