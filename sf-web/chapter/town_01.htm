Towns and Places of Chapter 1


  Guardiana Town

People you should not miss:



The daughter of Varios, Mae, will join to avenge her father, who was killed by Kain.

The old-fashioned dwarf warrior Gort awaits you in the house to the top right. After Guardiana is overrun by the Runefaust army, he joins as a member of the Shining Force.


  Hut next to the Gate of the Ancients

People you should not miss:



This monk called Gong will join once you speak to him. Try to get him before the first battle starts (Use Egress and walk to the hut by foot).

You will meet Yogurt here for the first time. No way to get him in your party now, but wait a bit longer, you will meet him again!


  The Town of Alterone

People you should not miss:



Young healer Kris will help you to set you free from prison. SAhe then decides to help you as a Member of the Shining Force.
