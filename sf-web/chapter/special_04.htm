Special Events Chapter 4

Visit the church when being in Pao for the first time. If you stand near the priest you will spot Yoghurt for the second time. He awakens and will join your crew automatically.





In one of the tents a strange guy will wait for you. His name is General Elliot.
He is a truesome follower of King Ramladu of Runefaust, which was former known as Granseal. He will tell you more about the invasion and who is responsible for the war. Sad to say so, but you have to kill him when meeting him again!



After Pao left for a new camp place, Kokochi will land as a new member of the Shining Force. Now walk around the deserted place to meet Vankar. He decided to split from Pao only to come with you.



In Pao everything and everyone seems to be on the move. After defeating Elliot in the prarie, you will meet Guntz, the Machine Warrior, inside the sheepyard. The professor in Rindo finally fixed the problems with the suit and so Guntz becomes a party member.



If you ever wondered what to do with the Domingo Egg you found in the statue in Manarina, you should talk to this guy in one of the tents. In his machine Domingo will hatch from its egg and, voila, you got a new friend.



You will find this strange item which has no use (?) here. You must find it during the battle in Urbanatol town. Afterwards it has vanished.
