Special Events Chapter 1

To get your hands on the chest which is located on the little island in Alterone you have to hit the little car so that the little lady will get a little mad about her spoiled dress. She throws you in the little lake so that you can swim a little to the island (Littleary speaking).


After your party rescued Alterone from the Runefaust army, the king will tell you a secret about a hidden doorway to Rindo village. Search the mask to reveal the passage.
