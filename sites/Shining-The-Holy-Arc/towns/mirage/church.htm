Mirage Village Church

Mirage Church's inner outfit does not differ from those of Desire and Enrich. Meanwhile you should know what you can do here.

Churches are very important during your quest due to the quest that they offer special services to their visitors. Not all of them are free of charge:

Saves the progress of your adventure

Free of charge
Revives unconscious party members

Pay a fee
Cures status changes like Poison, Paralyse, Curse

Pay a fee
Promotes party members (min. Level 20) to a higher class

Free of charge
