Desire Village Blacksmith

Although (at the beginning) it seems that this guy can't do anything for you he will be the most visited guy at the end of your quest.

The Blacksmith needs and too craft wonderful accessory and equipment.
For details what you can expect from him, refer to the Blacksmith Section of this site.

Fees for crafting: 800 coins for items, 12000 coins for items
I nearly forgot: in the barrel right to the Blacksmith you can find an Arcane Garlic
