Techniques

One this page you will find a short overview about the various techniques, which will be used by the different characters. Sorry, there are no pictures on this page, but in other sections of the site, there are so much of them, that they might help to forget this flaw.

Over thousands of years the Algo Star System has formed and created many magic techniques. And different life forms, such as humans and Biomonsters, have evolved their own special and protective magics.

Some techniques you'll learn. Others you'll earn as you succeed in battle. The most valuable are those techniques you receive as presents from other beings in the Algo Star System.

Use the techniques for attack, defense, healing, and bringing back to life. As you travel with your comrades, you'll discover the unique knacks each one has for using techniques.

Anti Magic

Cleanses poison from the body of anyone who's been poisoned by Biomonsters. Only two comrades can use this to help the others.
Deban Magic

Creates a dense air barrier that keeps the enemy from attacking.
Doran Magic

Impairs an enemy's aim so his attacks miss their mark.
Fanbi Magic

Use this to draw life power away from your enemy and into yourself.
Foi Magics

Available in three strengths: Foi, Gifoi, and Nafoi.
They compress the oxygen in the air until ignition, enflaming the enemy it's directed at.
Gifoialso burns through metal.
Nafoiincinerates an enemy to ashes.
Gra Magics

Its three forms, Gra, Gigra, and Nagra, create sudden gravitational waves that compress whatever is in the area.
Gigrapressure crushes a body or object into a tiny leftover.
Nagraaction is so fast the victim explodes, very effective in narrow corridors.
Hinas Magic

Use this to escape from mazes.
Machine Magics

These were created during the research and development of new machinery.
Brosemagic damage computers so they can't control machinery.
Contemagic damage computers so they can't control machinery.
Crashmagic weakens machinery.
Eijiacorrodes machinery so that it falls apart.
Forsamakes machinery malfunction
Rimetparalyzes machinery.
Ner Magics

This technique increases the comrade's speed.
Nerspeeds up one comrade.
Sanerspeeds up all comrades.
Numbing Magic

This magic is giving you the chance to escape by numbing the inflicted enemies.
Rimitnumbs and confuses an enemy for a short time.
Shimbnumbs and confuses an enemy for a short time.
Res Magics

In all three forms it heals wounds.
Resis regular strength.
Giresworks faster than Res.
Naresis the most effective of all.
Rever Magic

Brings dead comrades back to life. Ineffective in cities.
Ryuka Magic

Transports you to the city where your travel information is stored.
Sak Magics

Very special magics, with which you offer your own life to save a comrade's. When you give your life with Sak, the comrade who receives it recovers and gains the ultimate life power. With Nasak, you sacrifice your own life so that all your comrades recover and gain ultimate life power.

Shiza Magic

Annihilates an enemy's central nervous system so he can't use his special attack skills.
Shu Magic

Shu increases the defensive power of one of your comrades.
Sashu increases the power of all comrades.
Tsu Magics

All three forms concentrate the light of the Algo Star System into a fatal laser beam. As the magics increase in strength, so does the devastating power of the laser.
Tsuis regular strength.
Githuis more effective than Tsu.
Nathuis the most powerful.
Zan Magics

Used in three forms: Zan, Gizan, and Nazan.
It works by creating a vacuum in the atmosphere which draws all air out of your enemy and finally crushes it.
Zanis standard strength.
Gizanhas a crushing effect that is so powerful, the enemy's body splits into fragments.
Nazanis power that completely destroys an enemy so nothing is left.
