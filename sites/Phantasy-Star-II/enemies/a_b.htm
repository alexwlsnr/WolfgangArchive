Enemies A-B


Welcome to the enemy galery of Phantasy Star. This section is devided into separate parts, displaying the enemies alphabetically. Sorry, but you will only find a picture, the name and the typical location where the enemy can be found. I did not make any statistics about HP, EXP or Mesetas.

Enemies (A)
Picture
 Name
 Location
Aerotank Floating Robot type on Dezo, inside and outside the dungeons.
Amoeba Never to be seen outside the dungeons. Seems to like dim light like in Shure dungeon.
Archdragon This dragon is the strongest enemy that you will encounter during your quest. His first appearance is inside Naval Dungeon, but you will meet him again at Noah.
Armored Ant Inside the Shure dungeon and around.
Army Eye This robot will attack you after you opened the last Dam on Mota. It only depends on which Dam you visited last. You can fight this robot but never will defeat it. It will be wiser to save your HP for the prison satellite Gaira and defend yourself.
Attmech One of the Blue Dam main Robots.This one seems not to be fast, you can escape him more often than other robots.
