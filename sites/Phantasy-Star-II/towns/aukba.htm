Aukba (Dezo)

Aubka, one of three cities on Dezo

This town is located in the northern part of Dezo. From here you can travel to the Crevice to reach the Esper Mansion.

After you received the Aero Prism as a donation by Lutz you will find all the locations you could'nt even see before.

Only now it is possible to reach the Menobe Dungeon west of Aukba.
