Oputa (Mota)

Oputa, the town of Ustvestia, the composer.

After crossing the North Bridge you finally arrive Oputa. Here you can stock up your supplies, buy new stuff, etc. Visit one special house in Oputa to learn something about Muzik, or just to listen to some songs.

Return to Paseo before you head towards the Biosystem Laboratory for meeting a new friend.
