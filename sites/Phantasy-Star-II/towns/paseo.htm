Paseo (Mota)

Paseo, capital of whole Mota

Start your adventure in the capital of Mota, Paseo. All sorts of shops are present here amongst other buildings you will not find anywhere else.

In the town centre you see the Central Tower where the major awaits you to give you new orders. The Library is located in this building, too. And you can visit the Luggage Room to store items not necessarily needed at the moment for free. Get them back whenever you want to use them. Shir will steal a tool here which will be sufficient for your success.

There is another building in Paseo, your Home. Meet new friends here and re-arrange your party if needed.
