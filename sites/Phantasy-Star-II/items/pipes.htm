Items


No Role Playing Game would be that fun if you can't buy new (and better) weapons, find special items, trade rare things, etc.

In this section you will not find detailed statistics about all items which can be found during the adventure but only a rough overview what is available. Most pictures are original scans of the manual which can be found in the first Phantasy Star Collection, published for the Sega Saturn.

Ocarina Pipes

Carrying Ocarina Pipes increases you powers. These items disappear after one use.

Escapipe

Use this to escape from a maze.		 Hidepipe

Constantly vibrates at an ultra-high pitch heard only by very weak creatures. As a result, they don´t hang around to get in your way.
Telepipe

When you´re deep in the wilderness, injured, and almost out of Hit Points (HP), the power of this pipe will return you to a city. Mazes confuse the Telepipe and make it powerless.
