Townspeople

 Hospital

He will heal and cure your party members. The prices for the service differ from town to town.

 Clone Lab

Inside the Clone Laboratories you will meet this guy. The fee for cloning one of the party members depends on his / hers level status.

 Save Point

She will tell you how much points each party member needs to gain the next level. After this information you can save the progress you made. If you use your Visiphone you will contact her to save where ever you like.

 Teleport

She awaits your inside the teleport buildings on Mota. From here you can teleport to any town on Mota you have visited before.

 Tool Shop

He owns all Motavian Tool shops. Buy items for transportation and healing here. Sell stuff not needed anymore in these shops. You only get half the price back, but that's better than dropping the items.

 Armor Shop

Here you can buy the armor and other equipment to protect you. Be careful when buying. Not all party members can use the same sort of equipment, and some armor is very expensive.

 Weapon Shop

This lady sells weapons of all kind. She will tell you if the one who will take the weapon can be equipped with it. Trust her, she is always right.

 Dezo Shops

These guys live on Dezo and can be found in the shops of all towns on the ice planet.
Only the Clone Lab clowns seem to have a service monopole. They reside in Dezo, too.
