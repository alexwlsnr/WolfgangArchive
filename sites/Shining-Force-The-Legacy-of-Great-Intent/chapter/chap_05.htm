In the second part of this section some more or less important events or tips are listed.


You will have to fight your way even on the sea. In your first battle the ship was damaged and so you have to land in the town of Waral, only to find more difficulties here.

A hidden shrine underneath the sea might be a shortcut, but in the very last moment the path to Runefaust will be blocked by a rockslide. So you are forced to cross over to Runefaust on sea again.
