In the chapter sections you will find the towns of each chapter with short descriptions which people you will meet, who will join your forces etc. I did not include a list of items that can be bought in shops because you will find out yourself, what's for sale. If something special can be found refer to the second part (see below).

In the second part of this section some more or less important events or tips are listed.


Before you meet Anri in Marinarina you visit the town of Rindo. Stock up your supplies, because you way to Marinarina may be hard due to the Runefaust army.

Once you reached the town of the wizard's school and met Anri, you have to get your hands on the Orb of Light. Back in Rindo you must seek the lost grandson of the village elder. If you are successful, he will donate his last ship to the Shining Force.

The last part of this chapter leads you to Shade Abbey.
