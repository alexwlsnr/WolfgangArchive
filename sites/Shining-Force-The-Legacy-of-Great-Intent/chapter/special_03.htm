Special Events Chapter 3

You will meet Kokichi in this room crowded with machine parts.



Open the top chest inside the cave after the first battle is over. You will find the Moonstone which is needed to brew the Lunar Dew for Zylo.

If you search the walls inside the cave you will find a second chest with the Sugoi Mizugi, which is a bikini swim suit for Tao. Put it in her inventory and have a special look at her during the next battle.



After an old guy of Bustoke finished his chemical experiments with the Moonstone, he will hand over the Lunar Dew to you. Visit Zylo and use the medicine on him.
Zylo will join the Shining Force as a mighty party member.



Looking for a new outfit for Anri? On the bridge next to the three Dark Elfs you will find a hidden chest. You have to search here to get the Kitui Huku dress.
