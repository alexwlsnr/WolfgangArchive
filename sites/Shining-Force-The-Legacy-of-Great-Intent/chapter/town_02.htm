Towns and Places of Chapter 2


  Rindo Town

People you should not miss:



Make sure to meet this 'steam' knight here. Due to the fact that his armor needs some more tests this armadillo like beast will join you later in the traveling town of Pao.


  Marinarina, the home of the wizards

People you should not miss:



Anri is the daughter of the Guardiana King. You will bring her really bad news. Although she has not finished her wizard training yet, she will do her very best to help the Shining Force.

This former knight called Arthur was tired of fighting but now will join your forces. Living in Marinarina for some time he was able to learn some spells, too.


  Shade Abbey

People you should not miss:



First Sky warrior you will meet. Joins after you escaped the trap set for you in Shade Abbey.

This Sky warrior was turned to stone by the evil Dark Sol. Once free he will help you alongside his 'fiancee' Amon.
