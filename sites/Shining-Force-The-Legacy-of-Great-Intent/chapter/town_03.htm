Towns and Places of Chapter 3


  Bustoke Town

People you should not miss:



Diane, a female elven sniper will help you to set all men of Bustoke free from slave labor.



The wolfman Zylo is locked in 'prison' due to a mysterious illness. So find the Moonstone inside the Bustoke mines. This ingrdience is needed to brew the Lunar Dew, a potion to cure Zylo's illness.



Meet Kokichi in Bustoke and see a more or less successful demonstration of his flying machine.
If you miss to speak to him, he will not join the Force in Pao some time later in the quest
