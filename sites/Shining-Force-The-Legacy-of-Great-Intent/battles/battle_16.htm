Battle 16


  The Hidden Shrine of Waral

Underneath Waral, the sea town, your party tries to reach Rune via the back door.

But there are two things which make your plan fail.

The first, a Master Mage, may be no problem. But the second, the collapse of the whole shrine will lock the door to Rune forever!

Enemies you meet:
Skeletons
Hell Hounds
Sea Bats
Worms
Master Mage

Master Mage is the main enemy in the Waral Shrine battle.
