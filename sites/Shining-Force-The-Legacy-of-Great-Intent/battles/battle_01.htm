Battle 1


  The Ancient Shrine

Once you got some money from the Guardiana King and assembled your first party,
you will be transported here via a horse cart.

If you want to have another fighter and healer, use Egress and come back here by foot.
Don't miss the hut next to the shrine. Gong, the monk, and Yoghurt are waiting here for you.

Gong will join your party at once, Yoghurt will become a Shining Force member later.

Enemies you meet:
Goblins
Dark Dwarfs
Rune Knight

The Rune Knight is the main enemy here. Beat him and the fight is over.
