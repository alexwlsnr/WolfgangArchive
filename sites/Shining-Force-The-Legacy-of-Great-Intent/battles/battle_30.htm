Battle 30


  Dark Dragon and the final'countdown'

At last. Dark Dragon awaits his doom. He was revived by the sacrifice
of Dark Sol, but the Shining Force will take care that he won't last long.

Enemies you meet:
Armed Skeletons
Dark Dragon Heads

Little Tip:

If you don't occupy the holes located on both sides of Dark Dragon,
for each defeated Armed Skeleton a new one will appear.
