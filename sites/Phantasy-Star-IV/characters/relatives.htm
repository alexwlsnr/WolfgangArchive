Relatives and friends

Not essential necessary for the plot, but cute, here are two people worth looking
at. Both are somehow related to two of the heroes.


This little Motavian native is Pana, the sister of Gryz. Both barely escaped the Molcum desaster. Unlike her parents Pana and her brother managed to escape the fire which burned whole Molcum.



This is one of the rare pictures of Saya, the fiancee of Hahn, stolen from his
pocket. She looks after Alys, after our heroine has become seriously ill.


Saya's profession is that of a teacher in the local school of Krup. All children of Krup love her and miss their teacher while she tries to cure Alys.
