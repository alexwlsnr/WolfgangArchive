Enemies G-J


Unlike in Phantasy Star I, where only a single ememy type could confront you, but similar to Phantasy Star II, in Phantasy Star IV you often will run into different kind of monsters at one time. The ammount of experience points and mesetas depends on the number of party members, the less members the group has, the more money and experience points you will gain.

On the other hand no information is given about the strength of the enemy (no hitpoints are displayed). So it is too difficult to create an exact list about the monsters, robots, and main enemies. The enemies are ordered alphabetically with a short description and a note where it normally will appear.
