Maybe, that´s the reason that his father does not present new equipment for the party members as a gift. You have to pay like a normal customer.

But not all in the beautiful town near the ancient soldier temple treat Hahn like this. His fiance is living here and works in the local school as a teacher. Visit her, so that Hahn will receive his present for returning to her. Ok, we are not materialistic, but some money would have been more helpful.
