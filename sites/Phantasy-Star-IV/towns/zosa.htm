Zosa (Dezoris)

  Zosa, Town of the Dezorian Penguin

This town must not necessarily visited during the quest. Visit the weapon shop for some weapons but you will find the stuff in the town of Meese, too.

If you want to have some fun hire a penguin as a comrad of a day. It costs 500 mesetas to see a pengu running after your party. If you leave the town or enter a building the pengu will return to its owner.

Beware, do NOT buy the Pengu food in the tool store. It won´t last for long.
