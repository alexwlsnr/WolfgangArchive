This little town is more worth than just walking through and buying items. More than in any other town it is necessary to speak to the people, especially with the barkeeper named Gyuna, who is known as an encyclopedia on feet all over Dezoris like Grandfather Dorin in Tonoe.

If you don't listen to him carefully you will never reveal the secret of the hidden spaceship underneath Tyler. Buy armory, if you can afford them (Dezoris seems to be the favorite resting place for Algol millionaires).
