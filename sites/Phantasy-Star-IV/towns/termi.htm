This statue indicates that Alis has claimed her birthright 2000 years ago and became queen of the Algol Solar System.

Finally you reach the town of Termi, the last resting point before conquering the Ladea Tower. But you should walk all over the town first. Here you will find a statue of Alis and Myau, two of the heroes of ancient times, who first encountered the evil Dark Force 2.000 years ago.



Besides, in the gift shop you can find two items which come in handy later in two of the hunters guild jobs. Look at the pictures for some clues. Psy-Armory is sold only in Termi and you should buy them for a better protection inside the Ladea Tower.
