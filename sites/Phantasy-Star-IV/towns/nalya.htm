Nalya, a small town north of Zema, can be reached only after the Zema citizens are cured from Zio's evil spell.

They then will repair the broken bridge which blocked your way to the north. A wreckage of what supposed the be a former spaceship lies in the crater north of Nalya.

This crash caused some destruction all over the town. The people hardly survived the disaster and so not much can be bought here.
