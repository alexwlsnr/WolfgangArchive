PHACIA
Althena´s Alternate?

Phacia is the head priestress of Althena´s Shrine, located at the foot of the Goddess Tower.
Much like Althena, she has a friendly demeamor and a hypnotic beauty that dazzles anyone in her presence. In fact, many of the Shrine´s inhabitants are under the impression that Phacia is the physical representation of Althena herself.

Could it be true?
