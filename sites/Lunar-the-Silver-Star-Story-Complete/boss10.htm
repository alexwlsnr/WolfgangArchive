 Ok, you will argue, that the Black Dragon has three different attack forms. But I was unable to catch all of them. The picture shows the attack called Fire Breath. This usually means a huge loss of HP for one in your party. If you see white clouds coming from the dragons mouth its time for Cold Breath, causing medium damage to all.
The electric trick of good old Blacky, called Thunder Breath will cause a moderate loss of hitpoints for all. Finally you feel what your enemies suffer when Nash uses his Thunder spells.
