 This is the worst attack you ever encountered until now. A ´Hot Dog´ will rush over the battlefield hitting all party members hard.
A Bronze Dog flapping his like this will breath fire in the next round. Normally he concentrates on one of your friends then.
Allthough only two attack forms are mentioned in the Official Strategy Guide by Working Design, there are three attacks in total. Here is the last one. A Bronze Dog (not glowing or flapping) will cross the battlefield and bites one or two of your friends. Weakest attack form.
