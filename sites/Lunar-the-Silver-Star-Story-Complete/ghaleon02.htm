GHALEON
Evil Enemy

Who could ever imagine that, in fact, Ghaleon is behind all the evil that spread over Lunar?

Because he never understood the decision of the Goddess Althena to become a human, he formed the plan to become the new God of Lunar.

It took him 16 years and now all power is in the grasp of his hands.

Will Alex and his friends be able to beat him?
