HEROES SECTION
MIA
Distinguished Daughter

Mia is the daughter of Lemia, one for the Four Heroes, and the leader of Vane. Lemia has protected Mia from the outside world for so long that she is oblivious to the joys and hardships found in the world below.
Despite her mild nature, she is actually a very powerful magician.
If she could learn one new magic trick, it would be making Nash disappear.

(Text taken from the Official Strategy Guide
