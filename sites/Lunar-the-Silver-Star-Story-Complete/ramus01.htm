HEROES SECTION
RAMUS
Freaky Friend

Ramus is Alex´s best friend. Not that he has much choices in the little village of Burg.

But nonetheless Ramus is a guy you can rely on if you want to get in trouble.

In fact, Ramus´ search for adventure and money starts a quest in which the whole world of Lunar must be saved from doom.

And the initiator of all this will quit your party after you visited only three towns!
