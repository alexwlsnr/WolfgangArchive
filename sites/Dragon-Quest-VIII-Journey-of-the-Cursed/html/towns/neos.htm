Holy Town of Neos
Neos is a holy town due to a giant statue of the goddess which is rumoured to be carved by the Goddess herself. The innkeeper tries to make his own profit out of the town's publicity, the prices for the service are really high.

A curious fact about the holy city is that there is no church with a prior where you can confess (save)!!
