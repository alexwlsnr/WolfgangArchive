    Town of Farebury
    This is the first town you will visit during your long quest. You will meet the fortuneteller Kalderasha here, whose own future lies in your hands.

    Use the town to rest (and confess) often, because you are a two man party and your experience is low when you start the adventure.

    You have to return here much later to find a very important item.
