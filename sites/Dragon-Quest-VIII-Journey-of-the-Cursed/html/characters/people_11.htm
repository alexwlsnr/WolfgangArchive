People you will meet
These pages are dedicated to some of the many people you will meet during your quest. Some of the are nice, some are evil, and some are..., decide yourself.

 You will meet Chen Mui inside the Dragovian Sanctuary. Although you have never met him before, he seems to know very much about your entire party.
Fishy, fishy, I must say!
The Lord of the Dragovian has made a terrible mistake that may have resulted in the annihilation of his race. Luckily he can depend on our friends who will help to sort things out by hitting him (hard on the head).
Wonder why this guy is to be found back here even if you may have met him at the beginning of your quest? There is a certain reason behind this, which you will find out once you have completed the Dragovian Trial at least once.
