People you will meet
These pages are dedicated to some of the many people you will meet during your quest. Some of the are nice, some are evil, and some are..., decide yourself.

 David is the servant of the great Magician Dominico. That's what all say who know him. But is that all what is interesting about him?
Dominico is a great magician who resides in the towen of Arcadia. His magic powers seem to have turned him into an arrogant man. He demands two things you have to get for him. But in the end (and too late) he will recognize how much he has failed.
Builder and architect Rydon is said to know the whereabouts of the Kran Spinels. If you want to meet him (and you want to), you have to visit him on the very top of his last project called (obviously) Rydon's Tower.
