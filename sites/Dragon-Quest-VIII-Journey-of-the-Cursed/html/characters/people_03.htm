People you will meet
These pages are dedicated to some of the many people you will meet during your quest. Some of the are nice, some are evil, and some are..., decide yourself.

 You will meet Alistair, the brother of Jessica, not in person but inside a story told by a statue and in a dream of Jaessica herself.
Marcello is Angelo's half-brother and due to this, he is not very fond of his younger brother. Does not sound very logical, but you will find out the reason behind.
Abbot Francisco of Maella Abbey is not only a wise man but very funny, too. Search his books for some laughter.
