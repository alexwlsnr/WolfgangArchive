Dragon Quest Music Page


First of all, I am no composer. The two files you will find here are merely those that did not make my ears bleed after I converted them from WAV to MIDI with the help of a freeware wav2midi converter. Luckily you can choose to surf the site without hearing one of the two files listed below!

The chosen music will play as long as you are surfing the site.
If you want to hear a different theme, come back here and simply choose another file.

To stop the music, please click on   STOP MUSIC.

BTW, if you are aware of more files in the MIDI file format that should be published here,
