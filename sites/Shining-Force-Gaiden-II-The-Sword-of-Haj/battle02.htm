BATTLE 2
´Blocked by the Enemy´
Short Description
The thief was not alone. His comrades block your way. Try to be quick when reaching the first bridge. You should cross over because of the bats. They even can put one or more of your fighters to sleep.

Enemies:
3 Goblins
2 Scabens
2 Dragonewts
2 Big Bats
1 Dwarf
