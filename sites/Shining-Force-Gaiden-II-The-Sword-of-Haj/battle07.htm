BATTLE 7
´The Fake King´
Short Description
The Sword of Hajya, finally. But the king who carries it is a fake. It´s Gordon, one of the Iom generals. He casts a spell causing an earthquake and jumps through a hole in the floor to safety.
His minions try to prevent you from following him.
