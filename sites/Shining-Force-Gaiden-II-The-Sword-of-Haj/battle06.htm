BATTLE 6
´An Army in Disguise´
Short Description
After the Dark Mage gave the Sword of Hajya to a Gargoyle, Graham shuts the beast right out of the sky. But the sword can not be found where the Gargoyle must have landed.

The Shining Force decides to ask in the nearby castle. But the enemy was waiting in uniforms of the castle troops.

Chester, an archer, notices the disguise, but can´t help you because of the closed castle gates and the drawbridge up.

Enemies:
4 Ratmen 2 Death Archers
2 Incubus 2 Ratwings
2 Dark Mages 1 Dark Soldier
