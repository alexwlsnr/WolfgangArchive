BATTLE 12
´Fight around the Fissures´
Short Description
Back on the safe ground our rest troop faces the next enemies.
The field, the evil forces chose for the confrontation is scattered with fissures making the ground very weak. There is a nasty Sorcerer waiting. After you finally managed to eliminate him, the ground under your feet collapses and you fell into one of the pits.
