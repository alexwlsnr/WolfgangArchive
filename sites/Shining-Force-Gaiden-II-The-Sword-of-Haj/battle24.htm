BATTLE 24
´Iom, at last!!´
Short Description
Iom has resurrected and it depends on you to send him back to where he came from. There are four tips for the final battle I can give you:
  • Place two members of your crew where the Iom Dolls stood. Otherwise a new Doll will appear for every killed one.
  • Finish off the Iom Worm tentacles from the distance with Graham and Chester. They then neither can attack you directly nor reach your party with their Demon Breath.
  • No attack on Iom will work before Nick (equipped with his Sword of Hajya) does the first strike. So, at last, this peace of steal is useful after all! After Nick has hit Iom use the best attacks and skills on him.
  • Don´t fear to stay crowded this time. The Demon Breath has lesser effect to more people that it has on a single member.

Good luck. Hope you saved the Healing Rains or Sarah knows about AURA Level 4!!

Enemies:
2 Iom Dolls 2 Iom Worms 1 Evil Bishop and Iom, of course!
