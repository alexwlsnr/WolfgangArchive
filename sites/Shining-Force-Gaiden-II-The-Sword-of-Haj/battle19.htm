BATTLE 19
´Forward to the Iom Shrine´
Short Description
You are near the end of your quest. The Iom Shrine comes in sight. But until the very end there is a bit more to do. First of all you have to reach the shrine.

Be careful. There are some Belials around here. They can and will cast BOLT spells. So try to avoid standing together too close. Again a Sorcerer will try to transform your party to ice cream.

The Minotaur is the main enemy in the battle. But he should be no problem.

Oh, I nearly forgot. There are 4 Golems hidden in the hills!

Enemies:
4 Ghouls 2 Belials 1 Sorcerer
4 Golems 2 Gargoyles 1 Minotaur
2 Cerberos 2 Evil Clerics
