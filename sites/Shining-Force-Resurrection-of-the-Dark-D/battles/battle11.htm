Tip:  This battle is a bit tricky and needs some preparation. Before you enter the battlefield, buy some healing stuff for all party members.
If you want both extras (Laser Eye and Silver Knight Card) your slow mages have to finish of these enemies. So you cannot send faster force members to destroy the Laser Eye for a safer passage!

By the way, I hope you have not promoted any of your friends until now. Due to the fact that they are weaker shortly after their promotion it can be nearly impossible to get both cards and the bonus in one fight. Promotion can wait until you reach Pao.
