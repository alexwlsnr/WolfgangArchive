The "Home of Shining Force" site welcomes you!

Since the day I was aware of the relaunch of "Shining Force" for Nintendo's Gameboy Advanced I was planning to build a section for this game, too. Now you can see the result of about two months work on these pages.

As you will know from the other sections, you will find more statistics about the site in the News section.

All those who played the original game on the Sega Megadrive/Genesis back in 1991 will soon be familiar with to game even on the GBA. On the other hand, the storyline was changed slightly, so three new characters will take part in the relaunch. In addition to this an all new card system can be found, too. For details visit the different sections within these "Shining Force Resurrection of the Dark Dragon" pages. So, surf and enjoy!
