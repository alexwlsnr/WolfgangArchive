Shining Force Enemies

The world of the Shining Force is inhabited by a mass of ugly creatures, mighty mages, fierce fighters, and technological nightmares which all try to prevent Max and friends from reaching their final goal: restoring peace again.

The main intention of this section is to give you a brief overview about every enemy, their abilities, strength, possessions, etc.

You will find them in alphabetical order, not in order of their appearance. If you need to know in which battle an enemy encounters your party, refer to the Battles Section on these pages. Most Boss enemies are to be found in the Casts Section.
