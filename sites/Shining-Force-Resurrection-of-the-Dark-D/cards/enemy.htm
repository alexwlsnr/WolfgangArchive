Enemy Cards

Gaining an Enemy Card sounds simple: kill the "boss enemy" with a special party member and you will
receive its card. But during the quest you may easily miss getting all enemy cards
due to an unexpected super attack of another party member or an unlucky
defeat of the appropriate character who is expected to make the
final blow. You then have to use the chance to get
the missing card when replaying the game!

So a bit tactic is needed to get all cards within one quest.
This section might help you by telling which Shining Force
member is expected to finish off the enemy in order
to get his / her card.

Pressing the buttons below will get you to a page with more
information about the character's card.
