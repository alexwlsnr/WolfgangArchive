Hidden Cards

To get these cards you have to search various places, chests, signs, etc.

Some locations (chests for example) are very easy to spot, some cards are hidden inside books
which makes them easy to get but some cards are hidden in places which
don't offer a clue what is behind the surface. To make sure that you don't
miss a single card of the game you may use the screenshots inside
this section to refer to the hiding locations of each card.

Pressing the buttons below will get you to a page with more
information about the character's card.
