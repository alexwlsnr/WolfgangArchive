How to use the cards  (for further information about the cards refer to the card section)
Icon Effect

Description

  Copy

Copies the special ability of the displayed character
on Mawlock himself for the duration of the entire battle

  Effect

Choose an ally or enemy to cast the effect of the card
onto him / her

  Imitate

Summons a "clone" of the character displayed on the card

  Move

Grants one extra move for the card's character if the
character is a member of the current party formation
