Shining Force Magic

The world of the Shining Force is truely a magical one. Various spells can be used not only by your friends but by many of your enemies, too.

The only way to attack more than one foe at the same time is
to cast fierce elemental magic like Blast, Bolt and Freeze.

You need magic to heal or depoison your friends
with Heal or Detox if you don't have healing items ready.

Last but not least you can use different techniques
to boost up the abilities of your party and to
decrease those of your enemies.
Examples for these magical spells can be found
in the sections listed below named "Attack Magic",
"Healing Magic", "Support Magic"and "Supress Magic".

Press the appropriate button below for more information.
