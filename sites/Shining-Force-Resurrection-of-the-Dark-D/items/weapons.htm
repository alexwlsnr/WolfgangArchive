Shining Force Weapons

Depending on the characters class he / she can be equipped
with different weapons. The list below shows a rough overview
about who and what can be equipped:
  • Swords: Swordsmen, Birdmen, Ninja, Samurai
  • Axes:      Warriors
  • Spears:  Knights
  • Lances:  Knights
  • Staffs:     Healers, Mages
  • Rods:      Healers, Mages
  • Arrows:  Archers
  • Maces:   Shaman
Some weapons are equipable only after a character was promoted to a higher class!
More details about the different weapons can be found by pressing the buttons below.
