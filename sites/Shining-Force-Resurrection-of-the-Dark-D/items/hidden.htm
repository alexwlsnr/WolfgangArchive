Hidden Items

The most wanted items in the Shining Force adventures are the three alternative outfits for Anri, Khris, Narsha and Tao.
Here you will find screenshots of the locations where two of the dresses (Maid Outfit and Darling Dress) can be found.

About the Teeny Bikini: I cannot offer you a real screenshot here. You will get the dress after you have beaten the game once and ask any person of your choice who in the previous game has given you his / her card. Instead of the card (which is in your inventory already) you will get the Teeny Bikini! In the example below it is the girl which gave Max the Gong Card!

The last two screenshots show the location of the Domingo Egg and to whom you must talk in Pao to get the Blazing Bomb which will come in handy in your attempt to conquer Urbanatol.
