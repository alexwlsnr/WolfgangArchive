Shining Force Royalties

The King of Guardiana is the father of Anri who studies magic in Manarina. He is one of the first victims of the Runefaust invasion being slaughtered by the evil Kane.

The King of Alterone was tricked by Kane during the invasion of the Runefaust army. In order to save his people, the king even arrests Max and his friends. Will he find out the truth in time?

Queen Koron is still young but carries a lot of responsibility on her small shoulders. She leads the traveling town of Pao through the Pao plains. Does she have some information for Max?

Shell of Waral is a mermaid who helps Max to reach the island of Waral after a fierce sea battle. In Waral Max will meet many of her relatives and gets a mass of useful information.

The King of Waral could be called a shame of his class (honestly!). Besides his royal duties he seems to have much spare time and has opened two shops in Waral to relax as a shopkeeper!

King Kusko of Prompt seems to know much about the past of Max and Kane. He, too, is worried about the Runefaust's plans to conquer the world and sends Max to save Alef and Torasu.
