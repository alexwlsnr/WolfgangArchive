Paseo is the capital city of Motavia and the home of the gouvernour. No audience is given until you offer a sweet present. Maybe, the SHORTCAKE can help. The gouvernour will give you a letter which you have to show Noah. Without this letter he will not join the quest.

In the house near the palace you can heal all party members free of charge. So spare your money and do not visit the hospital. Poor nurse.

At the beginning of your journey you will meet Myau in the shop on the right hand side. Do not try to gain all the 1,000,000,000 mesetas in battle because your electronical purse only can store 65535 bucks. Trade the LACONIAN POT instead.
