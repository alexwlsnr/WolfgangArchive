Items


No Role Playing Game would be that fun if you can't buy new (and better) weapons, find special items, trade rare things, etc.

Phantasy Star was one of the first games ever on a video console with these features. So it is obvious, that you will find a complete list of all weapons, armory and other items (in alphabetical order) on this site.
Oh, the pictures of the items are original scans from the game manual published back in 1988!
