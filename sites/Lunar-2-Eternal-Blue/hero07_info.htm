Heroes Ruby

Ruby has been Hiro´s constant companion for as long as anybody can remember. She claims to be the offspring of a dragon, but Hiro doesn´t quite believe her.

Like another little dragon, she has the bad habit of speaking her mind at the wrong time.

Sooner or later, this little wisecracker´s bound to get Hiro into a lot of trouble...
