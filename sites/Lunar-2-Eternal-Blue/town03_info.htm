Towns Dalton

Even if you rush to Dalton in no time, you will be too late to save Lucia from making a big mistake. Mega big trouble (as Lemina would say), Lucia is caught by Leo on board of the Dragonship Destiny. Now you have to free her, which obviously would be no easy task. So buy better equipment in the General Store before boarding the Destiny.

Sad to say but some hooligans broke Althena's statue at the town entrance. Use the statue inside Althena's shrine and you have to pay dearly. Take my advice, save your bucks!
On the first deck of the Destiny you will find a statue with "free service".

By the way, don't miss the Angel Ring () hidden in Dalton. Don't equip it, save it for the final battle. Ruby will take good care of the ring until (much, much) later.
