Sidecasts Ramus

Since the long forgotten times of Dragonmaster Alex the family of ancient Ramus kept up a tradition: every boy born in the family is called Ramus after his famous ancestor.

So this Ramus is not the one you know from Lunar the Silver Star although he looks like and also behaves like Ramus the First.

Lemina knows him very well because of his marvelous ability earning money. Nonetheless she tried to learn this special magic but failed. There can only be one (financial genius)!
