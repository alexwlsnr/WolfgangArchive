Omni Zophar's new Outfit
As long as the shields are up and Lucia is caught by Omni Zophar, you can't do anything but heal yourself from the last fight with Zophar. Try to strengthen your party with some spells cast by Lemina (Power Drive, Cryo Shield).

Omni Zophar will cast all of Lucia's spells like Lightning Bomb or Plasma Shock.

Once Lucia wriggles free from Zophars hairs your HP and MP will be refilled and the battle finally can start.
Although not shown in picture the attacks will be named here:
  • Hell Wave
  • Cosmic Burn
  • Metal Shot
  • Ultra Ray

The Hell Wave will inflict instant death to everyone in range, so try to get out of its range under all circumstances!!

Near the end
Onmi Zophar does not know when to stop. Even now he tries to win. But a few Poe Sord attacks by Hiro, the last in combination with some aid of Lucia, will finish him off, finally!
