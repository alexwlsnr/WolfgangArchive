Towns Azado

Once being a priest to the Althena cult, ronfar knows of a transporter hidden inside the tower of Azado. So your first goal in Azado will be the famous tower. But unfortunatelly the bridge leading there is closed. Search the gatekeeper and remind him of his duties. As I always say, speak to everybody in the towns, it's important for every quest.


Once the gatekeeper is found you can enter the tower. Some nasty foes might await you, including two very strong ones, so it would be wise to stock up your supplies and buy the best equipment available in town.
