Your first intention was right, the guy in the right corner of the inn is Ronfar. After gambling with him for a while, Lucia faints and Ronfar takes her to his house in the left part of Larpa. You should follow him there.

After mastering a tricky situation (Leo nearly stumbles into your party), Ronfar decides to help you curing Lucia from Zophar's curse.

Visit the shops before entering the Larpa Cave, and don't forget to get your hands on the first Star Light () of the game in the chest on the cliff.
