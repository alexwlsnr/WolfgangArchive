Heroes Jean

Jean is a fan dancer in the Carnival and is highly valued for her talents. She is a tomboy with a big-sister complex, who tends to be a bit overbearing at times. She tends not to develop strong relationships with people.

However, Jean is an extremely talented fighter. Those in the Carnival say she was trained to fight as a child. Unfortunately, her past is going to catch up with her, and is may not be a pretty sight.
