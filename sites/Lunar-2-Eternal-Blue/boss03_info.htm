Tips

Being Hit
Even if this guy looks nice, don't underestimate his abilities. His attacks can be tough, be careful not to enter his room unprepared.

If your group arrives here when being exhausted from previous fights you should return to Takkar, use Althena's statue and return here.

Drum Attack
If the eyes of the Tester will roll aound like crazy, he will walk over to one unlucky party member and plays his drum over his / her head. This might change the victim's status to daze.

Jumping Jack Flash
Tester LX1 is equipped with some rocket which will be used after he lost his drum (and arms, of course). He flies high to the ceiling and lands on one of your heroes. Splash!

Trouble Bubble
Again, a new trick of the Magical Tester. Using Trouble Bubble may cause a "sleepy character" amongst your fighters.

Won't he never give up?
Even without arms and feet LX1 continues fighting. Here you see the "footless" version of his jump attack.

Trouble Bubble (II)
At last, the abilities of our clown seem to be limited. Nothing new from the front line. It's Trouble Bubble again.

Finished off
Every fight will have its end. After the Magical Tester is beaten his head pops off and he somehow looks cross eyed. Prepare yourself for a "nice surprise" after you ruined the LX1.
