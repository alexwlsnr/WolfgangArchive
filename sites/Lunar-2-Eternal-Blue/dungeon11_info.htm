Dungeons Ancient Ruins

Ok, this dungeon in fact may drive you crazy! So, here is a shortcut through those "damn" mirrors:
  • You start at the stairs marked by the (x).
  • Walk through mirror 1 to leave through mirror2
  • Use mirror 3, exit at mirror 4
  • Mirror 5 will take you to mirror 6
  • Use the same mirror 6 to be warped to mirror 7
  • Now go through mirror 8 and you see your party coming out of mirror 9
  • Walk to mirror 10 at the top and exit at mirror 11
  • Take mirror 12, finally leave mirror 13 to reach the stairs down (o)
