Rods / Staffs


No RPG with an assortment of various weapons, healing items, rings or other stuff. Equipping better weapons will help to raise attack power, special weapons even let non magic folks cast mighty spell, rings, for example, offer useful abilities otherwise cannot be gained.

Most items can be bought in shops or, even better, found in various locations for free. Within this section you will find all items of the Shining Force adventure listed in different categories.Some are useful, but some are cursed. So be careful in what you will use!
