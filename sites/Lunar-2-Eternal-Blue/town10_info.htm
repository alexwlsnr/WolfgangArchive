Towns Vane

The skyhigh fall thousand years ago caused much damage in Vane and obviously the former glorious town has not recovered from the desaster. Nonetheless it still is the home of the Magic Guild. Miria, the guild leader and Leminas mother, will be kidnapped by the Black Magician Borgan right before Leminas eyes. Shocking!


As lemina realizes what "really makes the world spin" (it is not money, you know) she offers all her treasures to the party. The chests you found in Vane can now be opened! Oh, and don't forget those in the Cave of Trial!
