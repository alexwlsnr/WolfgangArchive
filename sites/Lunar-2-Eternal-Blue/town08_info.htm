Towns Zulan

Although Zulan never had a tropical climate this years ice period lasts too long. Many townspeople are severly ill, amongst them the grandchild of the town's chief. Rumors speak of an ice monster which is held responsible for the climate changes.

Being nice in the first place Hiro and his friends decide to help the people getting rid of the beast.

But Lucia does not agree and leaves the party for her meeting with Althena in Pentagulia. Time passes and Zophar gains more strength every minute. He must be stopped without any delay.
