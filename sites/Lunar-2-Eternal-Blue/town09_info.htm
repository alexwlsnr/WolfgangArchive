Towns Meribia

Remember Meribia from Alex' adventures thousand years ago? It has'nt changed much, indeed. With the exception of the missing Black Rose Street in the western outskirts of the town, everything looks so very familiar. Once Mel ruled over Meribia as a famous member of the four heroes, and in his castle a new gouvernor has settled: Lunn. Visit him and you get your next job, explore Tabens Peak and chase some bandits.

But first explore the town, go shopping and visit a god friend of Lemina who is well know as Ramus. Yes, you read right, Ramus. He is the grand grand grand ...... grand son of the first Ramus, best friend of the last Dragonmaster Alex.
Remember Ramus' shop well, you have to return here later.
