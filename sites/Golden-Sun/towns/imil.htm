The Village of Imil


You have to pass Bilibin Cave northwest of Bilbin to find Imil. Winter has fallen in this northernmost part of the world and the people suffer from the cold, harsh weather.
In Imil you will see Mia for the first time. If you help her she will join to help you as well.
Last but not least, try to find the hidden Mars Djinn Fever.
