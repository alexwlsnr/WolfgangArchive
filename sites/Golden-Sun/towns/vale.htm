The Village of Vale


Vale is the hometown of Isaac, Garet and Jenna. It was here where the adventure starts and hopefully the heroes will return to their childhood's harbour of safety. The map of the village is astonishingly huge, so it is devided into three sections displayed separately. Furthermore you will find a link to special puzzles which must be solved (this link can be found in every town, of course). I tried to show the village maps in their original size, but in case of Vale I had to shrink the map to 50% of its size!
