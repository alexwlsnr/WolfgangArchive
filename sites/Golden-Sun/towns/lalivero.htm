The Town of Lalivero


After you passed Suhalla Desert, crossed over the Sullahan Pass, visited the Venus Lighthouse, you eventually will find the town of Lalivero. Saturos and Menardi brought mischief over the whole town. You don't have to waste time, you must follow them. Still it would be wise to search the town and speak to their people! Even the way to a Mars Djinn called Torch must be found.

Babi has a big gadget hidden in this town, too, but you won't see it unless you have beaten Saturos and Menardi (or on this page, perhaps)
