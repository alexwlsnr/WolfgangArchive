The Town of Bilibin


Once you passed Goma Cave you soon will see the town of Bilibin. The first harbingers of an evil curse lying over this part of the world can be seen at the town entrance. Try to convince Master McCoy to give you the order to investigate what's behind all this! You will meet him in his Palace in the northern part of the town.

Even a Jupiter Djinn named Gust is hidden in this town.
