The Village of Suhalla


In the southern part of the continent you will pass the village of Suhalla. It lies near the Sullahan desert, a vast and very dry region. Grab what you can find, stock up your supplies and follow what seems to be Saturos, Menardi and their hostages Jennna and Kraden into the unfriendly desert.
