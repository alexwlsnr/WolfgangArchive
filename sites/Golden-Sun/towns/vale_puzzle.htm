Puzzles and Secrets in Vale


Golden Sun is one of those adventures were it might be good to return to towns you visited before after you found a new member or one or more in your party gained new abilities. Don't expect to get everything shown here at the start of the quest, simply because only Ivan but not Isaac and Garet can perform the "Whirlwind" spell, for example! For more details about the Vale Cave refer to the Map section.
