The Village of Kolima


East of the Bilibin Barrier you will find the lumberjack village Kolima. Cutting too much wood in Kolima Forest turned the former friendly Tret upset with the humans. He cursed the whole Kolima Forest so that no human being can enter it again because being turned into a tree prevents from walking around too much! But then something went terribly wrong. Tret got ill and the curse went out of control. Can Isaac and his friends heal Tret to lift the curse?

Back to Kolima Village. after entering the village for the first time, the curse even "reaches for you", but your party will be saved by Psynergy. After all is over, feel free to explorer the village. You can't buy new supplies because trees don't sell or buy. Some nice items can be found, though, and you will see the Venus Djinn Granite. Refer to the Kolima puzzle section for details how to reach him.
