Puzzles and Secrets in Kalay


The amount of tasks in Kalay isn't that much. Your first aim should be to find the hidden entrance to the Kalay sewers, where Scorch is waiting for you.

The next thing to do is to wait for a group of people gathering to leave to Tolbi by boat. As long as the party has'nt left the town, the boat won't set sails. If this scene does not take place, you must have missed to speak to the tour guides somewhere in Kalay!
