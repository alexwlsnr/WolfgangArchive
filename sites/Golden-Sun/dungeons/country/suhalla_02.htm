Suhalla Gate


 Map of Suhalla Gate and Pass (1:2)


Saturos and Menardi must be near. Wounded soldiers are to be seen at Suhalla Gate.

If needed stop at the house on top of Suhalla Gate to visit the priest for some anti cursing, depoisoning, or similar stuff.

Then pass over the Suhalla Moutain, take the right chutes for a MINT and to meet the Mercury Djinn DEW.

If you are interested to investigate the cave in the moutains you are invited. But you cannot do something important here, alas you need the Psynergy Stone hidden inside. The boat found on the peninsula at the caves exit will not be useful to you, unless you don't know how it works.

So don't bother about that, you eventually will find a way sooner or later.
