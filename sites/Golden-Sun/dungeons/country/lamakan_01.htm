Lamakan Desert (Part II)


You will enter this part of the desert from the southeast and have to find your way to the west. But you should search this part a bit closer. There is a Djinni called SMOG hidden in one of the stone circles. Besides 777 gold coins (middle chest) you will eventually find a Potion to the south east and a Lucky Pepper in the northwest region, too.
