Sol Sanctum (Part I)


 Sol Sanctum Entrance



This is the first dungeon you have to explore in the long quest. Your party will not come in tricky situations which they can't handle, though.

If you fell that your party is not strong enough to venture forth, it could be wise to walk near the entrance, fight some foes, earn some money, and buy some better equipment in the village.

The puzzles are not that tricky but I took some screenshot nonetheless.
