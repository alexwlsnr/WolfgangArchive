Mercury Lighthouse (Part IV)

Details can be found in the Puzzle section of Mercury Lighthouse. Once the statue has fallen, leave the floor and continue on floor 2.

The next ime they enter this floor Isaac and his part are nearer on the lighthouse roof. Allthough the first part of this floor has no real challanging puzzles you still might get stuck here.
