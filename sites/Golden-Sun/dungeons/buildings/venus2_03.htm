Venus Lighthouse 2 (Part IV)

Hurray, you are near the top. Do you see the floating platform in the top middle part of the floor? That's your goal. You will fall on it when sliding down the chute which can be fought in the room displayed on the map directly right from the platform. But be careful, use the chute only if your party members are healed on mind and body up to the max. So it's nice to find a Psynergy Stone here! The final confration lies strait ahead.

Just one more tip. Save your progress on two different slots, once the fight is over and you were awarded with the epilogue, the game cannot be continued any more!
