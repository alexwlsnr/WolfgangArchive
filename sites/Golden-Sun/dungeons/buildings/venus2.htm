Venus Lighthouse 2 (Part I)


Using the backdoor can be very useful sometimes. Venus Lighthouse is an example for this statement. After passing the Tunnel Ruins you arrive in a room just past that room which was blocked by an energy barrier when you entered Venus Lighthouse from the front door. If you need refreshment, heal up your party and use the Psynergy Stone lying in this room.

Reaching the top of the tower is very strait forward, not much psynergy spell are needed to conquer the building. But you have to hit switches , move sandy barriers and use some kind of electric tricks (do you remember Catweezle?).
Enough smalltalk, let's move!

Just one more thing, again the circles show how it should look like while the main map shows the original rooms. And if you need details refer to the puzzles page at the end of this section.
