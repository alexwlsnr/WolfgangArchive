Tunnel Ruins (Part I)

Depending on the lights lit on the flooor of the statues room at the entrance of the ruins your way will be a bit different but at the end the exit will be the same.

If you want to get your hand to the items in both chests you have to return to the statues room of Venus Lighthouse to speak to the statue. The lights on the ground will switch to the other constallation. Now go back to the Tunnel Ruins and explore the second passage.

Once exiting the ruins you will notice that you are back in Venus Lighthouse, but this time you are standing behind the energy barrier which blocked you off the first time you entered this room.

Careful, if you use the switch to turn off the barrier (MOVE the statue over the switch) and exit the room through the doorway, the energy barrier will 'come back to life' and you are forced to pass through the Tunnel Ruins once again!

Again, the main maps shows the rooms as they are when you enter them. The circled aereas in the map will give you a clue showing how special details have to look alike for 'beating' the rooms puzzles!
