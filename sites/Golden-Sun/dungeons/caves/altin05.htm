Altin Cave (Part VI)


Do you miss the sunlight? Ok, only some minutes to go and you can use RETREAT to go back to the surface.

Use FORCE on the wooden pillar with the huge bolder on top and you will cause a boulder avalanche. Run for your life! The massive boulder will crash trough the floor to reveal a new passage to an forgotten underground level. use the chute and climb down to the bottom of the newly formed pit. At the end you have to face Hydros Statue.

As a present for your victory you finally get the LIFTING GEM.
