Bilibin Cave (Part II)


The first (and only) item you can find inside Bilibin Cave's south side is the Elven Rapier. So don't bother if you won't see the hole in the wall next to the weapon, when you are here for the first time. In fact, you can't see it even if you try every trick you know until now. Ivan must use the REVEAL spell, which he will be aware of after his visit at Master Hama in Lama.

 Use GROWTH on the vine.

First use REVEAL to find the hidden entrance to the cave part seen on the left!

The next trick will be DOUSE on the little fire between the lamp posts. Now your way is free! Oh, I nearly forgot. You have to use DOUSE on another fire in the northern part of Bilibin Cave BEFORE you come here. Details are to be found in the next part on this section.
