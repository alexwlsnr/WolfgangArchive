Fuchin Cave North and West (Part II)


Now here comes the trick of Fuchin Cave. First you have to find the Dragons Eye which you are supposed to put in the empty eye hole of the dragon statue. The room where the statue is standing will be lit and you will see a path (slightly dark colored).

The trick is that the path is not on the floor on the lower part of the room but in "mid air", accessable from the leap to the left (reminds me of Indiana Jones when he jumps off the head of the lion in "Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade").
