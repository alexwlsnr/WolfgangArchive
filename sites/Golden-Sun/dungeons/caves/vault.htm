Vault Cave (Part I)


Maybe you noticed the Venus Djinn in the northeast corner of Vault village. You tried everything to reach him but all was in vain. But of your visit at Master Hama in the Lama Temple Ivan knows about the REVEAL spell. So you will be ablwe now to find the hidden entrance to Vault Cave in the middle of the stone circle which is "guarded" by a cute little dog.





Items to gain:

None at all


Be careful with the chest near the entrance! It's a Mimic.




The trick is to get to the switch somehow, which is placed next to the door in the center room filled with water. Only then the water flows out the room and you can reach the door which was flooded before. Shown below is the room as it should look like after you hit the switch!



You will reach the switch via the 1. floor.
