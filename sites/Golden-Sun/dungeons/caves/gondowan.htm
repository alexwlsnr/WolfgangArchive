Gondowan Cave


This cave connects the continent Gondowan with the region south of Kalay. You may enter it from its east entrance once you visit Kalay, but you cannot explore it due to missing abilities. After you got the LIFTING GEM in Altin you may enter again from the western entrance and find your way back to Kalay. Don't miss the APPLE and the LUCKY MEDAL inside.

Meanwhile you don't need explanations how to get rid of the blocking statues any more!
