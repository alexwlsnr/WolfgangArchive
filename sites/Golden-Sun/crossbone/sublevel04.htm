Crossbone Island Floor 4


Hope you read about Hippogriffs and how to behave near them in 'Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban'. Ok, it won't help much here but the Griphons are too similar to ignore this little comment.


To master this room, get all treasures and exit finally, two psynergy spells will come in handy.
The CLOAK spell is not essentially necessary, but you surely will need HALT.
So visit the Cave of Vale if you don't have the HALT GEM yet.

Try to sneak past the statues with CLOAK but the effect using HALT on them is far better!
