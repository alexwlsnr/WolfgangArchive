Crossbone Ground Floor


Finally, you have reached the ground floor.
Surprisingly, no enemies are to be seen. But this can be changed immediately, if you dare.

The minor problem is the chest near the mast which is one of the hardest Mimics you will find. If the beast is history, you will not only gain a mass of EXP and money but also a Potion as a reward, if I remember correctly.

Now, DON'T dare to explore the ship without having healed all party members to their max!

It's Deadbeard, who is the main cause why no one has entered this forgotten place for centuries. He, in fact, is the most fearsome foe you will encounter in the whole quest, in my oppinion even mightier than the final enemy you still will have to face!
