Ivan


This page will give you some information about Ivan, who was raised by Master Hammet in the town of Kalay. On one of the journeys the Shaman Staff was stolen from Hammet and Ivan has to get it back. On this occasion he meets Isaac and Garet and joins the party.

Below you will see some tables showing the abilities he gains after reaching special levels, the classes Ivan will aquire when being equipped with different djinns, and last but not least a table with psynergy spells depending on different classes.

The table mentioned last is far from completion but I think it has enough information even for those of you who are pure statistic fans.
