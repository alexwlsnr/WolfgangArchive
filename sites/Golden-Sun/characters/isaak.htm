Isaac


This page will give you some information about the main hero of "Golden Sun", Isaac. He, together with Garet will be in the party from the start of the quest.

Below you will see some tables showing the abilities he gains after reaching special levels, the classes Isaac will aquire when being equipped with different djinns, and last but not least a table with psynergy spells depending on different classes.

The table mentioned last is far from completion but I think it has enough information even for those of you who are pure statistic fans.
