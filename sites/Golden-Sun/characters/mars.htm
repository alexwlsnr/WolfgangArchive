Mars Djinns


These nice creatures are called djinnis. After the element stones were stolen from the shrine in Sol
Sanctum, the djinnis were scattered all around the world, not knowing what they should do. So Isaac
and his friends have to find them one by one. In total you will be able to find 28 of them during your
quest. They are devided in the four elemental classes Earth (Venus), Fire (Mars), Water (Mercury)
and Wind (Jupiter).

The list below shows details like their names, where to find them, if you must fight them, etc.
And, last but not least, they are displayed in order of their appearance during the adventure.
