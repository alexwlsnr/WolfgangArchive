Sheba


There is not much to say about Sheba, a strange girl Isaac, Garet, Ivan and Mia meet in
Babi's palace. She, alongside Jenna, Kraden and Alex follows Saturos and Menardi to
the Venus Lighthouse.
Sheba is well known in Lalivero where she had lived before. It doesn't matter whom you ask
in Lalivero, all seem to be very worried about Sheba's vanishing.
A small troop of townsfolk even try to rescue her by following Saturos and Menardi into
the depth of the Tunnel Ruins underneath Babi Tower, but a door which cannot be opened
by nonadepts blocks their way.

Just before Isaac's party, who managed to solve the door problem, have to fight
Saturos and Menardi on top of the Venus Lighthouse it will be revealed that Sheba
is a wind adept like Ivan and really important to Saturos. She shall open the Jupiter
Lighthouse for him. Saturos even promises not to harm her if Ivan hands the Shaman
Staff over to him. Isaac and friends misunderstand this offer of Saturos as a promise to
set her free, but now is the moment for Felix to step forth and give Sheba a helping hand.

After Isaac's party finally have finished off the Fusion Dragon, the tower breaks apart and
Sheba falls of the top of Venus Lighthouse into the sea. Felix tries to rescue her and
jumps after her. Can both be saved?

Don't worry, you will see them once again in the epilogue section and, of course, in a
main role in the adventures of Golden Sun 2 !
