Golden Sun Epilogue


Every game has its end, even Golden Sun. I hope I was not the only person being surprised by the fact, that after Isaac and friends entered the Lemurian ship, the credits scene appeared and the game was over!

But the Golden Sun sequel named Golden Sun "The Lost Age"has been published in Japan already and should hit the US stores in December 2002 followeed by the European publication in Spring 2003.

So we don't have to wait too long to see how the story ciontinues with Felix, Jenna, and Sheba as main heroes!
