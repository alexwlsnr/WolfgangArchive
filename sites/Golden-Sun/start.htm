The unofficial "Home of the Golden Sun" site welcomes you!

Again, another "great" page for a great game has opened. This time it deals with a game for the
Gameboy Advanced.

All those who know my further pages know that I normally only create gamesites for Sega
consoles. Ok, there are two sites about the Lunar versions for Playstation, but these games
were published for the Sega MegaCD, too.

Indeed, Golden Sun is the first game I played on a Nintendo device and, to tell the truth, it was
because of this game I actually bought a Gameboy Advanced to play it. As I always say,
Software sells Hardware!

Golden Sun was created by Camelot Soft, a Japanese game developing company, which
published the Shining Force series for Sega. If you ever played one of the Shining Force
games, you will soon be familiar with the colorful battle scences, items like Mythril and last
but not least, the detailed work on the whole game which shows how much love and effort
the developers put into it. So it was only natural that Golden Sun gets its place within my
series of "Home of the ..." websites.

Ok, enough smalltalk, surf and enjoy. If you like to get more information take a look under
News in the Site Info section.
