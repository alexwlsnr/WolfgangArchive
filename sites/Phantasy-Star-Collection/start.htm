Welcome to the Home of Phantasy Star Website (Phantasy Star Collection)

After a severe headcrash of the harddisk of my computer, I had some trouble in making my Saturn emulator run again. So I could not work on a section I was planning for a long, long time. But today I want to present you the best part of the Phantasy Star Collection, which was published for the Sega Saturn back in 1998 by Sega Ages.

All owners know that the CD not only contained all four Phantasy Star games but four TV commercials which were to be seen in Japan. I think all fans of Phantasy Star will have seen them because they are published on many pages all over the net.
But there was still more: an image gallery of each game.
And I would like to present you these images in this section within my Phantasy Star pages.

Hope you like what you will find. Surf and enjoy!
