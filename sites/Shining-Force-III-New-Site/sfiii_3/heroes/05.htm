This Republican commander is called Edmund. He was tossed down a waterfall by a general of the Empire named Garzel. There are only two possibilities to get him as a Shining Force member.

1. Play Scenario 3 without a save game data of the previous scenarios.
2. Kill Stella in Scenario 2 so that Profound, Stellas fiancee, will not join the force.

Otherwise Edmund will be a boss enemy in the fight in the plains right after Bearsol Town.
