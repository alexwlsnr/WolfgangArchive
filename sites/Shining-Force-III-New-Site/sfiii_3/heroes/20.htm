If you enter Dormant for the second time (right after Julian and friends rescued Synbios) you might obviously see a tiny man running around the village.

Hurry to the item shop and buy a Delicious Meat there. Now talk to Turk, then together with Turk examine the well in the outskirts.

A huge ogre will jump out of the well and you have got Turk,
an Ogre Rider, in your party!
