Status:    PromotedClass:Dragon God

Another spectacular member of Julian's forces may be Thousand! I wrote 'may be' due to the fact that you have several options if and how the dragon will get to the party. Thousand appears during the fight in the dragon valley.

  1. Thousand is defeated by any other than Cyclops.
    He will die and never joins.
  2. Cyclops kills Thousand, the dragon will join but
    cannot be controlled!
  3. Cyclops talks to Thousand during the battle.
    The dragon will join fully controllable!
