It is as I promised in Scenario 2. Donhardt is back, fully controlable. No longer suicide like actions on his own as we could see it sometimes during Medion's adventure.

If you play with a save game data of Scenario 2 he will continue on board of the flagship with the same level he had reached in Scenario 2. Otherwise he starts the new adventure at level 5.

Still better with halberds than with lances, Dohardt will be to person of your choice to enter the first tombs, equipped with some Swift Boots he will be faster than the most of the thiefs. So he can block the exit and 'capture' them.
