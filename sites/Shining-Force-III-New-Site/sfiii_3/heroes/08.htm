If you playing Scenario 2, Honesty is a well known character to you. Now is the time to have her in your party.

Like the birdmen in the previous scenarios Honesty can get to places which are unreachable by all those who cannot fly.

Honesty is waiting for you outside the town of Guarder, as a security guardfor Melinda, who is the mother of Medion and Isabella.

Honesty is best with all kinds of lances but you can equip her with wings, too. To tell the truth, I have to confess that I have never tried this during my adventures.
