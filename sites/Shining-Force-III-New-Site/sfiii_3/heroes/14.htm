Pendolf is the only member of the Penn family with some knowledge of magic. Besides a helpful Support spell he can summon large doubles of his parents Penn and Penko with some cool (or hot) action!

So you can imagine that Pendolf will wait for you in Aspinia only if you found the other penguins, Penn and Penko, in the previous scenarios.
