Shining Force III - Scenario 2 - Traders, Blacksmith, Priest    

Priests: Like in the previous scenarios priests will help you in various ways. But if you reach Remotest don't expect to get help from the Bulzom priest seen on the right.

Innkeeper:
This is the only picture of an inn keeper in all three scenarios. Meet him in the inn of the Floating Village.

Item Shop: These nice ladies await you in item shops. Standard items like Herbs, Antidotes etc. are offered freely. But to get more special items you have to be very persistant. To the right, you see the item shop keeper of the Floating Village.

Weapon
Shops:		 Get better weapons here. It could be wise to ask for special deals from time to time, many magical weapon are only offered in the deals department! Again, to the right, you see the shop keeper of the Floating Village.

Black-
smith:		 Blacksmith appear in Scenario 3, too. The guy to the right is the only blacksmith who can forge Orihalcon material besides the Mithril stuff. Best weapons guaranteed!
