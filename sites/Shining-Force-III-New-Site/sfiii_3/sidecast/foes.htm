Shining Force III - Scenario 3 - Foes

Fiale: Together with two of his three colleagues Fiale will wait at the end of the quest for the Shining Force to arrive at Bulzom's hideout. Along with the others you have to beat him. But in the very moment of victory Bulzom will finally awake!




Next

Basanda: Together with her son Braff, Basanda tries to prevent Julian from entering the snow fortress where Bulzom is hidden. Again, she will be no match for your well trained party of fierce fighters, magician, and healers. The Shining Force will win again!

Dessheren: Meet Dessheren for the last time. Her death will break the evil floating aura which surrounds Jean. Seeing her sister die will drive Jean mad unless Gracia uses the "Blessing" spell on her. This will bring her to back to sense again and makes Jean join.

Goriate: You will have to fight Goriate two times. First he tries to wipe out Julian's forces in front of Destonia. He then will be rescued by Jean. At the end of the quest you will meet him again as he tries to protect Bulzom during the awakening process.
