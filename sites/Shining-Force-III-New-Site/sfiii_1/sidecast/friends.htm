King
Benetram:		 He is the leader of the Republic, a former part of the Empire , which separated from it because political differences could not longer be negleted. Benetram leads the delegation in a peace conference with the Empire which is held in the floating city of Saraband.




Next

Brutus: This bald-headed guy is one of the advisors who came with King Benetram to the peace conference in Saraband.
He seems to have a good relationship to Saraband's gouvernor Grevy. Brutus, too, is leader of the Bearsol territory within the Republic. Will he stay loyal until the end?

Edmund: He accompanies King Benetram to a meeting with the emperor Domaric just before the trouble in Saraband begins. He, too, has a secret, but whether he stands one the side of the good guys or on the side of the bad guys will depend mainly on your gameplay!

Tybalt: He is a general loyal to the Republic, but being a bit strict, he is not loved by all Republican citizen and their leaders. He seems not to have much trust in Synbios because he thinks that our hero is much to young for taking responsibility.
Medion's forces will meet Tybalt again in Scenario 2.
