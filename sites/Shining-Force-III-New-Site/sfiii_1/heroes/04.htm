Eldar is a bird woman, who belongs to the Republic forces.
She joins just in time to fight against the thief (chief) Shiraf.

First Eldar is very weak but if you trust and train her, she sooner
or later becomes a valuable member of your forces.

Because she is the only one who can fly, Eldar alone can reach certains places with nice goodies!
