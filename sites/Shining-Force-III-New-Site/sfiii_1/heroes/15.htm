Masqurin is in your party from the very beginning. Her "childish" behaviour will change to that of an reliable adult during the quest.
As a magician and later as a wizard she is very good in using and resisting magic but very weak if it comes to a hand to hand combat. So leave her in the backrow and use her to "warm up" the enemies for the fighters in the frontline.
