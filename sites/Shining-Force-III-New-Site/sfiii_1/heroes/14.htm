Kahn is suddenly standing in the gravejard of the haunted town of Quonos. He is an Elbesem monk and carries with him the Orb of Elbesem.
This gem will become very important during your quest.
In the first battle with Kahn in your party he is not controllable but always follows Synbios.
Kahn then uses the Elbesem Orb to free the Kyon-shi's. After the fight is over, Kahn finally joins! He is a nice and strong fighter, his healing and support abilitiys may be very helpful, too.
