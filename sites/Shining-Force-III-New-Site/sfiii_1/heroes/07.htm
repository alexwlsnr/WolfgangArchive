Grace, a capable healer and lioness is a member of Synbios' forces from the very beginning. Don't let her fight by standard hits (she is not very strong) and due to her weak defense she might get hurt deadly and you will need her to heal the rest of the party members.
Her Antidote spell will help much if one of her friends gets poizoned or paralyzed!
And, being equipped with magic wands or rods, she can perform mighty magic attacks, too.
