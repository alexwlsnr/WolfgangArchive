Once being a servant for the Vandal in Quonos, goblin Ratchet
now is somehow unemployeed. He uses his new freedom to roam the countryside in his unique battle suit.
To get him into your party you have to do two things:

First, enter a room in the basement of Quonos mansion and examine the machine inside. Ratchet then will appear, jump into the steam suit and leaves the mansion.

Second, during your first vist to Vagabound find Ratchet standing in a corner of the village. Speak to him. Again he will not join! Once you attempt to enter the Dragon Cave, Ratchet will finally become a member of the Shining Force.
