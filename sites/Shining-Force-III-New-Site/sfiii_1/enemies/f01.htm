Fiale    (Shining Force III - Scenario 1 - Enemies F)    

Fiale, one of the four Bulzom Highpriests, is a master of disguise. He mimes a fake King Benetram to abduce the Emperor Domaric at the peace conference. This starts all the trouble Synbios and friends are facing. Synbios can beat him in the battle at the tower of Lookover, but Fiale will be saved by Basanda in the very last moment!
