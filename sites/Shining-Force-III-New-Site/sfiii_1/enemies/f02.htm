Franz    (Shining Force III - Scenario 1 - Enemies F)    

You will meet Imperial General Franz right behind the village of Quonos. He tries to prevent Synbios' forces from crossing a bridge leading to the Republic territory. His plan may have succeeded, if Spiriel and her troops, bewitched by Basanda, would not have beaten his reinforcement party to mere dust. Now Synbios' small forces will be able to gain victory!
