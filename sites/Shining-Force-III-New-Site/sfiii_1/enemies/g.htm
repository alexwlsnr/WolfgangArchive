Garzel    (Shining Force III - Scenario 1 - Enemies G)    

Imperial General Garzel's troops are awaiting you on the bridge leading to the Republic. Weakend by the tragic loss of Julian, Synbios' forces will have to beat them to get home.
To make things even worse, Garzel and the Republic general Edmund have hidden most of their men in the back of Synbios' party. They will appear as soon as the first of your forces have reached the middle of the battlefield! Be sure not to have only weak party members in the back.
