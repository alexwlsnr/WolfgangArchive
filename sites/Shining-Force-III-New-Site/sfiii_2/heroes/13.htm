You will meet Papesh inside Storich inn. Looking not very strong he nevertheless is the best you can get your party is confronted with monsters. And the best of all:

Certain monsters can be tamed by Papesh if he finishes them off. One of them can then walk along with the rest of the party fully controlable! This makes an "extra warrior" free of charge.

But be careful, the tamed beast will not rise in its level and if it get's killed, it cannot be revived as normal party members!

Oh, I nearly forgot: if you miss Papesh in Storich he will wait for you in the town of Remotest in Scenario 3.
