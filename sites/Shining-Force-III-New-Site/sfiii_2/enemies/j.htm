Jade    (Shining Force III - Scenario 2 - Enemies J)    

Jade is a magnificent tank driver and you will have to fight him only of Synbios did save his older brother, Garosh, in the second battle of Scenario 1. If Garosh was killed by the Bulzom monks and the King Benetram imposter, Jade will join Medion's forces instead of fighting them! Again, your acts in a different scenario alter the story line in the following scenarios.
