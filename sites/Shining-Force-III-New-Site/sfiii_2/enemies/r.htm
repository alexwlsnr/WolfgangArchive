Rainbloods    (Shining Force III - Scenario 2 - Enemies R)    

Unless you plan suicide you should try not to get near the six Rainblood demons who appear during the fight inside the Elbesem sanctuary. So you will not find much more than two character pictures of each rainblood here on this page. Medion's forces are too weak to challenge them, so no statistics are displayed here.
But be patient, you will get your chance in Scenario 3 where you will face them two by two or on the Premium CD where you have to fight all six of them together!
