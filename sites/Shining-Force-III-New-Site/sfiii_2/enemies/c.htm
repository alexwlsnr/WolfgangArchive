Creward    (Shining Force III - Scenario 2 - Enemies C)    

Creward is a cruel (like his Japanese name "Cruel" impies) Empire general who seems to have secret connections to the Bulzom sect. For example, at the beginning of Medion's quest, Uryudo and Synthesis eyewitness a "meeting" of Creward and Fiale, one of the four Bulzom Highpriests. Later Medion and friends have to fight a seabattle against this general.
