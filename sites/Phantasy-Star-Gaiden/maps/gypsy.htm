Gypsy

Gypsy Village next to the desert

No shop, no inn, wonder why you came here?

Visit the tent of the soothsayer, he (she) will give you some advice, tells your fortune and gives you a very important item (needed to beat Kaburon).

Nothing is for free,  but you will be thankful that  (s)he has
helped you, even if it was for cash only.
