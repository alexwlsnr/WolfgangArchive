People you meet


Below you see a list of people whom you might meet during your quest to find Alis. Some of the are more or less important, some may be ignored completely.

Tedo Village Major
He is the major of Tedo village.

He will give you your first but weak weapons and armor which should be replaced as soon as possible.

Alec's Mother
The Mom of Alec.

In her house, of course, you don't have to pay for a stay.

Visit the churches
Cure yourself and save your efforts with the help of this beautiful lady.

Taylor
This guy is Taylor.

He was attacked and this event starts the adventure of Minina and Alec.

Sooth Sayer
Meet her/him in the Gypsy Village.

Listen to the advices carefully, if you want to find Alis at last. You will get an important item here, too.

Panin
Call him Panin.
He looks after the ruins east of Karakatto and will not allow you to enter them until he has received a 'First Hyoutan Aid', which seems to help (see right).

Poppo
Poppo is an old archeologist whom you meet in the cave in the far northeast.
He will give you a map as a present. But you can use the more detailed versions on the site, too, if you like!

Mogu
I am not sure about this one. He appears after you beat Daidalos. Can it be Odin from the original Phantasy Star team?
A bit older, but who knows. Here is a picture of Odin from Phantasy Star. Make your own decision.
