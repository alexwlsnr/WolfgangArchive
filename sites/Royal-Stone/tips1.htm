Tips for Playing The Royal Stone (1)
After a battle is over your party will enter the next town automatically.
You now control a character named Kokotto, a small elf.

He was present at the prison while Eva was sleeping. Now he will walk around towns to help you in his human form. After a battle you normally stand inside the local inn and it would be wise to save your progress before you leave and explore the village or town.		 At the Inn
