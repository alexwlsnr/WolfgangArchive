Tips for Playing The Royal Stone (12)
Ok, now let the show begin. Here the different menus are displayed which can be seen during the battle scenes. If a character is unable to use magic you can move him/her within the range of the highlighted area.
If your character is a spell caster, this menu will appear if you character does NOT stand side to side next an enemy.
This menu pops up if your party member (with magic abilities) stands next to an enemy.
If you decide to attack, you finally enter the attack screen. Choose from attack, magic, or retreat, if you must.
