Tips for Playing The Royal Stone (8)
Here you can (and should) donate items to a selected character:

Use the SPEED POTION on any character you like. It will level up the speed value by 1. Normally it should be translated as Motion Potion, which somehow reads more funny, but then the text would not fit into the screenshot window.

The SACRED BOOK should be given to healers like Pauline or Paris. They then will be promoted to the Bishop class. More spells are available within the Bishop class.

If the MAGE TRICKS are donated to Puppy and Too, they are promoted to Wizards and you can buy more powerful spells in the next magic shop, too.

If you can get your hands on the MONSTERGUIDE, give it either to Puppy or Too for a promotion to the Summoner class. although he/she looses their magic spells, the character now can summon all monsters which were tamed by Eva before.

I have not found out what can be done with the BOTTLE OF ZUU or the CROP OF ENGUS items. They can neither be equipped nor can they be given to any character or monster.
