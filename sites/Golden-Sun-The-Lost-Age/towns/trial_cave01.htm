Shaman Trial Cave (Part II)

Short description of Trial Cave (Part II)

Left Trial: This puzzle is no one at all. Move the pillars to the positions shown, climb the vine to the right, jump over to the left and run for the door!

Psynergy needed

None at all!!

Item to be gained

Vial


Right Trial: Use WHIRLWIND on the vine covered pillar, then POUND the iron sticks into the ground. This causes the others to pop up. Please don't try to jump on the iron sticks, they will drop down again. To avoid this, push the stone pillars over the sunken sticks. Climb the vine, jump to the right and up you go to reach the door in time.

Psynergy needed

WHIRLWIND
POUND

Item to be gained

Vial
