Madra Underground House

Without Piers you only can search some parts of the underground house.

The most important goal is to reach the basement via the 1. stairways due to the fact that the psynergy item Tremor Bit can be found in the chest hidden there.

You will need the TREMOR psynergy on the pirate's ship in Alhafra and later, once again in this spooky house.

After a long journey return here with Piers and use TREMOR to let the chest with the Ruin Key drop down from the bookcase in the upper floor into the room below (the one where a large stone smashed the desk). In the room you finally can reach the chest containing the key.

Now go down to the basement and find the room behind a massive and locked door. So use the key to open up.



In there a Combination Tablet is to be found. From now on you can summon Molok or Morock (both translations are possible). Two Mercury Djinns ( ) and one Jupiter Djinn ( ) are needed.
