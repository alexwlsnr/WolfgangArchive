Mikasalla Northern Part

After entering the cave via the trap door in the southwest part of Mikasalla, walk through the cave and you will see the sunlight again after exiting the cave through the hole in the north. Now simply walk up and you see Gutts.

To get the chest you must return to the village square, use the wooden tower, jump over the gap to the rlght cliff and go up. That's all.
