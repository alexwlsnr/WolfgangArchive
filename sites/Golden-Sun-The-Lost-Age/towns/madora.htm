Madra Town

 Sunset over Madra

After you passed the Dehkan Plateau you soon will find the town of Madra in the southeast part of the Indra continent. So much will happen here that it is difficult to explain everything without missing something. First search the town for some useful items, especially the Nurse Cap (on the cliff top left) is worth searching. To get there simply jump over the shop roofs.

Now visit Piers in the prison (the house to the left with the two guys guarding in front). Speak to him via MINDREAD, otherwise a very important scene never will take place. After you leave the prison, two "celebrities" of Madra will appear and ask you something. Blame on me, my Japanese is not good enough to tell you what they are talking about. But I think they will allow you to use the bridge right to Madra.

After this event you can enter the Madra Cave via a ladder which can be found near the graveyard to the right. Once you found a psnergy item in there you can leave the town and travel to the Osenia Continent via the Indra - Osenia Pass.

Later in the adventure when Piers became your friend and with the Black Orb in your possesion you will get the Cyclone Chip from the towns mayor. Even a Mars Djinn named Chili can be found here, but you have to trade the Green Mushroom for him. The mushroom only grows on the hills of the Indra - Gondowan Cliffs.

And another thing is remarkable in Madra, you will get a first glimps at Karst, one of the two main foes!
