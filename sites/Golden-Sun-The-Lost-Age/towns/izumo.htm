Izumo

 Totally eastern style, Izumo town!

Far east in the world ocean you will find an island with one town named Izumo and a mountain called Gaia Rock, another place to explore.

Izumo differs from any other town you have seen before and which you will visit later. All is dominated by a nice to look at eastern style.

Again, search thoroughly for hidden items and other goodies not to be found easily.

Two things I would like to mention and both are bound to Gaia Rock somehow. You will need the Dancing Idol (to be found on top of Gaia Rock) to trade it for the Mars Djinn Turbo.

A second not so obvious item to be found is another Combo Tablet deep inside Izumo Cave. To find your way down where the tablet is hidden, you must know about the SAND psynergy (also gained inside Gaia Rock).
