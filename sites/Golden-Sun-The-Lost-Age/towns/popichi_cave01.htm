Garoh Cave (Part II)

When you return here to speak with Maha after you spend one night in the village inn, you will get a really nice present.

No more no less than the Jupiter Djinn Zen is what Maha is offering to you. One more djinn for victory!

You may use REVEAL on the big stone in front of which Maha is standing, but the only thing you will find out, once you entered the room, is that Maha is in need of some chairs, a table and a bed inside his oh so poorly equipped living room!
