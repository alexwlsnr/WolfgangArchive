Naribwe Village

 Naribwe, the first village on South Gondowan

 Use WHIRLWIND to see the ladder

 Use REVEAL to see the chest

Items to gain
18 coinsThorn Crown
ElixirNut


Northeast to the Indra - Gondowan Cliffs you will find Naribwe, a nice place to stock up your items, buy new weapons and to meet a Voodoo Doctor in the hut to the top right. About this guy I am not quite sure.

You can offer items to him, maybe he bewitches them for some upgrading, but I did not manage to offer an item to him on which his voodoo will work properly. I think we have to wait for a translated version to find out!
