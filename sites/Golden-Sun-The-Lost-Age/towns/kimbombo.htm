Kibombo Town

 Watch the ceremony in Kibombo Town at night

After you passed Kibombo Moutains venture north to find the town of  Kibombo. The day you visit a ceremony is held in which the Black Orb seems to play a main role. Knowing that you need the orb to make the Lemurian Boat work properly, not only you are trying to get your hands on that item.

You soon will notice, that Piers, too, is here and that he has the same in mind, getting the Black Orb. So it's only natural that you join forces. Finally, Piers becomes a team member and he brings witch him two Mercury Djinns, Picu and Shade.

How to get the orb is explained in the Kibombo Statue section.
