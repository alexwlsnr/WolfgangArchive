Champa Cave (Part IV)



In case you gained all the three Trident Parts you should come back to this room. After a conversation between Briggs and Obaba, the pirate leaves the scene.

Obaba then will ask you a question. If you answer YES, you soon will have to fight the ugly Abyss Salamander.

If your answer is No, you will never finish your quest. So, indeed, you really have no choice at all!

After the fight offer all three trident parts to Obaba. She then will throw them down the hole and after a nice transformation, the finished Trident will float in the air, just above the hole.

 REVEAL will do the trick!

To get it, simply use REVEAL to grab this most important item!
