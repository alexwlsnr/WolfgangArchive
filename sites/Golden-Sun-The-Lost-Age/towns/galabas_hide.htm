Apo jii Cave and Worlds End

 Ideal for some bungee jumping, Worlds End an Apo jii

If you used SAND at the beach in Apo jii you will enter here near the coast line. Don't be afraid to jump down the cliff to the left, you will land safely on a platform below.

Pass the waterfall and you will enter a hidden cave. Inside the cave use WHIRLWIND on the bushes for a new doorway. Following the new path you will leave the cave next to the Jupiter Djinn Hide. Once he joined, go back through the cave and take the middle exit.

Now use LASH to get back to the top of the cliffs.

Psynergy needed
WHIRLWIND
LASH
