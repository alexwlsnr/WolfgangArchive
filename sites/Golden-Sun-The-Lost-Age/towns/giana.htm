Contigo Town

 Contigo is the main town on the Atteka continent


Northeast of Atteka Gate, next to a meteor crater you will see the town of Contigo. You will return here several times, even if you found Mars Lighthouse near the end of your quest, it could be wise to come back here, before attempting to conquer the last lighthouse.

The reason for this is the Temple of Anemos, which was erected here in former times. In this temple, two more Combo Tablets are hidden which you shouldn't miss.

After you finished your task on top of the Jupiter Lighthouse which is lying north of here, you will meet Isaac here again. He and his friends finally will join to form a party of eight mighty psynergy users waiting for your command.

A Mars Djinn named Shine and a Venus Djinn called Salt can be found, too. Refer to the pictures to the right to find out where they are hidden.
