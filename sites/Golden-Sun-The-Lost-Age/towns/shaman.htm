Shaman Cave

Until you are unable to perform the TRANSPORT psynergy your way to Shaman always leads through this cave. No items are to be found here but two things should be mentioned nonetheless.

First, a Mercury Djinn is waiting inside the cave. The djinn Kashis can only be reached once you united force with Isaac due to the fact that the Lift psynergy is needed to get near him.

Second, if Isaac is in your party you will be confronted with three well known enemies of the previous adventure:

Azart, Navampa, and Satraga will appear to gain revenge because they were beaten by Isaac in the Colosso Tournament of Tolbi.
