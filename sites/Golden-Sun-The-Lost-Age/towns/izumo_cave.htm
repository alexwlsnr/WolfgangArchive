Izumo Cave (Part I)

After you found the hidden entrance to Izumo Cave, you may get tricked to use POUND psynergy to get past the sticks and stones. But if you do so, you will leave a gap too big to jump over. Better use SAND to crawl under the sand and use the frozen pillars and the wooden sticks to get to the door into the next room.

Here first go right, use POUND on the wooden pile, then SAND to get past the stones. Create a stepstone with the wooden statue, which you will need to reach the ladder when coming back here, this time from the left direction. Climb down the ladder to the next part of the cave.

Psynergy needed
POUND SAND MOVE
