Kandorea Temple

 Kandorea is locked, you cannot enter via the front door!


The next building on your way to glory is Kandorea Temple, south of Daila. The monks inside seem to need it quite and cosy for their levitation lessons. So the front door is locked. You must find a way to sneak inside somewhere else.

Use WHIRLWIND on the bush in the top left corner to open a hidden entrance inside the mountain wall.

Enter and you find yourself in the cave shown below. Climb up the rope inside the well and you are where you wanted to be.
