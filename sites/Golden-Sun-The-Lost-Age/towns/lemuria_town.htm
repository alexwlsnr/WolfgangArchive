Lemuria

 Lemuria, the place that Babi dreamed to find

After you finally enter Lemuria your first task is to find a way into the palace to have some talk with King Hydros. It is not easy due to the fact that the guards block the entrance. Don't search a hidden entrance but try to find someone who will act as a guarantor for you. In Lemuria it is really important to speak to everybody, because as you don't understand what the people are talking about you might easily overlook the "trigger" which grants progress.

In one of the house (with many bottles lying around) you talk to a guy who will tell you a sad story about Piers' mother (I think she died in his absence!). Piers then will leave. He later can be found next to one grave at the graveyard. He refuses to join. But one after the other, after Piers leaves the house, the boy inside will send a letter via pigeon to a guy named Lunpa. You heard right, the name is Lunpa, founder of a village in Angara named by him and who wandered around the world together with Babi of Tolbi in former times.

 Worldmap in Lemurian Palace

He decided to stay in Lemuria when Babi left and resides in the house to the right. Once he got the pigeon post you can enter the house and talk a bit, then Lunpa runs off to meet the king. Follow him and the guards will step aside.

Inside the throne room you will not only find King Hydros and Lunpa but also a person called Conservato and Piers. All talk for a while and I think Conservato is getting really upset because Poseidon has been beaten by your party. After some TV show (see above) Hydros decides to give you a very precious item, the Grindstone. That's where Conservato leaves in anger. Equip the stone on Felix which will grant him the ability to sink huge rock down into the water by using GRIND on them. Now you can leave Lemuria.

 There's a Lucky Well in Lemuria

Before doing so, you may want to visit the Lucky Well in the center of the town. Earn some money, of try to get precious items, amongst them even a Combo Tablet by throwing some Lucky Medals into the center of the well!
