Shaman Hill Foot

 Find this abandoned harbour and get another Mercury Djinn

If you are fond of Mad Plants, use CYCLONE on all leafs, sooner of later one of those ugly beats will pop up.

Pay more attention instead on the Jupiter Djinn called Moriban. he only can be tricked by the HALT psynergy just like the one you may have met in the Vane Cave while helping Isaac to fulfill his task.

If you are in urgent need of an Elixir, use REVEAL next to the top right pond for seeing the hidden stepstone inside.

Psynergy needed

CYCLONE (optional)
REVEAL (optional)
HALT (absolutely)

Item to gain

Elixir
