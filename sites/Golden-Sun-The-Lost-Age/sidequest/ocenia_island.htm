Osenia Islet

 South of Angara Islet is the location of Osenia Islet.

Your main goal here is to get near the cow behind the fence. Easy, if you know about SAND.

Being in posession of the Red Cloth and using MIND READ to talk to the cow rewards you with some nice milk, which again has to be traded somewhere.

Don't forget to grab a Lucky Medal hidden somewhere on the island.
