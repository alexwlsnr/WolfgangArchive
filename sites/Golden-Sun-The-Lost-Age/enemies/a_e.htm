Enemies A-E


Before you are disappointed not to find any HP values of the enemies, just let me explain that this time I tried to concentrate on what really counts: Experience Points and Money.

You will soon find out yourself if an enemy is beaten, and no one refers to a HP list when being encountered in a fight!

If an enemy drops rare or valuable items, it will be shown, too, but this list is far from completion!

Oh, and just one more thing, I tried to translate the names of the enemies from Japanese to English, so please don't blame me if they differ from the names which the enemies probably will get in the English game version.
