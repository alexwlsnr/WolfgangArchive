Indra Gondowan Cliffs (Part II)

 Enter Yampi Desert

Three thing sould be mentioned here. First, you shouldn't miss the green mushroom, called Healing Fungus. Later in the adventure you can trade this vegetable for a Mars Djinn. Inside the chest you will find a Sleep Bomb, so you may ignore it!

Second, you will find another Mars Djinn called Axel or Accel if you prefer. You have to fight him to make him join.

Third, there is a huge rock to be seen right under the rope connecting the Indra and Gondowan continents. Once you have your own ship it will block your way south. Visit Lemuria, learn about GRIND and return here to remove it.
