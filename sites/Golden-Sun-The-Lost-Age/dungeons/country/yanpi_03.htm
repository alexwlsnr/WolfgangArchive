Yampi Desert (Part IV)

 Hunting the Scorpion in Yampi Desert!


Your party will enter from the extrance to the southwest. Use the Psynergy Stone after you pressed the pillar into the sand in case your psynergy level is low.

Now take your tour to the north and you will see something which is moving under the sand.

You are supposed to "guide" this mysterious animal living under the sand to the east exit by using POUND on the wooden pillar. The animal then will hit the wood underneath the sand and changes course!

Once it climbs out you will notice that it's a giant scorpion. Follow him to the next part of the desert.
