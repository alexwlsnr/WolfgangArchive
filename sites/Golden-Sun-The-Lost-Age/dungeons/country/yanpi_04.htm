Yampi Desert (Part V)

 Corner the King Scorpion in the stone formation!




 SCOOP will do, not Scooby Doo!

 Well, well, a well

 Back at the surface and a new pond

It may be a bit tricky to lead the King Scorpion to its trap, but, if it does not work as you planned simply leave the scene, re-enter and start once more! After your victory, you gain a Scoop Gem which one in the party should equip at once.

Now that you obtained the SCOOP psynergy, make a test in the center of the stone formation. A fountain will appear which will carry you back to the top.

With the new trick you can visit the townsfolk inside the desert for a second time
