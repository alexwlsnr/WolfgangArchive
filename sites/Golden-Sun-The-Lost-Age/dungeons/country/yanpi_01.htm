Yampi Desert (Part II)

 Yampi Desert seems somehow crowded!


When entering this part of the desert you will notice some guys standing and wondering how to climb the cliffs. You cannot help now, but you will soon!

First you have to learn a new psynergy spell called SCOOP. Details about how to help the Major of Madra can be found below.

Use SCOOP in the center of the stone formation,
climb down the ladder, follow the cave to the top
and you will come out next to the small lake to the
right. Now climb the cliff.

You just opened a new path to Alhafra.
