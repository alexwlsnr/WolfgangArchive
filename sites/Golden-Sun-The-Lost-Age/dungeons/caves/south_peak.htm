Atteka Cave


Atteka Cave can be entered only after Isaac and friends are members of your party. In the meantime, your Lemurian ship has been upgraded with wings by the friendly people of Atteka Harbour and Contigo Village.

Now your ship can fly over dry land. Don't expect too much, you cannot fly over the trees, maybe the ship is still too heavy for that. Nevertheless, it is a very helpful gimmick.

Back to Atteka Cave. Inside you have to convince Piers to perform his PARCH magic to get the combination tablet of Koatori or Quatori, both interpretations of the Japanese Katakana are possible. It is a rare Healing summoning, no attack summoning with the help of three Water Djinns ( ) and three Wind Djinns ( ).
