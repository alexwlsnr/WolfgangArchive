Treasure Isle (Part I)


In the northeastern part of the world of Golden Sun you will find a cave filled with treasures and, in the last room, a Combination Tablet.

Be warned, the enemies inside this cave are really strong and, until you haven't trained your party to a certain level, you will have trouble reaching the last room.

You also will need one spell which you will gain once you found Lemuria and talked to their leader Hydros, the GRIND psynergy.
