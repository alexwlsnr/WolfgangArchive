Kibombo Cave (Part IV)


So that's the room where your exploration will finally get you. Two things are important in here.

Number One: you will get the Tomegathericon , an item like the Mystic Card you found in Kandorea Temple Cave. Equip it and your character will change the class. This time he or she will gain "Black Magic" psynergies. You can summon beasts which attack the enemies or heal your party.

And two: if you use CYCLONE next to the plants a Mad Plant will pop up for the first time. It's like a Mimic and can appear in different locations during your quest.

Item to be gained
Tomegathericon

Psynergy needed (not essentially)
CYCLONE
