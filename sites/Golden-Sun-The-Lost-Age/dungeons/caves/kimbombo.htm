Kibombo Cave (Part I)


Do you remember the hidden trapdoor which opened in Kibombo Statue after you got the Black Orb? If you climb down the ladder you will end in this little cave.

You cannot reach other dungeon parts unless you have the knowledge of the CYCLONE psynergy. You will get the Cyclone Chip from the head of Madra village when you present the Black Orb to him.

Use the psynergy when standing next to the leaves on the ground and they will whirl away. Sometimes you will find some goodies, from time to time you find hidden puddles and very often you use the psynergy in vain. If you see a strange mark on the floor use CYCLONE and you will be transported to an other location. So that's the trick you should perform here, too! But do it on the right place!
