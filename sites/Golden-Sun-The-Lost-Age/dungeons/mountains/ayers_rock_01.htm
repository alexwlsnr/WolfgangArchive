Air's Rock (Part II)

 Up you go, don't look below (scary rhyme)

Fight your way up to enter the upper parts of Air's Rock. Use the whirlwind blown by the statue to reach cliffs which can't be climbed to. Ignore the door at the top left, it only can be opened from inside.

To get the chest in the center, you have to return here with Piers for the FROST spell is needed. You have to decide if a Vial is worth to come back here.
