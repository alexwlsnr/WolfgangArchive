Magma Rock (Part X)

 Jenna will gain her "private" psynergy here!

At last, you enter a room where you might have been before. It is the room at the foot of Magma Rock with the Mimic inside the chest.

But now you can move the stone pillar and use it right away to enter the door to the left. Jump over the magma and enter the inner sanctum of Magma Rock.

Inside Jenna will gain the BLAZE psynergy, needed for blowing flames gently over from a lit candle or fire to an unlighted one. This comes in handy, once you reached the Mars Lighthouse.

Please, don't forget the Magma Ball which can be found inside the room where you were forced to jump over the lava pitch. You will need it in the village of Loho as a "cannonball".

Item to be gained
Magma Ball
Psynergy to be gained
BLAZE
