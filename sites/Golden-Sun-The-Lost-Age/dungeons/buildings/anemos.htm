Anemos Temple (Part I)


 Anemos Temple Outskirts



Next to a huge crater on the Atekian Continent you will find the town of Contigo.

Here you can visit the temple of Anemos, too. But there are two things you should keep in mind.

First, you cannot use the front door because the entrance is blocked by the temple architecture.

Second, even if you take the "backdoor" via the transport circle of Contigo, you won't come far inside the temple if you have not collected ALL 72 Djinns during your quest.
