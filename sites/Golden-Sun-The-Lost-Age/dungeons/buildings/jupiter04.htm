Jupiter Lighthouse (Part V)

a) Felix will enter this room for the first time through door (4), go north and push the pillar into the pit. This seems not to be very useul, but you just created the first part of a neat shortcut needed later. Now you should leave throught the southwest exit (A). The party will be outside the lighthouse now. Ignore the CYCLONE symbol between the statues, but keep it in mind for later. Re-enter the lighhouse via door (B).

b) If you finally gained the Red Key, come back here, use CYCLONE on the symbol mentioned above and you will blown up to the floor above right in front of the red door.

 Outside of Jupiter Lighthouse

c) The tricky part to reach the entrance to the left tower of Jupiter Lighthouse is to get behind the stone head, which is blowing you back once being near it. So use the pink pillars on the bridge as a "hideout", fool the head by moving up and step back in the same moment. The statue blows and at the very moment the windhose is right past you take a run (B-Button) to the left to reach door (I).

d) The next time you will find yourself outside of the lighthouse you will step through the door next to the stone face on the right. Step on the levitation point, perform HOVER and the statue will blow you to the right tower of the lighthouse. You can (and must) enter through door (K).
After you solved all puzzles of the right tower return here and use LASH on the rope to get back to the central tower.
