Jupiter Lighthouse (Part I)

 The third lighthouse is dedicated to Jupiter

Northwest of Contigo you will find the Jupiter Lighthouse. To tell the truth, if you havn't been to the village of Shaman on the Hesperian Continent before, you will not come far inside this building. A special psynergy is needed to master the lighthouse, and to get it, you will need to proove yourself worthy in Shaman.

All those of you who went through the first adventure together with Isaac may remember one of the last scenes where the Shaman Staff was mentioned. So this may give you some clue.
