Anemos Temple (Part III)

You will enter through the door at the bottom of the screen.

Try you reach door No. 5 to the 2. sublevel.

Two tips on the way: the elevator will only start, when all pitched on the floor have turned to white!

And the second tip deals with the stone block with a human shape on in that you see to the left. When you enter the room the stoneblock will mimic all your movements like a mirror.
So watch you steps and try to steer the stone to the switch next to the door. Only when you managed to do so, the door will open. If the stone falls down a pit, simply leave the room and re-enter it for a next try!
