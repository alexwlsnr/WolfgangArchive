Jupiter Lighthouse (Part III)



a) Inside this room you will see a light beam. Remember this room well, you will come back here more than once. For now, take the door to the left (1) and enter the room you see below. Use the stairway (2) to come to the next floor after you pushed the rightmost pillar back in position using MOVE.

b) The next time you enter here will be from the door (3). Use CYCLONE and you will be transported up one ledge. Leave the room by the door (4).

c) After you have activated the lighthouse power, you will return here through door (11). Push the "missing link" down to the ground floor and place it in the hole to let the energy flow right to the next levitation point. Use HOVER on it but don't use the levitation point to the left now.

Better use CYCLONE on the purple cyclone symbol above you for being whirled up. Exit the room via door (13).

d) This part describes what to do here once you have the Blue Key in your inventory. Use the levitation point on the ground floor to get lifted up on the left leap. Step onto to, make your HOVER hocus pocus and steer to the left to hit the central beam. Now your private elevator will take you up to the top!
