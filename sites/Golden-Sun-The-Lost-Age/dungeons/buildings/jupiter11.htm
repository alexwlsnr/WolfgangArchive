Jupiter Lighthouse (Part XII)


The room to the left is where you will emerge next. Be careful, some tiles on the floor will crumble if you step onto them for the second time, so plan your way to reach the door below. There are two chest here, one containing 306 coins can be plundered now. The second one can be explored while being on your way back! You will find a Mystic Draught inside.

The next room (to the right) is boringly empty, if you don't want to mention the cracked tile in the center, so advance through the door!
