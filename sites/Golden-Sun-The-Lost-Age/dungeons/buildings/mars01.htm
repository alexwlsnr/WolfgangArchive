Mars Lighthouse (Part II)

a) Your first goal is to get rid of the massive iceblock, that has frozen the doorway in the middle room. To do so, use the leftmost door in the entrance room and countinue to walk until you found the stairs leading to the next floor.

b) Back again in this room you will notice, that now there is a crack in the iceblock, perfectly prepared for using BURST to finish it off. You can enter the room behind and you will see a circle symbol on the floor which still is somehow meaningless. Walk westward to the stairs, push the dragon statue east and use the stairs down.

c) The third time you reach this floor will be via stairs (4). Use the chute, open the chest and get the Teleport Lapis. Now you can use the TRANSPORT psynergy. Test it on the next circle symbol and you will be transported down one circle. Now head back to the room with the stone circle where you have to use REVEAL.

d) After you placed the Mars Stone back to the dragonhead in the basement, you now can light the switch of the dragonhead to the left. It will crack the massive ice to the right and after a final BURST you can walk through the door next to the statue. Move forward to the stairs (8) and follow the dungeon until you face Samba, the Mars Djinn. Then return here and finally take stairways (1).

Oh, I forgot, if you like, fight another Mimic, the chest to the bottom right!
