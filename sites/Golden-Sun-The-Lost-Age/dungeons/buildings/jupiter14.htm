Jupiter Lighthouse (Part XV)






Again, some POUND and HOVER psynergy is needed to master this room. The trick is to use the block which pops out of its place as a stepstone in the gap between stairs and the leap to the right to reach the ladder for the ground floor. The arrangements of the blocks for getting the chest (Water of Life) and for the exit are displayed to the right.

Leave to room, push the pillar near the bottom to the right. The drawbridge now can be used once again. Don't use it now if you don't want to run around the tower in circles but take the stair up to the next floor.
