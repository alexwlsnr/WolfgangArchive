Jupiter Lighthouse (Part VIII)



The first thing you will see once you entered this room through door (7), is the Red Key which you cannot reach. So use the chute, climb the ladder and exit via door (8). Move southward and leave the lighthouse through the door (D).

Ouside the lighthouse walk up, take a look at the closed top, then climb down the stairs to the right and re-enter via door (E). Again walk up through (9) and you are back in the room seen to the left in the middle.

Slide down the chute and step up the ladder to the very top. After a short information (given in Japanese, of course) use HOVER and you have "pulled a trigger".

 Use HOVER here!

The suspicious looking "cables", which can be found everywhere inside Jupiter Lighthouse are glowing now. Be asured that not only the white pillars to the left and right in this room are levitating but all the other you might have seen on your way here will do the same. So its obvious to stroll the lighthouse once again!

As this room is a dead end, jump over the now levitating white pillars to the right and exit through to top right door into the room with the key. As you might notice, not everything in Jupiter Lighthouse seems to be in properly order.

The levitation point to reach the Red Key is not functioning yet. So you have to find out where to problem is.


Head back to the previous room via exit (7) and walk south to the outside of the lighthouse via exit (C) now.
