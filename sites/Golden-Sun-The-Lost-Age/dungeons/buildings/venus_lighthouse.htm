 Venus Lighthouse Entrance


Your adventure starts where the last one ended, in Venus Lighthouse. Due to the fact that Jenna don't has MOVE psynergy, Felix will open the barrier for you and leaves the party. You are supposed to leave the Lighthouse by the front entrance, but don't forget to take the Herb with you, which is stored on the first floor.
