Jupiter Lighthouse (Part XVI)


To the left you see the floor you will enter first, totally bare, so walk up to the stairs on top of the map. You will find your way up to the room on the right. Here your task will be to pass the two stone heads without being blown to the floor below. Avoid the first statue by going left and by running a bit (B-Button). But be careful not to step on the darker cracked tiles or you will fall back to the floor below, too.

Use the second stone face for your advantange and get blown over to the right part of the map. From here it will take only a short way to the stair at the bottom. You are nearly there!
