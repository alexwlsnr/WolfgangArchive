Jupiter Lighthouse (Part XI)



You will enter the left tower of Jupiter Lighthouse via door (I). The chest to the left is in fact a Mimic, so leave it alone to avoid a nasty confrontation. Decide for yourself if a whooping 1420 EXP and 700 coins are worth a battle!

a)Walk to the right then and enter the next room with some pillars that can be rolled over the floor. You will notice an unreachable Blue Key in the center of the room. Move up for the stairs leading up to the next floor.

b) After your long fall (interrupted, of course) from the top of the left tower you will land next to the Blue Key. Grab it, leave the tower (best choice is using RETREAT) and re-enter Jupiter Lighthouse from the main entrance. Find your way into the central room with the glowing beam and use the beam itself for your advantage.
