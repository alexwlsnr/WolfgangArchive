Anemos Temple (Part II)



On the bottom part of this page you can see the front entrance to Anemos Temple. Due to the greenish pillar on the floor and the chest containing a Dragon Skin you cannot walk further inside.

But the transport circle on the floor will give you a clue, what you are supposed to do. Exit the temple and use the corresponding Transport circle on the townsquare of Contigo.

But even if you do so, you can't do anything else here than to admire the achitecture unless all 72 djinns have joined your party. Step inside the bigger circles with the different symbols to release the "powers" of your djinns. For each djinn one of the smaller circles will be lit and after you have "turned on" all circles of one symbol, a light above the entrance door will be automatically lit.

Once all four lights are burning the door will open and you finally can enter the temple itself.

If you ask yourself why you should enter this temple, the answer is given in the next room. A Combo Tablet free of charge is standing and waiting for you. Now you can summon Karon / Clown with the help of 8 Venus () and 2 Jupiter () Djinns.

Dont retreat yet, there is one more Combo Tablet hidden deep inside the temple, this time heavily guarded!
