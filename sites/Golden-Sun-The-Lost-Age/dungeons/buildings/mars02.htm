Mars Lighthouse (Part III)

You will enter this floor three times. First time you are in the left area. Please keep in mind to get the Mars Djinn called Samba later. The third time coming here will take you to the stairways top right!

a) Push the pillar down with POUND and use GRIND on the iceblock. This not only will sink the block but also causes a fine crack in the massive iceblock one floor below. So go back there and find a way to remove it completely.

b) After you solved the puzzle with the ice around the right dragonhead in the entrance room, you can get the Mars Djinn Samba. Climb up the stair (8) to find him.

c) The last time you will enter here will be via stairways (1). Hop over the stepstones to reach stairways (9).
