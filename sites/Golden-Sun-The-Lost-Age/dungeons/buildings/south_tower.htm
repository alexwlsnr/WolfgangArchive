Tundaria Tower (Part I)


 Tundaria Tower Outskirts


In the remotest part of the world of Golden Sun you will find the Tundaria Tower. It is located on the icy south continent. If you have not visited Aqua Rock and Piers does not know PARCH, a visit of the tower is completely useless. Another psynergy is needed to reach the main treasure of this tower, the Center Prong. The spell mentioned is BURST but you will find the Burst Brooch which grants the spell on top of the tower itself.

Psynergy needed
PARCH POUND
