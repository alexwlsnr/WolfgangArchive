Kibombo Statue (Part V)

 Now the Statue will open its eyes, and the
 door in the statue's room will open!





After the priest Akafubu has run through the door and into the elevator, you should follow him down to the "cellar". He is standing next to an other statue. Now you will get the Black Orb, finally. You will need it to use the Lemurian ship.

A hidden trapdoor including a ladder will appear to the right, but currently no one in your party knows about the CYCLONE psynergy, so you won't get far down there. You will find details about that in the section Kibombo Cave, of course!
