Psynergy Spell List


As you venture forth in your adventure your party members will gain levels and their experience, and cleverness will rise continuosly. They will learn more magic spells which will be very helfpul.

Many spells are not bound to a character but to the djinnis which join the character. Some spells are available only if you mix the djinnis, some spells depend on a characters level as well. A few spells are bound to special items you will find and equip.

The list below shows all available spells in alphabetical order without any hint which circumstances are needed to make the spell available.

Due to the fact that there are so many spells, I decided to split the section into different parts.
