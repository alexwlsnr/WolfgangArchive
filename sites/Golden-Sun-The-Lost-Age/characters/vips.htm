Very important Persons List


In this list all those characters are described whom Felix and his friends will meet during their long journey. They are listed in alphabetical order of their Japanese names, so their occurance is completely different while meeting them during your adventure.

And I am not quite sure, if in a translated version, their names will stay the same! Besides, some of the facts written here about the VIPs still are based on imagination because of my bad knowledge of the Japanese language. Please don't blame me if I guessed totally wrong, try yourself, you will soon see what I mean!

BriggsHead of a pirates's gang! You have to fight him and his men in the town of Alhafra on the Osenian Continent on board of his own but somehow wrecked boat.
ChauchaThis nice looking woman is the wife of a nasty pirate named Briggs.

FeizhiIf you helped during the adventure of Isaac and friends to rescue her boyfriend Hsu, she will meet Felix in the village of Champa with news about Isaac and a nice present.

KushinadaThis beauty of Izumo shall be (obviously) sacrificied to a fierce dragon. Will Felix and friends be there in time to prevent this madness?
LunpaLegendary founder of the village still carrying his name. Traveled the world together with Babi once, but then decided to stay in Lemuria.
Madra ChiefThis young man is the chief of Madra. You meet him first in the desert of Yampi and will get an important item from him later in the quest.
Madra ElderSeems to be the former chief of Madra. After his retirement he is still helping his precestor with some advice.
MahaAt night you can meet this wolfman in the village of Garoh. He does not only offer you some tips but a Jupiter Djinn, too!
Master HamaWell known from the first part of the adventure as the head of Lama Temple; will be met again in the town of Contigo, where the story of Ivan seems finally to be revealed.
Master PoiOnce you found your way up to the top of Kandorea Temple you will meet this old priest. His present to you is your first new psynergy called LASH.
MoapaHe is the chief of Shaman. Show him an important item and he will not only allow you to compete in the Shaman Trial, you will have to race against him and his best men!
ObabaShe is true ruler of the pirate's village Champa, regardless everything Briggs might tell you. She helps you getting a rare item needed to complete your journey.
Prox ElderNear the end of your adventure you will meet this funny looking guy in the town of Prox far in the north.
PuelleLike the village elder of Prox the chief of the town has this slightly insane looking greenish face. Does the eternal winter in the northern region not only freeze the people but their human feelings, too?
RikiThe second of the two trapped boys. Help both to return to their hometown Daila.
Shaman PriestI am not quite sure, what this guy wants from Felix and his friends. We have to wait for a translated version first!
