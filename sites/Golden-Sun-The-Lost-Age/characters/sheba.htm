Sheba


Sheba is a strange girl Isaac, Garet, Ivan and Mia met first in Babi's palace. She, alongside Jenna and Kraden followed Saturos and Menardi to the Venus Lighthouse in the first story.

At the end the tower of Venus Lighthouse broke apart and Sheba fall of the top of Venus Lighthouse into the sea. Felix tried to rescue her by jumping after her. As we all know he managed to save her.

Now she follows Felix to help him. Sheba is a wind adept like Ivan, and she will get much stronger during the quest, although you should take care of her because, compared to the other party members, she always will be the weakest in the group.

Like Ivan she will learn about the REVEAL psynergy, which indeed is a most helpful ability, but it will be a hard and long journey until she finally can use this nice spell.
