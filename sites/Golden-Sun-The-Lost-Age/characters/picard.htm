Piers


When you meet this mysterious guy for the first time, it will be in the prison of Madra. But don't get it wrong, Piers is a good one and was kept in his cell due to a big mistake. Try to gain his reputation back, find him for a second time and he will join you in your quest.

Piers is a Mercury adept and seems to be very knowledgeable if it comes up to the legendary town of Lemuria. Maybe you will find out more once you travel the sea on a Lemurian ship, that indeed, belongs to Piers himself. Are the any more secrets he has to offer?

Due to my lack of knowledge of the Japanese language I cannot tell you much more about Piers, we have to wait until a translated English version is published for much more detailed character descriptions.
