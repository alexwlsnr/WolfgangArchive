Venus Djinns


These nice creatures are called djinnis. If you found all djinns in the first adventure and collect all those to be found in the second one you will end up with a total of 72 Djinns.

Only then you will be able to enter the Anemos Temple to collect even the last two Combo Tablets.
If you did not use the transfer link option or the passwords to transfer your game data of the second Golden Sun game, there might be some more djinns to be found in places I did not detect. Please tell me if the rumors are true that, for example, a Mercury Djinn is waiting somewhere in the town of Prox or if it stays to remain just a rumor!

Oh, and if you need some advice about the other djinns of the first part, refer to the appropriate pages in Home of the Golden Sun!
