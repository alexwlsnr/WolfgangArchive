Jenna


Jenna is a friend of Isaac and Garet since childhood. Raised as an orphan together with her brother Felix by her grandparents, she suffered a lot as during one disastrous night her brother was supposed to have drowned.

As the adventure of Isaac and Garet started she tried to help to solve the puzzles of Sol Sanctum. But then the two foes Saturos and Menardi appeared just in the moment as they found out the hidden secret of the shrine. As a big surprise their enemies were accompanied by Jenna's lost bother Felix. But he did do nothing, as Jenna and Kraden were taken hostage by Menardi.

Now, in the second part of the adventure, Jenna fights side by side with her older brother, although not quite sure, if that was the right decision.

We all will see!
