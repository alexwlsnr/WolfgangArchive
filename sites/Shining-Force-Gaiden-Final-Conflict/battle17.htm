BATTLE 17 ´Lynx is waiting´
Short Description
The confrontation with Lynx is near. This battle seems easy but during the fight more enemies will appear which are 2 High Witches, 2 Archknights, 2 Stone Golems, 1 High Priest, 1 Harpy Queen, and 1 Executor in all. Lynx takes his last breath when a Warrior named Rideion shows up. It seems that he knows Lynx. Maybe that Lynx was missguided by Mishaela. Rideon joins to avange Lynx´s death.
