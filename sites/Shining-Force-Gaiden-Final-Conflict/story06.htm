Part 6

Observe falling rocks, they won´t hurt you but the former wide path will become too narrow for two people to stand side by side. So the two Dark Mages will become a living danger with their Blaze Level 2 spells. The chest contains a Protect Milk.

Before the next battle outside the cave starts you see a great flood washing away a whole army.
On the now not so dry ground you have to face a big army blocking your way towards a castle.

In the chest to the west a Brave Apple can be found. Use it to level up Cynthia, if you like.
After the castle is free again a female Centaur named Sylvia and the Bird(wo)man Julia become members of the Force.
