BATTLE 18
´The Castle under Attack´
Short Description
After you enter the castle and speak to the King, the building is taken over by the enemy troops. The castle is set on fire. You have to fight them back. Inside the chest to the left you will find some Kaiser Knuckles (CURSED !!) either for Knuckles or Motton. If the fight is over you can finally enter the shop for some real good weapons. In the Bargain section you can buy a Power Ring and a Healing Rain which normally can be taken from a defeated enemy. It seems that I missed those items during the last fights.

Enemies:
4 Arch Knights
3 Stone Golems 2 High Witches
3 Cannon Knights 2 Harpy Queens
2 Skull Warriors 1 Brass Gunner
1 High Priest
