BATTLE 2 ´The Town in Ruins´
Short Description
After Ruburan is caught, the party returns to the town only to see it in ruins. The enemy has invaded the peaceful town. The old mentor of the Shining Force is kinapped by the evil Misheala. (Remember her from Shining Force I ?)

Ian, the swordsman and future hero joins the Force. Don´t forget the chest with a Quick Chicken to the left.

Enemies:
3 Blue Oozes
3 Goblins
2 Orcs
2 Hunter Goblins
