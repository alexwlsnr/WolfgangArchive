BATTLE 19
´On to the Tower of the Ancients´
Short Description
This one is really tough. Not only 6 High Witches stand against you, but there are 5 Lesser Demons which can cast spells, too. So move onward carefully and try not to loose to many comrades in the battle. At the end there is a Mishaela Doll waiting to sparc you to pieces. So keep yourself healthy until the confrontation starts. The shining balls will have no effect, neither they move nor do they attack. Wonder why they were placed here?

Enemies:
6 High Witches
5 Lesser Demons
3 Skull Warriors 2 High Priests
3 Dark Shamans 1 Mishaela Doll
