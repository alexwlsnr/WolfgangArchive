Part 4

You are inside a cave which seems to be the headquarter of some thiefs with Ruburan as their leader. Misheala, Lynx and Dark Guss are the uninvited guests. After some discussion Misheala knocks out some of the thiefs including Ruburan, who what seems to be obvious, must have worked for them. Mishaela and her friends flee the scene and the battle starts.

During the fight a hidden room opens, guarded by four Giant Bats. In the two chests you will find a Protect Milk and a Power Wine (Power Water).
Ruburan joins after the fight to help you.
