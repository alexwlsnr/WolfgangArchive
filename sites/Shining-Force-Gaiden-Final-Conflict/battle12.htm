BATTLE 12 ´Sarah Pond´
Short Description
Here Sir Astral met his former student Sarah. Find a Protection Ring inside the chest, will you? Some more enemies are hidden within the fog clouds. After all is settled you meet an old guy. Don't underestimate him, his abilities are quiet opposite to his weak appearance. His name is Motton and his profession is that of a Master Monk.
