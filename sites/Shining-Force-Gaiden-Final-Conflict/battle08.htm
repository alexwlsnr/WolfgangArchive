BATTLE 8
´Cross the bridge, if you can´
Short Description
Before the battle starts you see a great flood washing away a whole army. On the now not so dry ground you have to face a big army blocking your way towards an enormous castle. In the chest to the west a Brave Apple can be found.
After the castle is free again a female Centaur named Sylvia and the Bird(wo)man Julia become member of the Force.
Enemies:
3 Brass Roaders 3 Worms
2 Dark Smokes 2 Dark Mages
2 Orc Lord 2 Priests
2 Dark Knights
