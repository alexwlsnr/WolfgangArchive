BATTLE 10
´The disguised Townspeople´
Short Description
After you entered the town (the location looks somehow familiar for those who played Shining Force II on the Genesis/Megadrive) an ambush awaits your troop. The dragonman flies towards you and wants to warn you but the town gate closes just before he can reach you. The townspeople take off their disguises and the attack starts. Don´t forget to open the chest in the northeast corner of the town. You will find a Robin Arrow. Search the two wells to find a Quick Ring and a Steel Sword. If you miss those items you can buy the in the next shop in the bargain department but the Quick Ring costs you 10,000 bucks. Too expensive, if you can have it for free. Dragonman Eric becomes a new Shining Force member.
Enemies:
2 Dark Snipers 3 Dark Mages 3 Vampire Bats
2 Hobgoblins 3 Priests 3 Dark Knights
2 Hell Soldiers
