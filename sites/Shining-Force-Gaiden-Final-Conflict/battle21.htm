BATTLE 21
´Up to Tower´
Short Description
Let´s hope you bought enough Healing Seed. You need them nearly after every second step you take. There are five statues located on the top of the tower, but in fact they are ´deadly´ enemies, too. And the main problem you have, is to reach the top of the tower with as much troop members as possible to finish off the persistant Mishaela for the last time. Only those who survived can await the final showdown. If you retreat from this or even the next (and last) battle you have to start again from the bottom of the tower with all enemies back on their posts.

Enemies:
5 Evil Mimics
2 Cannon Knights 1 Dark General 1 Pyro Hydra
2 Red Dragons 1 Dark Shaman 1 Demon
3 Death Godders 1 Mishaela Doll Mishaela
