BATTLE 1 ´Taros Temple´
Short Description
The centaur knight Meddo and his crew are sent to the Talos Temple.

Someone wants to steal (or destroy) the old storehouse vehicle which helped the Shining Force team in former times. There is a thief named Ruburan who is inspecting the machine closely (it´s possible, too, that in fact he is damaging it, I don´t know!!). Be it as it be, the Shining Force team appears and has to fight some enemies, which helped Ruburan to fullfill his job.

Due to the fact, that no one in the team knows the EGRESS (RETURN) spell a retreat is not possible. Be careful not to loose Meddo. He is the main character during this fight.

Enemies:
4 Blue Oozes
4 Goblins
