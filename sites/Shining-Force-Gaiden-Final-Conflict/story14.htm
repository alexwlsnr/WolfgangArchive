Part 14

There are five statues located on the top of the tower, but in fact they are ´deadly´ enemies, too. And the main problem you have, is to reach the top of the tower with as much troop members as possible to finish off the persistant Mishaela. Only those who survived take part in the final showdown. If you retreat from this or the next battle you have to start again from the bottom of the tower with all enemies back on their posts.

So Dark Sol and Dark Dragon were behind all evil. You don´t have to fight Dark Sol, he only summons Dark Dragon and disappears. If you managed to get many friends to the top, this fight is easy.

Sonette has to finish off the hands first. Let her stand alone so that only she gets damaged by Dark Dragon´s magic. Use Healing Seeds or let Motton cast a spell from the distance.

After Sonette´s job is done attack with all forces. Use the airborne troops to surround the evil creature. Now hit, throw, and cast spells. Only the best for the bastard. After the fight is over, enjoy the ending.
