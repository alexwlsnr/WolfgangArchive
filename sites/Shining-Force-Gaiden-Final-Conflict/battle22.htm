BATTLE 22
´Dark Dragon´
Short Description
Those two were behind all evil, Dark Sol and Dark Dragon. Ok, you don´t have to fight Dark Sol, he only summons Dark Dragon and disappears. If you managed to get many friends to the top, this fight is easy. Sonette has to finish off the hands first. Let her stand alone so that only she gets damaged by Dark Dragon´s magic. Use Healing Seeds or let Motton cast a spell from the distance. After Sonette´s job is done attack with all forces. Use the airborne troops to surround the evil creature. Now hit, throw, and cast spells. Only the best for the bastard. After the fight is over, enjoy the ending.

Enemies:

Dark Sol

Dark Dragon
