GWYN
Gwyn is an aging scholar who dreams about making his mark in the world of archeology before he dies. He´s devoted his entire life to the study of the Blue Spire and similar ruins, and has become a notable expert.
He´s taught Hiro and Ruby how to explore the ancient ruins near his home, though he´s warned them never to go without him.
But, will Gwyn´s quest for fun and adventure turn out to be his last?
