Tip:

The main enemy is the Guardian Angel. You may beat the Angel Guards if you like, but it will be useless because the Guardian Angel summons more and more of them during the fight.
Their main job is guarding the Guardian Angel.

There are two of them at the beginning but they become more and more during the fight.
They use single slashes, electric attacks, hits and so on to fullfill their duty.
