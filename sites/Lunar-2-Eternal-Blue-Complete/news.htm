What´s new on this Lunar2 Homepage?

Welcome to the Unofficial Homepage of Lunar2 Eternal Blue.
What can I tell you? Ok, don´t expect to find all solutions for your problems on these pages. The main intention creating this site was to honor the joy and fun I had playing this marvelous game years ago on the Sega MegaCD. There is still no MegaCD Emulator available so I had to wait until I got my hands on a Playstaton version of Lunar2. Then it only took about two months time to create these pages.
No more, no less.

The game still is not released in an English verson (Hurry up, Working Designs) so I had to use the Japanese game for my up to now largest project. And this is the main cause why I beg your pardon. My Japanese is not this good and, although the bleem! emulator did a marvelous job, it seems to me that it had problems displaying the whole range of Japanese letters. Only a small amount (Hiragana and Katana) were to be seen which made it nearly impossible to read the texts. It was like trying to read a newspaper after my dog has played with it for a while! You now may say that I should be used to it, but don´t blame me, if there are some incorrect details to be found.
