 First part of the battle:

Zophar starts with an One Person Attack (normally Hiro) with a nasty side effect: the person hit will become mute. So use a Holy Water instantly.

Jean´s White Dragon Protect will be very, very helpful!

 Zophar uses his Electric Trick on an aera. Damage for all within range.

This time, a one person attack will follow.
Second part of the battle:
After you have beaten Zophar´s first form he will use Althena´s power combined with his own magic abilities forming himself to Omni Zophar.
Don´t attack him as long as Lucia is imprisoned in the red crystal on his chest. But make preparations to survive the battle coming.
First: spread all over the battlefield to avoid multiple injuries on more than one fighter.
Second: Lemina must use Grand Arms on Hiro and Leo for several times. So the main fighters are well prepared for the third part of the battle.
Third: Ronfar should heal as usual. Don´t bother about loosing your MP reserves. Lucia breaks free and will stock up your HP and MP after a while. Now part three of the fight against Zophar will start!

The best strategy (only my oppinion) is to attack the head only. Let Jean cast White Dragon Protect, Hiro should use Tri Slash, Ronfar, Lemina and Leo should concentrate on refilling Hiro and Jean with MP (Silver Lights / Starlights), healing (Leo with Life Crystal, for example) and reviving if neccessary. It will be hard, but it can (and must) be done!.
Forth part of the battle:
It´s not over yet. But don´t fear this one. There is not much left of proud Omni Zophar and his power. Allthough only Lucia and Hiro will stand side by side in this last confrontation, it will be the easiest fight in the whole quest.
If Hiro has enough magic left for a Tri Slash it will be a one hit victory!
Now the only thing you have to do is: enjoy the end of the story!
