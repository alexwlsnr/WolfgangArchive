SIDECAST SECTION
THE FOUR DRAGONS
White Dragon:
He seems to be, again, the wisest dragon of all. Find him and you will get the White Dragon Medallion.
You now can use the best defense spell ever: White Dragon Protect.
Blue Dragon:
To free the Blue Dragon you have to defeat the Blue Dragon Fiend. As a gift for your heroic deed you will get the Blue Dragon Medallion.
Its unmatched magic is: Blue Dragon Healing.
Black Dragon:
Again you have to free the dragon from a magic slumber. His donation is the Black Dragon Medallion.
Limbo Magic: Black Dragon Grief.
Red Dragon:
This is the last of the four dragons whose magic was sealed away by Zophar and his underlings. Get the Red Dragon Medallion to cast the Red Dragon Anger.
