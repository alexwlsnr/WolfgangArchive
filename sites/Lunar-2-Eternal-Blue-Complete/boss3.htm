 Again, a boss which has three forms.

If the Magical Tester opens its mouth it will blow bubbles (Bubble Blow) on one opponents.

If its key is turning the Tester will perform his Dizzy Drum trick on an unhappy victim.

Tip:
Be careful to be not in range of the blow the Magical Tester uses in its last form. Otherwise heal your party member as soon as possible.		 Besides its well known Bubble Blow the Magical tester sometimes jumps strait into the air only to land on the head of one of your friends.

Ouch, that hurts!
Although you knocked off all his armes and legs, the Magical Tester is no easy opponent.

It uses Bubble Blow (how boring) or its Strait Spit which will be indicated by some sparkles coming out of its mouth.

Its good to have no ´smell add-on´ installed to your Playstation.
