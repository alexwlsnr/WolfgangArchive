This section should help all those, who cannot read Japanese to play the game nonetheless.
I tried to 'translate' some of the menues.

To the right you see the main menu. Simply move the mouse over the appropriate button like you would do in the game and the translation will be displayed.

Click on the button for Status, Equipment, and System to get even more information.

As Consultion, Technique, and Items are a bit too complicated to translate everything, no further information will be given. Refer to the Item or technique section for more details.
Line up Order is selfexplanatory, so no translation will be needed here.
