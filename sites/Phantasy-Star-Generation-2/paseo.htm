Paseo Capital
Information


Start your adventure in the capital of Motavia, Paseo. All sorts of shops are present here amongst other buildings you will not find anywhere else.

In the town center you see the Central Tower where the major awaits you to give you new orders. The Library is located in this building, too.

There is another building in Paseo,
your Home. Meet new friends, re-arrange your party if needed or store items here.

Noticed this machine? You can find in nearly every town.
Use it to upgrade your weapons!

Tips
More

Visit your home from time to time, especially after you have reached new towns or locations.
Your comrades will join here one by one to help you during your quest.
If you seem to be stuck in the adventure, a chat with the major in the Central Tower may give you some clues.
