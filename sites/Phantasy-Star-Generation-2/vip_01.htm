Very Important Persons

  Name
  Darum

He is blocking the North Bridge leading to the western part of Motavia.
His motivation to do so is something you have to find out at the beginning
of your quest.

  Name
  Teim

She is waiting inside Nido Tower and has something to do with Darum. Find her and accompany her to the North Bridge.


  Name
  Ustvestia


This musician is living in the town of Oputa. Visit him to hear all music themes.
There is one thing I sould mention, too. One party member (or all of them) can learn a technique called Musika. This will be very important once you found out about the mystery behind the Climatrol Center and its serious consequences.

Learning Musika will cost 2000 Mes. for men and 5000 Mes. for women.


  Name
  Heram


He is a scientologist who resides in the town of Kueri. Heram will ask you to find a Maruera Leaf for his studies. Take this job very serious because he will invent an item which you will need badly to find out the truth about the Climatrol.

Once you found the leaf, give it to him and he presents the Maruera Gum to you. Chew it like Harry Potter using Gillyweed and you can breath underwater!

  Name
  Motavian

There are many Motavian natives in Optua, Kueri and Roron, of cause. But only one will be shown in more detail (picture to the left). This and only this one is the Motavian to whom you should offer a Maruera Berry. In exchange he will present you the Motavian Ring; from now on you can unterstand Motavians!

  Name
  Tyler


Rolf and his friends owe their lifes to this guy, because space pirat Tyler saved their necks during the Gaira satellite desaster. Without him your party would have crashed helpless into the planet Palma as the only prisioners onboard.

Tyler tells you why he and his crew are fighting against the mighty Mother Brain computer. Maybe, he has a better point!
