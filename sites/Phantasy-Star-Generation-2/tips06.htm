Attack Rack Menu
Information

Top right on the combat screen you will notice the Attack Range Meter. It can easily be filled up by pressing the - Button until your desired rank is reached. Now release the button and the rank is fixed.

Attack Rank 1 =
Fast but low power attack
Attack Rank 2 =
Medium speed and power
(Like Auto Mode)
Attack Rank 3 =
Slow but high power attack

Note: You will gain Bonus EXP if your party uses only Attack Rank 3 attacks!
