Reinhart, Ruler of the Tradnor Kingdom (Part I)
Reinhart, King of Tradnor

Though gifted with the countenance of a child, Reinhart is the offspring of Valhart, the war god. He is the youngest monarch in all of Legendra.

He rules Tradnor with a kind hand and has earned the respect of his subjects. He does not lust for blood like his father, but is willing and able to incinterate any that challange him. The nations neighboring Tradnor repect him.
