Leon, Ruler of the Topaz Kingdom (Part I)
Leon, Leader of all Topaz

Solemn and deeply religious, Leon rules his kingdom quietly. It is here that Leon chose to become a monk and pursue the martial arts in order to not only train his body, but also his mind.

However, his tacit manner of gouverning the people of Topaz should not be mistaken for weakness. He is a very capable warrior that likes to fight bare-fisted in battle when provoked.
