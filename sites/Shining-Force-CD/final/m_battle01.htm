Tip


What can I say, it will be the hardest fight ever. You are limited to the characters listed in the hero section. And they start at the level displayed in the status chart no matter what level they had in former adventures.

Try to use the Healing Seeds only if your character is in grave danger. You have no shop available for stocking up your supplies.

Thanks to the programmers, they equipped Natasha, Wendy and Sarah with Mystery Staff. They refill +2 MP after each move. So you don´t have to fear running low in MP with your main magicians.

Venture forth slowly and try to finish off the baddies one after the other. Save after you eliminated an enemy without own losses, so if something goes terribly wrong you can resume the battle from a save point. Good luck, you need it.
