Shopkeeper

This nice guy sells so many helpful thing which will aid you in your quest.
Buy weapons, healing items and support items only in his shop. You won´t find a better shop.
If a part of your equipment seems broken, he will repair it for you, too.

Not to mention that he is also interested in buying things. So use this weakness to get rid of unneeded item, weak weapons etc.
Oh, and ask for special items from time to time. Maybe, he has a special offer to make!
