Alis is the main hero of the story. Her brother Nero was killed by the evil emperor Lassic.

So she vowed revenge for her brother's death.

During the quest she will become a good fighter with only minor magic abilities.
Myau is a very interesting animal which almost looks like a cat. She is somehow bound to Odin.

Alis will recruit Myau as her first comrade.

Myau knows some good defence magic and can disarm traps and pitfalls.
ALIS / ALISA MYAU







Odin has promised to fight Lassic, too. So it is only natural, that he will join Alis to help her.

He is a brave fighter with guns and axes, but doesn't know any magic.

His Japanese name is Tyron.
Noah is a powerful magician of the ancient espers. He helps Alis to fight against evil monsters and enemies.

His knowlegde of magic covers not only attack but very mighty healing spells, too.

His Japanese name is Lutz.
