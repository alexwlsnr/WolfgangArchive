Getting Hanzou

All those who helped Max and friends in the original game on the Genesis/Megadrive will be surprised to hear that this time the Ninja Hanzou is hidden on the island of Waral. To get Hanzou you have to read the signs shown in the screenshots below.
1. Floating the sea 2. Not the bookshelf 3. A cross atop the spire
4. Those whishing a duel search the fish. Stand on the fish looking north Hurray, Ninja Hanzou joins
