Domingo

This mysterious creature is called Domingo. Hatched (in Pao, second visit) from an egg which Max will find in Manarina, Domingo becomes a nice addition to the Force. His Freeze spell should not be underestimated.
Refer to the Hidden Items section for details about the egg. Domingo cannot be promoted.
