Shining Force Card System

In addition to the original Shining Force game the relaunch adventure has a unique card feature.

You will have to collect up to 55 cards during the whole quest which then can (and should be)
used by a guy named Mawlock. This mysterious fighter is a cardmaster
who alone can use the special abilities which are sealed inside
the various cards on himself, his friends, and on the enemies!

There are three different possibilities to gain the cards.
The first possibility is easy: Max will get them as a
donation from some fellow friends when he
talks to them in the headquarter or he
meets them for the first time.

The second possibility is a bit more tricky: you have to find cards
which are hidden in chests, behind signs, inside books. etc. From time
to time a special person carries a card and offers it once being spoken to.

Last but not least: even enemies will carry cards which you only will get if a special member
of the Shining Force will strike the final blow on the enemy owning the card.

Details how to receive all 55 cards of the quest can be found in the three sections named "Friendship Cards",
"Hidden Cards", and "Enemy Cards". Press the appropriate button below for more information about each card of the selected section.
