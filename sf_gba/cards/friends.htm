Friendship Cards

To get the cards listed in this section you have to do the following:
  • Talk to the character after each successful battle (headquarter)
  • Have the character in your active party
  • Level him / her up for various rounds
  • Prevent the character from being killed during the battle
After a while the Shining Force members listed below will
offer you their friendship by handing over their card.

Pressing the buttons below will get you to a page with more
information about the character's card.
