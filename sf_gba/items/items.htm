Shining Force Items

Besides the tactical based battles Shining Force offers (like many RPG games) the feature to ugrade your party members by equipping various weapons or special accessory.

You may use healing stuff during the battles if your healer has run out of magical points. These items are sold in shops located in every town of the country.

Special items like potions for example which you should use to power up
your characters only can be found hidden in chests.
