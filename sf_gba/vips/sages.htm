Shining Force Sages

Otrant is the leader of the mages town Manarina. He will test Max and his friends. Only if they manage to retrieve the Orb of Light, he will accept them as the true Shining Force.

Mahato was the right hand sage of King Ramladu of Runefaust. Outsmarted by Darksol, he cannot do much but helpless observe the transformation of Ramladu into an evil monster!

The Spirit of the Spring seems to know Max very well. She finally sends him on his quest to save the world. Later she will enlight Max about his past, present and future.
