Shining Force Helpful Guys

Karin is the leader of the childs town Rudo. She is a good friend of Bleu, the last of the Dragons. She worries about him very much and sends Max to investigate the situation in Dragonia.

Krin is the keeper of the secrets in Rudo. She is very interested in ancient books and books about the Ancients. Her greatest fear is that Darksol will gain a special book from Dragonia.

Merchants can be found all over the world of the Shining Force. Here you can buy and sell items. Sometimes they offer special stuff and in addition to this you may even repair broken items here!

Nova once was the advisor of the Guardiana king. He joins the Shining Force not to fight but to help them with his knowledge. Meet him in the headquarter to discuss problems or regroup you party.

Priests are (obviously) to be found in churches. They will help by reviving unconscious pary members, lift curses, promote your characters and record (save) your progress.

Varios is the leader of the mighty Guardiana knight and the father of Mae. He is fond of Max and trusts him very much. Sad to say, but he will be murdered by Kane in his attempt to save his king.
