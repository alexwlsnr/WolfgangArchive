Tips how to play


Inside shops and churches these menus are diaplayed. You might refer to those shown below if you cannot read Japanese very well.

Church Menues

Two services are offered within churches, heal and save.

If you want to save your game the menu to the right will be displayed.


Shops and Inns Menues

The menues differ from shop to shop. You cannot sell any items in the armory shops, but the second hand shop owners will buy weapons and armory which are not needed anymore.

Inside inns you have two choices: Stay or leave.

The chosen command is marked by a red underline.
