Tips how to play

Walkthrough Part I


The adventure of Minina and Alec start in the village of Tedo. A guy named Taylor was attacked in the once peaceful world on a planet which was discovered under the reign of Alis, the Queen of the Algol star system.

Minina and Alec want to find out what has happened. They both get their first but weak equipment in the house of the village major. Don't forget to equip the weapons and armor. Before leaving the village visit Robu's house in the northeast corner of Tedo village. If you don't visit him you cannot leave the village!

After the visit you can take your first sightseeing tour around the village. Stay nearby until Minina and Alec are stronger for longer journeys. If the HP values of your caracters get low, visit the house of Alec's mom. Here you can stay for free to stock up HP and MP to their maximum.

To the southeast of Tedo lies Gypsy Village. You should visit the tent of the fortuneteller. Allthough the information you gain seem to be useless for now, but it may come in handy later.
