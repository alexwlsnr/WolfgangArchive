Hospital
Service

You can heal your party members here or cure them.

Healing will cost 1 Meseta for each HP or TP.
You have to pay a small ammount of money for curing (turning all status changes to normal), too.
Data Center
Service

This service is free of charge.

Save your adventure from time to time, especially before going to unknown places or huge dungeons.
Teleporter
Service

Use the Teleporter for an easy way to travel around.

For the small ammount of 60 Mesetas you are transported to every town you have visited on your journey.
Clone Lab

    Central Tower
    What to do?

    This building can only be found in the capital of Motavia, the Central Tower. Here your adventure will start. You get your job by the major, who should be visited from time to time. There may be new jobs or some important information waiting for you here.

    A cute lady is working in the library of the Central Tower. She will answer all questions about the Motavian history or special locations.
