This small town southeast of Paseo
Capital was visited by the rascals.
They bombed all high to the air what seemed to be too heavy to carry away.

Gain information here about Teim, who is held captive somewhere. Her father is Darum, he became a criminal to get all the money needed to set her free again.

Besides the destroyed houses some building are still intact. Amongst them you will see a Control Tower, a Clone Lab, a Save Point, a Hospital,
and a Transport Service but you will not find an Armory Shop or Tool Shop here, so take a look at
the items the Weapon Shop has to offer.
