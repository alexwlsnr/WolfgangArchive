Esper Mansion 1. Floor
Information

What's to find:
Eclipse Torch (given by Lutz)

Important:
After a long journey your party will reach the famous mansion of the Espers. Here their 'leader' Lutz has hibernated for 1000 years.

You will find him in a chamber under his throne.

Lutz will recognize Rolf as a descendant of Alis (Phantasy Star Generation 1) and burdens him
to find the legendary Nei Items which are to be found in various location on Dezoris.

A very helpful item to find those dungeons is
the Eclipse Torch which he will present to you. Without it you can't even see the buildings.

You will come here more often during your
quest, especially near the end.
