This part of the tips section deals with the ACM terminals. Here you can upgrade you weapons with different elemental gadgets.

I personally recommend to save your money due to the fact that it depends on the weak
point of your enemies whether the upgrade is effective or even contraproductive.

A non upgraded weapon may be not that strong but better versus all kinds of enemies while an upgraded device may become very weak against foes which defence is strong against your selected enhancement!
