Battle Command Menu
Information

Finally, the battle section. Here you will not only find some rough translation of the Combat menu but some description of the various icons and their meanings.

Move over the menu buttons between Rolf and Nei for their translation. Clicking the COM button will display some more information.

Choosing AUTO will cause a fight where your party member simply attack the enemies onscreen with a medium speed and medium attack power.

The RUN button will never be pressed by serious RPGamers!
