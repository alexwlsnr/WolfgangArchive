Rhode (Brass Gunner)

Description

Picture


Rhode is an archiologist who will only join your party if you are in possession of the Atlas Sword. He drives the vehicle for you which was guarded by Taros. After finding some dynamite and a cannon, Rhode will redesign those components to a fighting machine and becomes a brassgunner.

Nice outfit for a former man of science. Keep him in the back lines and he will finish of the enemies from affar so far.

No Promotion possible
