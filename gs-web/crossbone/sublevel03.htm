Crossbone Island Floor 3


There are two Fiendish Ghouls and a Lich waituing for you here. They try to prevent you from entering the room. Ok, let them try!


This room is a bit tricky. The clue is to use REVEAL to find the Red Key which is needed to exit the room through the door to the left.
The Blue Keys will open the blue doors. But you will need one key for each door.
Stand on the keys nearest horizontal or vertical pedestals and use CATCH psynergy to get the keys.
