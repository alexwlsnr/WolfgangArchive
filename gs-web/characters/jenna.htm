Jenna


Jenna is Isaac's and Garet's friend since childhood. She and her brother Alex
are orphans, raised by their grandparents. Her life was nonetheless peaceful
until an event which occured three years ago. Mount Aleph, north of Jenna's
hometown Vale erupted under mysterious circumstances.

Alex nearly drouned in the village lake and Jenna was looking for help all over Vale.
As all seemed to turn right and help was in sight, a boulder rolled through the whole village,
smashing nearly everyone who tried to rescue Alex. Since then Alex is missing and all
believe him to be dead.

Three years have passed since that awful day and Jenna has grown up. Together with Isaac
and Garet she studies alchemy in the house of the old village scholar Kraden.
One day all four decide to visit Sol Sanctum underneath Mount Aleph to find some helpful
clues. None of them could imagine which advnture is awaiting them.

Jenna is a Fire adept and very good in using fire dependend psynergy. This comes in handy
as the party ventures forth to the inner sanctum of Sol Sanctum.

But in the moment of Kraden's triumph (the discovery of the hidden elemental stones) faith once
again turn against Jenna. She and Kraden are take hostage by Saturos and Menardi,
two suspicious looking strangers.
