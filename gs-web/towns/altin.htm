The Village of Altin


What a mess. Altin is haunted by water spitting statues which have flooded nearly the whole village.
To get rid of the water your party has to follow the creatures deep into the Altin Cave, beat one after the other
to dry up all passages lying under water. You meanwhile know where to find a complete map of the cave.

Sorry, but the picture you see below was made after the flood was history. I did not want to get too wet, you know!
