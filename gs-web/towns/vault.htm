The Village of Vault


Vault is the first town Isaac and Garet will find shortly after their adventure has begun. It lies south of Vale. Here they will meet Ivan, have to solve a burglar problem to help Ivan getting back the Shaman Staff and, of course, later in the journey may find the Venus Djinn called Sap.
