The Village of Xian


Take some time exploring Xian, the first village you will enter after the long and hard journey through the Mogall Forest. Some nice items can be found, you will learn a bit more about Psynergy, and you can wipe some more Djinns from your "Missing Djinns List".

The Mercury Djinn Mist awaits you inside the village. The other Djinn of the Mars class is called Corona and can be found north of Xian in the countryside.

Visit the dojo of Master Feh, meet his nice daughter Feizhi, rest at the inn and finally move on to more adventures.
