Thiefs Village Lunpa


Although lying near Vale, (to the east) you cannot enter this town. Guards are blocking the way. Unless Mia is'nt member of your party, you can't sneak into town by using the cave west of Lunpa.

But even if you do there won't be much of interest. Believe me, wait until much later in your adventure. Forget about Lunpa for a while but return here under all circumstances once to met Babi in Tolbi. He will lend you a very precious item which will help you in Lunpa.
