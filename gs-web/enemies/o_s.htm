Enemies O-S


Before you are disappointed not to find any HP values of the enemies, just let me explain that this time I tried to concentrate on what really counts: Experience Points and Money.

You will soon find out yourself if an enemy is beaten, and no one refers to a HP list when being encountered in a fight!

The enemy locations are mainly focused on buildings, caves, and countryside maps. Various enemies will confront you on the main word map in random battles. Only if the EXP and money you will earn for your victory varies from those in the locations mentioned above, I will note that separately.
