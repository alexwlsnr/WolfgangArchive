Vale Cave


When Isaac and his friends finally reached Kalay, the bridge to the northern territory has been repaired. So the way is free back to Vault and, of course Vale.

Now that you know about the LIFT psynergy you can move the rock near Kraden Hut to enter Vale Cave. Hidden inside you will find the HALT GEM and the Jupiter Djinn KITE. Use your new psyergy ability HALT to stop the djinn running around to get him.
