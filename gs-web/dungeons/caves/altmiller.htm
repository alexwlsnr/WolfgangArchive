Altmiller Cave (Part I)


Maybe your search for Babi has brought you her or you found this cave by chance. Altmiller cave is different from all other caves you have been before. Normally most parts are pitch black, only small areas are lit by candles. The best way to beat this cave is an excessive use of REVEAL or to make it more easy, use the maps of this section!

Due to a strait forward "architecture" you won't get lost in Altmiller Cave. You only have one entrance and one exit on each floor.
