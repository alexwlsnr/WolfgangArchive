Altin Cave (Part I)


When Isaac, Garet, Ivan, and Mia enter Altin Village for the first time, nearly the whole town is flooded by water spitting living statues. There are three of them which you have to fight one after the other. After each fight, a new part inside the cave will be passable again.



You have to fight the first Living Statue in here. After the beast is history, leave the cave and go back to the town. A new entrance into the moutain has appeared, use it to enter the next part of Altin Cave.
