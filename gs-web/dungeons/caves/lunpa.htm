Lunpa Fortress (Part I)

 Lunpa Fortress Entrance


Although known as Lunpa Fortress this bandit's hideout is mainly a huge cave carved out of the mountain just behind Lunpa Village. Not only to enter the fortress is a bit tricky but also the exploring success depends to one spell, CLOAK. To perform this you need the CLOAK BALL, which Babi of Tolbi will lend you for your adventure.

You now can hide in the shadows, but beware, if you step into the light, your hide spell will stop to work. Sometimes you have to fight the bandits luring in Lunpa Fortress (mainly in locations, where hiding is almost impossible) but under most conditions try to avoid to be seen. If you are detected you have to start all over!
