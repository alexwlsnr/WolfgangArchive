Bilibin Cave (Part I)


This cave lies to the northwest for Bilibin. You have to pass this cave to find the village of Imil in the far north.
After Ivan knows how to use REVEAL, you should return here for some nice items.

 Bilibin Cave Entrance

 Use GROWTH on the vine.

Climb the vine, jump over the gap, use the chute, enter the cave and find an Elven Rapier in the chest.
