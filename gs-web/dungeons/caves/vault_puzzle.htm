Vale Cave (Part III)


Below you see parts of Vault Caves map showing how pillar have to be ordered, ice pillar have to be erected, and last but not least, a flooded room which once was free of water. Once you pushed the switch on the ground floor the water will flow to this part of the dungeon blocking your way. Luckily there is a parallel path which leads you behind the blockage.



Put the pillars in this order to open the doors. In the room to the right it's not that easy, mind the water dropping from the ceiling. Once the light is extinguished, the "open door" trick will not work. If this happens to you, simply leave the room and the light will be lit automatically once you reenter the room.




On the left you see the room, which is flooded after you hit the switch. On the right you can see which pulldles must be frosted to jump over to the exit on the top right corner of the room.
