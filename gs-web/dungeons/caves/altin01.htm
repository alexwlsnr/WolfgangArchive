Altin Cave (Part II)


After you entered via the new entrance find your way to the next Living statue. Once it is beaten, two new araes within this cave are accessable.



With the LIFTING GEM found deep inside Altin Cave, you should come back to the room on the right to get a COOKIE from the chest behind the big boulder you cannot lift until now. Use the door in the room to the left to find the third Living Statue.
