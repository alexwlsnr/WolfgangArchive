Mercury Lighthouse (Part V)


Finally your party managed to reach to top of Mercury Tower. But although you hurried there in no time your opponents Saturos and Menardi have beaten you. So the first thing to do is to heal up your party and then grab the Psynergy Stone nearby to regain mental strength. SAve your game now, its the last opportunity to do so.

Now you can try to rescue Jenna and Kraden, but Menardi takes them with her down to lighthouse via an elevator on the west side. Saturos is still waiting for you.

A hard fight lies ahead of Issak and his friends. But the 'bad' influence the Mercury Lighthouse has on Saturos' power (no matching elemental class) will help you to beat him. After the fight and to the astonishment of Mia, Alex appears and rescues Saturos. So it seems that you have lost in the end, the Mercury Lighthouse is lit, Saturos is saved and Menardi has both hostages and the three remaining elemental stones. Dammit!
