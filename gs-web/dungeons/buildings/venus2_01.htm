Venus Lighthouse 2 (Part II)

Once you found your way to the center room of this floor you will see sand streaming out of a hole in the wall. Stepping on the switch causes this sandfall to change from the left to the right position. So check the groundfloor again to see the a former unpassable gap now is filled with sand. RUN over the sand (by pressing the B-Button, or appropriate key when using an emulator) to reach the door to the right.
