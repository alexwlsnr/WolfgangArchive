Mercury Lighthouse (Part I)


 Venus Lighthouse Entrance


Once Isaac and friends have met Mia in Imil something weird happens on top of the Mercury Lighthouse next to the village. Mia hurries to the lighthouse to see what's going on. So why not follow her? The entrance is blocked, but MOVE should handle this. Inside the tower a Lizardman tries to hold Mia but your party makes some kind of mincemeat out of him. Proceed , remove some obstacles and find your way to the top. You will find some clues in the Mercury Puzzle section (last page of Mercury Lighthouse section).

Careful with the first treasure chest you see, it's a Mimic.

Don't care if you will not reach he chest containing the Psynergy Armor in the first attempt, you can't get near it unless you found your way trough the 1st floor to drop down to the statue room located in the ground floor. Once you have made it, you will be 'enlighted' how to jump over the water to reach parts of the dungeon you can't go to before.

Nonetheless, even now you need some timing due to the fact that you will sink after the third jump. So plan your way over the platforms. Keep in mind that you only can walk over water when starting from the glowing platforms!
