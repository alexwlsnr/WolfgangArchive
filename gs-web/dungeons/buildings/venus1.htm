Venus Lighthouse 1


 Venus Lighthouse Entrance


As your party reaches Venus Lighthouse for the first time, many victims of Saturos and Menardi lie around unconscious or even worse all over the place! If you try to follow them inside you will soon find out that an energy barrier blocks the way. Even your enemy didn't manage to get past it.

So you have to leave the lighthouse and try to reach the top of the building using the entrance 'far far away'. That's the clue the glowing statue on the second floor gives you.

Still, your first visit was not in vain. Dont't forget to grab the CARRY STONE and it could be wise to remember the lights on the floor of the statues room. They indicate which way you have to follow when entering Venus Tower by the back door.
