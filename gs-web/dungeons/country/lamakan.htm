Lamakan Desert (Part I)


As soon as Isaac, Garet, Ivan, and Mia enter Lamakan Desert they can feel the intense heat lying over the area. Venturing forth is somehow painful, not only because there are many creatures here which attack you but it feels like getting drained up by the heat. Even the air seems to swirl all around you.

Luckily there is some kind of meter showing your health (as shown on the right). If this damage-meter rises up to the maximum, your whole party will loose some HP. Try to prevent this by passing the darker areas of the desert as often as possible. From time to time you will pass stone circles. It would be wise to use REVEAL near them (not in the centre). You then will see what is hidden in the stone area. There are three possibilities, finding some goodies, revealing a well to cool yourself or getting caught by an Ant Lion! To avoid falling into the Ant Lion's pit, stay off the stone circle centre.
