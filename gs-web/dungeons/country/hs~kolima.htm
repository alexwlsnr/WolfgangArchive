Suhalla Desert (Part I)


 Northern Part of Suhalla Desert


Located south of Suhalla you will find the Suhalla Desert. No heat meter, but sudden whirlwinds await you here. The will appear mainly in narrow passages, and if you don't act quick your party will be blown away back to Suhalla Village. So use DOUSE Psynergy to stop the whirlwinds. Sorry to say, but now you have to fight the whirlwind causing monsters, the Tornado Lizards.

 Stop the wind with DOUSE

Two chests are to be found here, one of them is a Mimic. the other one contains a Virtuous Armlet.
