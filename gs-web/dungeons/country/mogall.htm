Mogall Forest


There are three possibilities to find your way through Mogall Forest: try and error, which is the hardest way, or use FORCE a spell you will have if you beat the Fuchin Cave. The third way, of course, is to use the map displayed below! Let's be serious again, Mogall Forest isn't that hard. If you take the wrong way you soon will notice, that you enter exactly the same location you just left again and again. Simply go back and you will reach the crossing from were you took the wrong way. No more, no less!

Oh, I nearly forgot, once you passed Mogall Forest you won't be able to return to all the locations north of Mogall before you reach the town of Kalay. From there you can cross over the broken bridge south of Vale which has been repaired in the meantime!


Psynergy needed            



Items to gain                     



Don't miss QUARTZ, a Venus Djinn. He is waiting for you half way
through the forest. You have to fight him. He rewards you with a
very important ability, REVIVAL!


Careful! Before you attempt to leave Mogall Forest, heal your party.
The boss of the woods, a Killer Ape, will jump out of the last trunk 
near the forest's exit. So be prepared. As a reward for this "horror  
out of the sudden", you will gain the DOUSE DROP, which enables
the carrier with the DOUSE psynergy.
