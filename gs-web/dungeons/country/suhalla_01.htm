Suhalla Desert (Part II)


 Southern Part of Suhalla Desert


Once you reached the southern part, you soon will see a Mars Djinn to the west. But the gap is to wide to jump, REVEAL might help to get to him. Once FLASH is member of your Djinn collection you might want to pass through the tiny cave north.



Near the exit you will notice a pinkish colored whirldwind, it's your passport to Crossbone Island. If you decide to use DOUSE, you will have to fight a semi boss monster called Tempest Lizard.

But you should wait a bit longer until you have visited the Venus Lighthouse nearby. Inside it there is the Carry Stone, an item needed to master Crossbone Island to its very end!

So leave the pink whirlwind and, of course, Tempest Lizard alone, pass the cave once again and continue south.

Next to the broken down Pharao statue you have to encounter Storm Lizard, the main boss of Suhalla Desert. Be prepared for a hard fight. Then leave Suhalla Desert via the southeast exit.
