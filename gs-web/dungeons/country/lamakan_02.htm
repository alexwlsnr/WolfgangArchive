Lamakan Desert (Part III)


Now you are near the eastern exit of Lamakan Desert. Reveal the entrance to a hidden cave to pass to a part of the map you can't reach directly. Get a Mint from the pit in the southwest, then walk north to another "sandfall". Once again you are supposed to use REVEAL here, and you stand against Manticore, the Lamakan Desert boss enemy. He won't be a challange for your well trained party. Sneak through the last cave and leave it, enjoying the fresh air which no longer is "overheated".

You have reached the region near Kalay, finally.
