Again, a character difficult to get into your party!

To gain Robbie isn't an easy task. He can be found in the tomb near Elbesem sanctum, but the only thing you can do is to look at him if you don't have the item called Robbie's Eye to finally activate him.

So, how do you get in possession of Robbie's Eye? It is a donation to you, but only if you managed to save ALL villagers who were attacked by the Bulzom monks in the battle in front of the Elbesem sanctum.

So save them under all circumstances, return to Elbesem after the fight and speak to the village people once again. One of them will give you Robbie's Eye. Now you only need the tomb map to enter the tomb, reach Robbie before the thiefs inside have left the tomb, use the eye and, hurray, Robbie is a party member!
