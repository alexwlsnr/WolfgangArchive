Shining Force III - Scenario 2 - Donhardt    


Status:UnpromotedClass:Paladin
Weapons:Halberds, Lances, Spears
Best: Arc Halberd

After Medion's forces reached the town of Barrand you will meet Donhardt imprisoned inside the Barrand jail. Free him and he joins temporarily.

Donhardt is, like Cybel, very fond of halberds and his preformance is best with this kind of weapon. But don't forget to give him a spear, too.

You cannot control him in Scenario 2 but once you help Julian's forces in Scenario 3, Donhardt will be the first (and well controlable) centaur in the Shining Force team of Scenario 3.

Oh, and just one thing: doesn't Donhardt look like Don Quichote de la Mancha to you, too?
