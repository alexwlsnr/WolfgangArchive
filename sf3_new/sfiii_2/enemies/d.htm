Dessheren    (Shining Force III - Scenario 2 - Enemies D)    

Dessheren (or Death Helen) is one of the four Bulzom Highpriests. Our hero Medion will meet her four times during his quest but have to fight her two times. The last confrontation takes place underneath the Storich railway station.
Here Dessheren will be saved by her sister Jean!
