Shining Force III - Scenario 2 - Traders, Blacksmith, Priest    

Priests: In every village or town you will find a Priest inside the local church
(ok, there is one exception!). But if you ever are cursed due to an equipped item, or being poisoned / paralysed, ask the priest for help.
A character promotion to a higher class, too, is a task for priests.

To Friends

Item Shop: These nice ladies await you in item shops. Standard items like Herbs, Antidotes etc. are offered freely. But to get more special items you have to be very persistant. To the right, you see the item shop keeper of Stump Village.

Weapon
Shops:		 Get better weapons here. It could be wise to ask for special deals from time to time, many magical weapon are only offered in the deals department! Again, to the right, you see the shop keeper of Stump Village.

Black-
smith:		 Bet you will love this guy. In certain towns a blacksmith will take the rare Mithrils (and much money, of course) from you to forge special weapons or accessory for you.
