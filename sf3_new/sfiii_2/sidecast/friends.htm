Bresbi: Not much to say about Bresbi here, He, instead, plays a major role as a non fighting member of Julian's forces in Scenario 2. He together with some generals will be seen at the start of the quest.

Domaric:		 After being kidnapped before the peace conference starts, Domaric is held hostage by the evil Bulsom sect. Setting him free is one of Medion's tasks. Domaric then accompanies his son for a short period until they reach the Empire.

Reliance: As his name inplies, you really can rely on Reliance. He, together with Domaric, will be part of Medion's forces, although not controlable. His main task is to protect the Emperor.
