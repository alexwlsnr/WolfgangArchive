In this first fight, try to gain some experience and get familiar with your new characters. OK, not all are this new, but especially Gracia offers a surprise.
His Shining magic truely is very powerful, but if you want to use it for a second time in this battle you will be shocked to find that all magic points of Gracia have gone! Always keep in mind that the use of Shining will strip off all MP completely. Since Gracia is the only capable healer in your party, better save the MP to secure the health of your party members.

No items are hidden, so you can concentrate on your enemies!
