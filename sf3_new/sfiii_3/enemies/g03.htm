Goriate    (Shining Force III - Scenario 1 - Enemies G)    

Being a sidecast character in the first two scenarios, Goriate will get his last chance in Scenario 3. At the very beginning of Julian's adventure he tries to help Prince Mageron to gain control over the capital city Destonia. And as a Highpriest of Bulzom he tries to protect his "master" in one of the last two battles. As if he has a chance!
