Valkyrie    (Shining Force III - Scenario 1 - Enemies V)    

Valkyrie is like Collossus a machine of an ancient civilisation. The Bulzom sect wants to use them for their evil plan. Luckily both Valkyrie and Collossus have a weakness. You need a Control Stick for proper use which, unlike the machines, can be influenced by a special magic: Shining!
