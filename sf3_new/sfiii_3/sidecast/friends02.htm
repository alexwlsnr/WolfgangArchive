Scholar: In the church of Destonia harbour, an old scholar called Scholar is waiting for you. Talk to him and depending on your previous gameplay Brigit (and Isabella) will join. If Edmund is a party member. only Brigit joins Julian's forces!




To Foes

Gabriel: In the Floating Village this guy resides in a house above the clouds. His real name is not Gabriel but he reminds me of him, so I decided to give him this name. He will give Julian some more details about Arc Hill and his final goal, Remotest.

Arc Hill
Highpriest:		 This is the Highpriest of Arc Hill. Once Gracia and friends have been tested and proven worthy, the Arc Hill priest wil do some magical jumbo mumbo and Gracia becomes a real Innovator, and that's what Bulzom had feared most!

Keeper: This priest lives in Remotest and is very important for all three Shining Force troops. He will promote all party members to their highest class once they have reached level 20. He, too, gives last advices before the Shining Force tries to finally beat Bulzom.
