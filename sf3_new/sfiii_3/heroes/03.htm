This eerie looking character is Cyclops. He will join once you returned from Destonia back to Destonia port. Cyclops is waiting
for you inside the church standing next to Scholar, an old advisor.

Cyclop's unique ability to talk with dragons makes him a very important member of the force. He alone can convince the white dragon Thousand to join, and not only him! Every dragon which will be defeated by Cyclops will join and stock up your party as long as the party is small!

But be careful, each dragon (except Thousand) that will be killed during a battle cannot be revived and is lost forever!
