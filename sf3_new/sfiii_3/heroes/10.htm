Jubei, the samurai, is really a hard to get party member. He tries to encounter two Rainbloods (Pawn and Bishop) all by his own, an attempt which will surely end in his death. So talk to him to convince him, that teamwork would be a better and make him join.
Due to the difficult terrain and the quickness of Jubei, the right timing is most important. It may take more than one attempt to reach him in time, otherwise he is doomed!
