This blonde beauty is called Isabella. She is the daughter of Lady Melinda and King Domaric of the Empire and therefore the sister of Medion, the third prince.
She is a capable healer with Antidote, Aura and Heal, of course.
There are different joining options for her!

  1. Isabella joins together with Brigit in Destonia port (Edmund is not in your party)
  2. Isabella and Honesty wait outside Guarder (Edmund is a party member)
