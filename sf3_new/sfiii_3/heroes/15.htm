You will have no chance getting Profound into your party if Edmund is a member of the Shining Force (for example: without any save game data of previous scenarios).

He will only join if Medion and friends spared the life of Stella during their adventure in Scenario 2 (continuing a saved game where you talked to Stella instead of killing her).
Even in case that Stella is saved, you still have to avoid killing Profound himself in the battle at the coastline near Bearsol. Only then he becomes a party member.
