Kate is a party member from the very beginning of Scenario 3.
It always will be a mystery where and when Kate joined. She was not on board of the flagship when Julian, Donhardt, and Gracia
split from Medion's forces in Scenario 2!

Nevertheless, Kate is a wonderful sniper with (if I remember correctly) more critical attacks in a row than Hayward or Garosh.

Leave her in the back rows due to her weak defense. But equipped with the right weapons (especially arrows / bows) she is best versus all flying enemies.
