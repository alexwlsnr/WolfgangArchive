Fafhard    (Shining Force III - Scenario 1 - Enemies F)    

Fafhard is a somehow misguided commander of the Republic. Left in charge for the time
of the peace conference, he did not trust Benetram any longer due to hunger and misery caused by draught and therefore bad harvest. So he turned against Benetram and challanges Synbios' forces. Synbios spares his life, but then he will be killed by Fiale.
