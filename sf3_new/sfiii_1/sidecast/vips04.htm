Galm

If you wonder why this character has such
a prominent page all for himself, I would like to fill you in.

Galm is my favourite character of the whole Shining Force III / Shining the Holy Arc saga.
It seems that he has planed every single step of your adventure through the three scenarios. His goal was to prevent the awakening of Bulzom all the time. Even the death of Julian's father was part of this plan.
So he in fact is the one who is responsible for our enjoyment, excitement, and fun we had when helping Synbios, Medion, and Julian during their quests!

Besides, his music theme is cool, too!
His theme can be heard if you choose Theme 19 or 20 in the music section.
