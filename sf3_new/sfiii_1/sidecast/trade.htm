Shining Force III - Scenario 1 - Traders, Blacksmith, Priest    

Priests: In every village or town you will find a Priest inside the local church
(ok, there is one exception!). But if you ever are cursed due to an equipped item, or being poisoned / paralysed, ask the priest for help.
A character promotion to a higher class, too, is a task for priests.

To Friends

Item Shop: This nice lady awaits you in the local item shops. Standard items like Herbs, Antidotes etc. are offered freely. But to get more special items sometimes you have to be very, very persistant.

Weapon Shops: Get better weapons here. It could be wise to ask for special deals from time to time, many magical weapon (for example: rods, wands, etc.) are only offered in the deals department!

Utility Shops: Very seldom to find (in Quonos, for example) this guy will sell bracers and circlets. These items are very useful if you want to increase the attack, defense, or luck (for example) values of certain party members.

Blacksmith: Bet you will love this guy. In certain towns a blacksmith will take the rare Mithrils (and much money, of course) from you to forge special weapons or accessory for you.
