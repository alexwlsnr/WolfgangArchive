Hayward is an archer who belongs to the bridge guard of Saraband. He will join your party at once if you talk with him
during the fight on your way out of Saraband.

He, too, joins if not spoken to after the fight, but I recommend the first option because a controllable character on a battlefield is far better than someone standing in your way doing nothing!
