Horst can be found in the soldier's barracks in Aspia. He offers you to join the army and it would be wise to accept.

You won't find a better mace fighter during the whole quest.
Some of his special attacks will kill various enemies with one hit!

You may equip him with a Hand Axe, too, because he then can attack enemies which don't stand next to him.
