After an assault a Republic general left all his injured men behind for an easier and safer retreat. Unlike this coward Justin stayed with them and they found a hideout in a traveling village called Vagabound.

Synbios tries to convince Justin to join but it seems that Justin lost all his trust. Nevertheless, once the force decides to leave Vagabound, Justin appears and finally joins.

As the only Bowknight in the party he will be a most welcome addition because his arrows are the best against all flying enemies.
